<p><strong style="color: #242424;">Eat, drink, and be merry.</strong><span style="color: #242424;"> Denton is a mecca for fine, fast, and fun dining.</span>

Looking for a casual place to unwind with a few good friends and a great meal? Perhaps you&#8217;re planning a special evening out with someone special? Or, maybe you just want a fun, family-friendly spot with great food at good prices. From ice cream at an old fashioned soda fountain to fine dining in elegant surroundings, you&#8217;ll find it here. With so many choices in exotic cuisine to local Texas favorites such as Mexican food and Barbecue, Denton&#8217;s got you covered! Click on the dining categories in the menu on the left to see listings for eating establishments in and around Denton.
