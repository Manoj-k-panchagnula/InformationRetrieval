				<h1 class="text-headline">
					Family Weekend</h1>

				<h2 class="text-headline">
					Friday, September 30 - Sunday, October 2, 2016</h2>

				<p class="text-headline">
					Family Weekend is the first big event of the year for students and their families. A variety of activities make Family Weekend a special time for students to share their new environment with friends, parents, and family members.</p>

				<p>
					Whether you live near or far, we want you to join your UNT family for a weekend of fun! This three-day event will be packed with numerous activities, meals, and the opportunity to cheer on the Mean Green football team. There&#39;s something for everyone!</p>

				<div class="row">
					<div class="col-md-8">
						<h2>
							What is the price of Family Weekend?</h2>

						<ul>
							<li>
								$60.00 for each adult</li>
							<li>
								$30.00 for each child (17 and under)</li>
							<li>
								FREE* for 1 UNT student (each additional student is $25.00)</li>
							<li>
								FREE for children under 4</li>
						</ul>

						<p>
							*FREE with the purchase of one or more adult passes.</p>

						<p>
							Parent Association members and alumni have the ability to receive discounts.</p>

						<h2>
							What does the cost of registration cover?</h2>

						<ul>
							<li>
								Friday evening&#39;s dinner (Scrappy&#39;s Extravaganza)</li>
							<li>
								Saturday&#39;s President&#39;s Breakfast</li>
							<li>
								Mean Green Extravaganza (tailgate) activities and food</li>
							<li>
								Family Weekend T-Shirt</li>
							<li>
								Evening activities</li>
							<li>
								Entrance to other Family Weekend events without special charges</li>
							<li>
								UNT&nbsp;football ticket (tickets will be provided to all registrants except current UNT students - current students get in free with student ID)</li>
						</ul>

						<h3>
							Why should I attend Family Weekend?</h3>

						<ul>
							<li>
								Experience student life first-hand</li>
							<li>
								Enjoy a wide variety of family events</li>
							<li>
								Learn more about the UNT campus and services offered</li>
							<li>
								Explore and get to know the city of Denton, Texas</li>
							<li>
								Network with other parents and families of students attending UNT</li>
							<li>
								Mingle with members of the UNT community</li>
							<li>
								Find out how much your student loves UNT!</li>
							<li>
								Compete for fun prizes!</li>
							<li>
								Cheer on the Mean Green!</li>
						</ul>
					</div>
