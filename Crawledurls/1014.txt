  <h2 class="node__title node-title">Forms</h2>
  
  <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"><p>Below you’ll find a list of commonly used and usually required forms for most Human Resources functions.  You’ll also find references and links to these forms elsewhere on this website, along with explanatory information about how and when to use them. (<a href="https://hr.untsystem.edu/employees/benefits-insurance">Find ERS insurance information and links to forms</a>.)</p>
<ul><li>Find <a href="https://hr.untsystem.edu/forms-alphabetical-order">forms in alphabetical order</a>, or, below, by category in order of most frequent use.</li>
</ul><p><strong>Please do not use old or blank forms that you may have saved in your personal files or computer. Some forms may have been modified or are no longer required, or are not compatible with current software. Find the form you need, save a form to your desktop and proceed.</strong></p>
<p><strong>Please note that fillable forms are most likely to function properly when using Firefox browser. When opening fillable forms using Firefox, you will find directions about how to open the fillable form. Internet Explorer users may want to save a form for one-time use before proceeding. Both browsers offer a Save option in Adobe Acrobat. If you need assistance with browsers or software, visit the Information Technology Shared Services <a href="https://untsitsm.saasit.com/Default.aspx" target="blank">Service Catalog website</a>. Log in with an EUID is required.</strong></p>
<p>If you have questions about a form or process, please call the Human Resources Support Center, 1-855-878-7650<a href="#" style="margin: 0px; border: currentColor; left: 0px; top: 0px; width: 16px; height: 16px; right: 0px; bottom: 0px; overflow: hidden; vertical-align: middle; float: none; display: inline; white-space: nowrap; position: static !important;" title="Call: 1-855-878-7650"><img alt="" src="data:image/png;base64,iVUVORK5CYII=" style="border:currentColor; bottom:0px; display:inline; float:none; height:16px; left:0px; margin:0px; overflow:hidden; position:static !important; right:0px; top:0px; vertical-align:middle; white-space:nowrap; width:16px" title="Call: 1-855-878-7650" /></a>, from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m., Monday through Friday.</p>

<div class="info">

</ul><p>Here are the most commonly used time and leave forms:</p>
<ul><li>Application for approval of leave (<a href="https://hr.untsystem.edu/vacation-leave" title="Vacation Leave Explained">vacation</a>, <a href="https://hr.untsystem.edu/sick-leave" title="Sick Leave Explained">sick</a>, jury duty, funeral, other) – UNT, UNT Dallas, UNT Dallas College of Law, UNT System – (<a href="//hr.untsystem.edu/sites/default/files/FormsForAllLocations/upo-15_request-for-leave_fy15-5.pdf">UPO-15</a>)</li>

<li><a href="//hr.untsystem.edu/sites/default/files/Flexible_Work_Schedule_Request_Decision_Form_UNT-Only.doc">Flexible Work Schedule Request - UNT only</a></li>

<h2>Leaving Employment/Insurance</h2>
<div class="info">
<p>You and/or your dependents may choose to keep your State of Texas/ERS-provided <a href="https://hr-dev.unt.edu/employees/benefits-insurance" title="ERS-provided benefits">health and dental coverage</a> for up to 18 months after coverage ends. If you choose <a href="http://www.ers.state.tx.us/FAQ/COBRA/" title="ERS COBRA explanation">COBRA </a>(an acronym for a federal law) coverage, you will pay the full cost of your premium(s). If you will work at another Texas public university or state agency, your insurance benefits will transfer if there is no interruption in service. Contact the benefits coordinator at your new job for information.</p>
