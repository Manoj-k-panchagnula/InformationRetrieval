<h3><img width="224" height="168" style="float: right; border: 0; margin-left: 8px; margin-right: 8px; margin-top: 4px; margin-bottom: 4px;" alt="Rec Center Picture" src="/sites/default/files/images/gen/Pass1.png" /><strong></strong></h3>
<h3></h3>
<h3><strong>Additional Guest Information</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>All guests must sign a waiver and must be sponsored by a Rec Center member.</li>
<li>All sponsors must be present when purchasing a guest pass.</li>
<li>The prices are based on the guest's relationship with the sponsoring member.</li>
<li>All guests must present a valid picture ID card.</li>
<li>Rec Center member can sponsor up to two guests per day.</li>
<li>Students may purchase spouse and dependent passes only one semester at a time.</li>
<li>UNT faculty and staff who are not members of the Rec Center may purchase a guest pass without a member sponsor.</li>
</ul>
