						<div id="page-title"><h2>Office of Technology Transfer</h2></div>												<p>The Office of Technology Transfer and Economic Development manages the technology transfer process for all patent, trademark, and copyright matters relating to the identification, protection and commercialization of UNT-owned and jointly owned works and inventions. It represents UNT in implementing <em>UNT Intellectual Property Policy</em> with governmental entities, industry, and the public, and serves the public interest by insuring that marketable forms of intellectual property are developed and made available to the public.</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/incubator/available_technologies">University of North Texas Technologies Available</a></li>
<li><a href="/sites/default/files/docs/UNT%20Intellectual%20Property%20Policy.pdf">University of North Texas Intellectual Property Overview</a> (PDF)</li>
<li><a href="http://www.unt.edu/policy/UNT_Policy/volume3/16_13_1.html " target="_blank">University of North Texas Intellectual Property Policy</a></li>
<li><a href="/sites/default/files/docs/IP%20Disclosure%20Form.pdf">Intellectual Property Disclosure Form</a> (PDF)</li>
<li><a href="http://discoverypark-dev.unt.edu/intellectual-property-disclosure-form">Intellectual Property Disclosure Form</a> (Online)</li>
<li>Technology Transfer Process</li>
<li><a href="/sites/default/files/docs/IPCommercialization_FAQ.pdf">Intellectual Property Commercialization Frequently Asked Questions</a> (PDF)</li>

</ul>
