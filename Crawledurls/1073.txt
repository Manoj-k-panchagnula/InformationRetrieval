<div class="singleCol large-12 columns"><h1 id="ContentPageName">Programs</h1>
<p>The UNT Alumni Association provides educational, networking and affinity programming to alumni as well as discounts and affinity benefits to dues paying members. Regional programs are open to all alumni and friends and are currently being developed in Dallas, Tarrant, Collin and Denton counties. Signature programming is also being developed for Homecoming, Alumni Awards, Golden Eagles reunion and a scholarship program for incoming and current students.</p>
<p>For more information, please contact the <a href="mailto:alumni@unt.edu">Alumni Relations Office</a> at 940-565-2834.</p>
