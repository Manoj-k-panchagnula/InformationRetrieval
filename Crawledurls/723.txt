Our goals will be to get healthy and recovery for the championship portion of our season."<br /><br />In the 100m hurdles, Stewart ran the fastest time in North Texas history touching in at 13.03 taking down Alysha Adams wind-aided mark of 13.08 set on April 2, 2011.
