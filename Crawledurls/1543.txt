			<div class="tab-pane" id="about">
				<p class="text-headline">
					The University Union is a prime location for promoting your event or service on campus. The Union has more than 16,000 visitors every day and serves a campus population of more than two million each year. In addition, more than 800 free events happen all year long.</p>

				<p>
					&nbsp;</p>
			</div>

			<div class="tab-pane" id="market-days">
				<p>
					Market Days is designed to showcase vendors who wish to sell products or services to the University of North Texas community. This is a unique opportunity for you as an outside vendor to make contact with approximately 36,000 students and 5,200 faculty and staff members. Market Days will take place the first Tuesday and Wednesday of each month and is an all-day event from 8:30 am &ndash; 4:30 pm. The University Union recognizes Market Days as a great opportunity for partnership between the university and the Dallas/Ft. Worth area vendors.</p>

				<h3>
					Spring 2016</h3>

				<ul>
					<li>
						February 2nd &amp; 3rd</li>
					<li>
						March 1st &amp; 2nd</li>
					<li>
						April 5th &amp; 6th</li>
					<li>
						May 3rd &amp; 4th</li>
				</ul>

				<p>
					<span style="line-height: 1.1; font-size: 14px;">Fees</span></p>

				<ul>
					<li>
						Table (6x2): $125/day (Outdoors)</li>
					<li>
						Table (6x2): $150/day (Indoors - first come first serve)</li>
					<li>
						10x10 space ( w/table 6x2):&nbsp; $175/day (Client may bring 10/10 tent)</li>
					<li>
						One additional table (6x2):&nbsp; $50/day</li>
				</ul>
				<strong>***TAXES WILL BE ADDED TO RENTAL FEES ABOVE***&nbsp;</strong>

				<p>
					Fee will include parking close to venue, campus-wide marketing and guest Wi-Fi account for web browsing purposes only.<br />
					If you are interested in participating in Union Market Days, please feel free to fill out a <strong><a href="/sites/default/files/union/pdf/market_days_registration_form_spring_2016.pdf" target="_blank">Registration Form</a> </strong>and submit with payment no later than ten (10) days before the event date and review our <strong><a href="/sites/default/files/union/pdf/union_market_days_policies_revised.pdf" target="_blank">Policies</a></strong>.&nbsp;&nbsp; Feel free to contact the Event Planning and Scheduling office for further assistance at 940-565-3804 or <a href="mailto:unionmarketdays@unt.edu">unionmarketdays@unt.edu</a>.</p>
			</div>

			<div class="tab-pane" id="media-productions">
				<p>
					At Union Media Productions we can help you to affordably transform your concept to a vibrant, eye-catching video piece that supports your program and/or marketing efforts. All of our current rates and packages are listed here. We are happy to meet with you at any time to discuss your project.</p>

				<p>
					&nbsp;</p>
				Located in&nbsp;Union 223&nbsp;with Design Works.

				<p>
					Phone: 940.369.5979</p>
			</div>

			<div class="tab-pane" id="media-network">
				<p>
					<img alt="" src="/sites/default/files/images/4winds.JPG" style="width: 450px; height: 300px; float: right;" />The Network is a full-color, digital event promotions and media system available in the University Union at the University of North Texas Denton campus for on-campus clubs and departments.</p>

				<p>
					The Network advertisements run on LCD screen monitors strategically located throughout the Union. Each advertisement runs for 20 seconds in a queue, playing once every three to five minutes.</p>

				<p>
					For more information, email us at union@unt.edu.</p>
			</div>

			<div class="tab-pane" id="lawn-signs">
				<p>
					<img alt="" src="/sites/default/files/images/lawn%20signs.jpg" style="width: 480px; height: 480px; float: right;" />Lawn signs are permitted in any grassy area surrounding the University Union. Lawn signs are prohibited in flower beds, inside of the Union, or in areas that may block or interfere with traffic. The Union is not responsible for lost or stolen lawn signs. Groups and event sponsors are responsible for removing the lawn signs after the event has taken place.</p>

				<p>
					For information about printing and lawn sign production, please visit <a href="/design-works">Design Works</a>.</p>
