  <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even"><h1>
	Search&nbsp;TRAIL</h1>
<h4>
	Internal Links<br />
	<a href="#contact">Contact Us</a></h4>
<h4>
	Related&nbsp;Links<br />
	<a href="/trail/about.html">About the TRAIL Service</a> | <a href="/trail/trailhelp.html">TRAIL Search Tips and Help</a>&nbsp;| <a href="/apps/lrs/agencies/index.html">Texas State Agency List</a></h4>
<hr noshade="noshade" size="1" width="620" />
<div>
	<a>Search TRAIL</a></div>
<div>
	<form action="http://www.archive-it.org/public/search" method="get" name="search">
		<a><input class="cleardefault" id="query" name="query" size="35" type="text" value="Enter search terms" /> <input id="go" name="go" type="submit" value="Submit Search" /> <input name="collection" type="hidden" value="414" /> <input name="hitsPerPage" type="hidden" value="10" /></a></form>
	<p class="center"><em>To search the archive, enter your key word(s)<br />
		and click the &quot;Submit Search&quot; button.</em></p>
</div>
<p>TRAIL searches and locates information collected in an archive of more than 180 Texas state agency web servers. After clicking the &quot;Submit Search&quot; button, you will leave this site and enter the Archive-It search results page. <strong>To start a new search, please use your browser&#39;s &quot;back&quot; button to return to this page.</strong></p>
<p>The search engine locates pages in the archive that contain <em>all</em> your search terms; search results are ranked by relevance. Using specific or unique terms will yield better search results. Avoid searching ubiquitous words like &quot;Texas,&quot; &quot;governor,&quot; &quot;state,&quot; etc. You may use <a href="http://www.tsl.texas.gov/trail/trailhelp.html">advanced search features </a>as well.</p>
<p>The search results page provides only one citation per web host address. The exact information you&#39;re seeking may be on a different page than the one cited in the results. Click on the <strong>more from</strong> link to see additional pages from a single web site that matched your term(s).</p>
<p>Sample searches:</p>
<p>Looking for retail sales tax information: <b>sales tax</b>, <b>retail and sales and tax</b><br />
	Establishing a new business: <b>&quot;starting a business&quot;, business start</b><br />
	For additional assistance, see the <a href="/trail/trailhelp.html">TRAIL search tips and help</a> page.</p>
<h2>
	<a name="contact"></a>Contact Us</h2>
<p>If you do not locate the resource you are seeking or have other questions, please contact the reference staff of the Texas State Library and Archives Commission via <a href="mailto:reference.desk@tsl.texas.gov">e-mail</a> or at 512-463-5455.</p>
</div></div></div>
