	<p>Knowing what to expect is important when planning for college costs. Combining quality with value is important and we give you both. We've been named one of <em>America's Best College Buys&copy;</em> for 20 consecutive years and offer lots of ways to afford your education. Join us and discover why UNT is one of the best investments you'll ever make.</p>

<div class="boxer-full tuition-average-cost"><div class="mobile-pad"><div class="inside">
	<div class="boxer grid-12"><div class="inside">
		<h3>Average Total Cost of Attendance for the 2015-2016 Academic Year:</h3>
		<p><strong>Estimated Average Cost of Attendance</strong> = Tuition &amp; Fees + Housing &amp; Food + Books &amp; Supplies + Transportation + Personal Costs</p>
	</div></div>
</div></div></div>


<div class="boxer grid-12 type-costs"><div class="mobile-pad"><div class="inside">
	<div class="grid-6 col-left"><div class="inside">
		<h4>Undergraduate Students</h4>
		<p>The average annual cost of attendance for a Texas resident living on campus and enrolled in 15 hours per semester is $22,826.</p>
		<p>For out-of-state students, the average annual cost of attendance is $34,526.</p>
	</div></div>
	
	<div class="grid-6 col-right"><div class="inside">
		<h4>Graduate Students</h4>
		<p>The average annual cost of attendance for a Texas resident living on campus and enrolled in 9 hours per semester is $19,800.</p>
		<p>For out-of-state students, the average annual cost of attendance is $26,826.</p>
	</div></div>
	
	<div class="grid-12"><div class="inside">
		<h4>International Students</h4>
		<p><a href="http://international.unt.edu/ISSS/detailed-i-20-expenses" target="_blank" title="Estimated costs for international students (opens in a new window)">Estimated expenses for international students</a> will be provided on their immigration documents by UNT International, which could vary from the above estimates, based upon immigration requirements. Some international students may <a href="/international/texas-resident-tuition" title="Texas in-state resident tuition information for international students">qualify for in-state, resident tuition</a>.</p>
	</div></div>
