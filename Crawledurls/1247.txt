<h3>Fast track your degree and save with the<br /> Eagle Express Tuition Plan</h3>
<p>UNT’s Eagle Express Tuition Plan lets you lock in your total academic costs and save up to $3,000 when you graduate on time. &nbsp;As a transfer student, you qualify for a graduation incentive based on the number of hours you transfer to UNT and how soon you graduate. The graduation incentives you earn will vary according to the chart below, based on a 120-hour degree plan.</p>
<p>Transfer students have a <strong>one-time offer</strong> to choose the Eagle Express plan during your first semester at UNT.</p>
<h3>Take these four easy steps for success</h3>
<p><img style="font-size: 12px;" src="/sites/default/files/u4/four-easy-steps-smal.png" alt="Four easy steps" width="900" height="120" /></p>
<h3>Eligibility</h3>
<p>The Eagle Express plan is available to all transfer students entering UNT.&nbsp;</p>
<p>You must meet <a href="https://registrar.unt.edu/transcripts-and-records/residency-information">Texas residency requirements</a> and be seeking a bachelor's degree.&nbsp;If you do not meet the Texas residency requirements, you will be automatically enrolled in the <a href="/traditionalplan">traditional tuition plan</a>.</p>
<p>If you choose not to opt in to the Eagle Express plan, you will be automatically enrolled in the <a href="/traditionalplan">traditional tuition plan</a>.</p>
<h3>Benefits of Eagle Express</h3>
<ul>
<li>You'll save up to $3,000 if you graduate on time.</li>
<li>You'll know your total academic costs for up to four years, helping you plan expenses. Course fees are included in the locked-in amount.</li>
<li>You'll graduate with less debt.</li>
<li>You'll get the best of both worlds – the best bachelor's degree experience in Texas at an affordable price.</li>
<li>You'll get a head start on your career sooner – whether you want to land a full-time job or continue on to graduate school.</li>
</ul>

<p>If you graduate in the allotted time according to the total number of hours transferred to UNT, you can save up to $3,000 in your final 15 hours with the Eagle Express graduation incentive. You also may be eligible for the <a href="https://registrar.unt.edu/graduation-and-diplomas/1000-tuition-rebate">statewide $1,000 tuition rebate</a>, bringing your total savings to $4,000 for graduating in four years.</p>
