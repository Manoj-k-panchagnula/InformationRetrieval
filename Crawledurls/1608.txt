            	<h1 class="title">The UNT Family</h1>
							<div id="node-7" class="node node-type-page build-mode-full clearfix">
  
  
  
  
  <div class="content">
    <div class="intro">
<p>This is such an exciting time &mdash; not only for your family, but the UNT family of faculty and staff who are committed to your child’s success. This guide is a great source of information about UNT and is where you can learn more about everything from academics and support services to scholarships and security.</a>
<p>We’re so happy your student has chosen to learn, grow and achieve at UNT.</a>
</div>

<div class="last"></div>

<div class="column-container">
<div class="two-1">
<img src="/sites/all/themes/unt/images/13_550_family_weekend_057.jpg" alt="Parents and Student standing by fountain" />
</div>
<div class="two-2">
<h2>UNT provides the best bachelor's degree experience in Texas</h2>
<p>The pursuit of knowledge and the personal growth of our students through an innovative, collaborative learning environment is at the center of UNT’s mission. UNT’s 218 high-quality <a href="http://www.unt.edu/majors/undergradList.htm">degree programs</a> in 12 <a href="http://www.unt.edu/colleges-schools.htm">colleges and schools</a> attract some of the best and brightest students.</p>
<p>UNT has been named a Best in the West college by <em>The Princeton Review</em> for six years and one of <em>America’s 100 Best College Buys&reg;</em> for 20 consecutive years.</p>
<p>Come see us! Our student-led tours are the best way to experience UNT and get all your questions answered. To schedule a tour, call 940-565-4104
or visit the <a href="http://tours.unt.edu">UNT Tours website</a>.</p>
<p><a href="http://apply.unt.edu/excellence?utm_source=excellence&utm_medium=print&utm_campaign=FTICViewbook">See how UNT can help you succeed.</a>
</div>
</div>

<div class="last"></div>

<div class="column-container">
<div class="two-1">
<h2>Affording college</h2>
<p>Your child’s college education is one of the best investments you’ll ever make. The Federal Reserve Bank of New York recently reported that the value of a bachelor’s degree for the average graduate has held near its all-time high of about $300,000. Furthermore, bachelor’s degree holders can expect to earn 78 percent more over the course of a lifetime than those with only a high school diploma. Simply put, a college degree pays off.</p>
<p><a href="/cost/">Learn more about paying for college</a></p>
</div>
<div class="two-2">
<img src="/sites/all/themes/unt/images/15_177_CoEdvbk045.jpg" alt="Students being helped" />
</div>
</div>

<div class="last"></div>

<div class="column-container">
<div class="two-1">
<img src="/sites/all/themes/unt/images/14_094_BurrHughes558B.jpg" alt="Students in collaborative learning space" />
</div>
<div class="two-2">
<h2>Academic success</h2>
<p>UNT students learn in an environment that combines academic excellence, hands-on training and <a href="http://research.unt.edu/">thought-provoking research</a>. We give students an educational experience that challenges and enriches them and gives them the power to compete with anyone, anywhere.</p>
<p><a href="/academics/">Learn more about UNT academics</a></p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="last"></div>

<div class="column-container">
<div class="two-1">
<h2>Support for success</h2>
<p>According to the National Survey of Student Engagement, more students are happy with their educational experiences at UNT, as compared to peer institutions, and would attend the university again.</p>
<p><a href="/support-services/">Learn more about UNT’s support services for your student</a></p>
</div>
<div class="two-2">
<img src="/sites/all/themes/unt/images/14_023_bellon_advising_011_AW.jpg" alt="Student gets help in Willis Library" />
</div>
</div>

<div class="last"></div>

<div class="column-container">
<div class="two-1">
<img src="/sites/default/files/15_242_commencement_fri_213.jpg" alt="UNT Commencement Ceremony" />
</div>
<div class="two-2">
<h2>Parent success</h2>
<p>UNT has an office solely devoted to parent programs, offering many ways for parents to get involved in their student’s college experience. One of the first things you can do to ensure your student makes a successful transition to college is to attend Parent Orientation, which is held concurrently with each Freshman Orientation session.</p>
<p><a href="/stay-involved/">Learn more about how to stay involved</a></p>
</div>
</div>
