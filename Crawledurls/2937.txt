<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>MATERIALS WE RECYCLE <br />(click on links for examples of recyclable materials)<br /></strong></p>
<p><a href="https://facilities.unt.edu/sites/default/files/aluminum.pdf"><strong>Aluminum</strong></a> and <a href="https://facilities.unt.edu/sites/default/files/plastic.pdf"><strong>Plastic</strong></a><br />• Please EMPTY cans and plastic bottles first.<br />• Deposit cans and drink bottles into recycling containers. <br />• Crushed cans preferred. <br />• No glass containers.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Various Grades of <a href="https://facilities.unt.edu/sites/default/files/paper.pdf">Paper </a></strong><br />• Nothing touched by food or water. <br />• No laminated paper. <br />• No tissue or paper towels.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><a href="https://facilities.unt.edu/sites/default/files/cardboard.pdf"><strong>Cardboard </strong></a><br />• Please flatten and place BESIDE the paper recycle bins</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Copier/Printer Toner Cartridges </strong><br />• HP, Lexmark, Dell and other major brands of cartridges ARE accepted. <br />• Remanufactured or refurbished cartridges ARE NOT accepted. <br />• Place on top of paper totes.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Fluorescent Light Bulbs </strong><br />• Call Work Control (565-2700) for drop off location.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><a href="https://facilities.unt.edu/sites/default/files/styrofoam.pdf"><strong>Styrofoam</strong></a><strong> (only white breakable) </strong><br />• CLEAN White styrofoam only. <br />• Call to request a pickup (369-8516).</p><h3 id='downloads'>Downloads</h3><table id="attachments" class="sticky-enabled">
