                <h2 class="title" id="page-title">Payment by Installment Plan</h2>
                        <div class="block block-system block-main block-system-main odd block-without-title" id="block-system-main">
  <div class="block-inner clearfix">
                
    <div class="content clearfix">
      <div  about="/payment-installment-plan" typeof="foaf:Document" id="node-page-53" class="ds-1col node node-page view-mode-full node-not-promoted node-not-sticky author-sgm0071 odd clearfix clearfix">

  
  <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"><p>The installment plan is a contract between the student and the University. By enrolling in the installment plan, a student agrees to comply with its terms and conditions, and failure to do so may result in additional charges. A student who fails to make payment of tuition and fees, including any incidental fees, by the due date may be prohibited from registering for classes until full payment is made. A student who fails to make payment prior to the end of the semester may be denied credit for the courses taken for that semester.</p>
<p>In addition, by enrolling in the installment plan, a student authorizes the University to assign any unpaid tuition and fees covered by the installment plan to a collection agency or credit reporting agency for the purpose of collecting amounts owed at the University’s option. The student agrees to pay all attorney's fees and other reasonable collection costs and charges necessary for the collection of any amount not paid when due.</p>
<h3>Student Pay Plan</h3>
<p>UNT provides the following alternatives for the payment of tuition and fees during the fall and spring semesters:</p>
<ul><li>Full payment of tuition and fees upon registration or by the payment deadline; or</li>
<li>Installment payment plan: consists of an initial down payment of 10%, with 3 additional payments of 30% each. The first payment is due by the payment deadline each semester and additional payments are due by the 3rd of each month. For Fall semesters, payments will be due on October 3rd, November 3rd and December 3rd <strong>(NOTE: For FALL 2016, the third payment due date will be Monday, December 5th instead due to the 3rd falling on a weekend)</strong>. For Spring Semesters, payments will be due on February 3rd, March 3rd and April 3rd. Payment plans are not offered for Summer sessions.<strong>*</strong></li>
<li>NOTE: A $20 fee is added when the installment plan is processed and is due with the initial payment.</li>
<li><a title="Steps to Using the Installment Plan" href="/sites/default/files/Setting%20up%20the%20Student%20IP-for%20website.pdf">Steps to Using the Installment Plan (PDF)</a></li>
</ul><p><em><strong>*</strong> Tuition and fees must be paid in full for each summer term upon registration or by the payment deadline. Payment for any additional fees resulting from Schedule Revision or Add/Drops is due by the end of the Add/Drop period. Tuition payment by installment is not offered during the summer sessions.</em></p>
<h3>Employee Pay Plan</h3>
<p>UNT provides the following options for employees for the payment of tuition and fees during the fall and spring semesters:</p>
<ul type="disc"><li>Full payment of tuition and fees upon registration or by the payment deadline; or</li>
<li>Installment payment plan: consists of an initial down payment of $100.00, with 3 additional payments of 33% each. The first payment is due by the payment deadline each semester and additional payments are due by the 3rd of each month. For Fall semesters, payments will be due on October 3rd, November 3rd and December 3rd <strong>(NOTE: For FALL 2016, the third payment due date will be Monday, December 5th instead due to the 3rd falling on a weekend)</strong>. For Spring Semesters, payments will be due on February 3rd, March 3rd and April 3rd. Payment plans are not offered for Summer sessions.</li>
</ul><p>If an employee selects this plan, there will be no installment fees or late fees associated with this plan during the semester it was selected. Charges due for additional fees resulting from Schedule Revision or Add/Drops will be added to the installment payments. Tuition payment by installment is not offered during the summer sessions.</p>
</div></div></div></div>
