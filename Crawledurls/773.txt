<div id="main">
<h1>Victory Hall Student-Athlete Residences</h1>
<div class="sidebar">
<p class="sideimage"><img src="sidebar-victory.jpg" width="350" height="275" alt="Victory Hall" /></p>
</div>
<p>Victory Hall is a resort among residence halls.</p>
<p>The dormitory offers private and double rooms with ensuite bathrooms and the largest lobby of all of the University’s residence halls.</p>
<p>Located adjacent to the Athletic Center, Victory offers easy access to Champs Cafeteria, the Student-Athlete Academic Center, the Strength and Conditioning Center, the Sports Medicine and Rehabilitation Center, meeting rooms and the multiple practice and game facilities of the Mean Green Village.</p>
<p>Victory Hall has two large courtyards with barbeque grills and a fire pit. Inside, there is a cyber café, a computer lab, a kitchenette, a media room with theater-style seating, classrooms, laundry facilities and a lounge seating area. There are ample entertainment opportunities, including ping pong, foosball and pool tables as well as DVD players and board games that residents can check out. Wireless internet is available throughout the building and courtyards.</p>
<p>It also has a large, well-lit parking lot which is serviced by North Texas shuttles to and from campus, allowing student-athletes to move safely and easily across 35E and eliminating the hassle of finding parking on campus.</p>
