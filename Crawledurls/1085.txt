<div class="singleCol large-12 columns"><h1>The BIG Event 2016</h1>
<p><img alt="" height="17" src="https://www.untalumni.com/image/alumni/bkgd_pagetitle.gif" width="730" /></p>
<p>Join the UNT Alumni Association, UNT students, faculty, staff and fellow alumni as we celebrate a day of service through the Big Event.<br /><br />On <strong>Saturday, April 2, 2016, from 8 a.m.-2 p.m.</strong>, thousands of UNT Eagles will join together in helping our community. Any UNT alum who signs up here will work on the UNT campus for the Stop Hunger Now project packaging meals.<br /><br />Tentative Schedule for UNT Alumni at The Big Event:</p>
<p style="margin-left: 30px;">8-8:45 a.m. -- Welcome all volunteers to The Big Event, Library Mall, UNT Campus<br />9 a.m.-Noon -- Volunteer work assignment; UNT Alumni works with Stop Hunger Now packaging meals, UNT campus<br />Noon-2 p.m. -- Party on the Mall with pizza and music for volunteers.</p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>Register here by Wednesday, March 23, 2016</strong></span>. More details will be emailed to you with the alumni association's community service placement!</p>
