                <h2 class="title" id="page-title">Global Engagement Collaborations and Programs</h2>

<h6 class="toggle" id="window"><a>Education Window (Ventanilla de Educacion)</a></h6>
<div class="animated" id="toggle-window" style="display: none;">
<p>The Ventanilla de Educacion is a collaboration with the Counsel General of Mexico in Dallas. UNT manages an information booth at the Consulate that provides information and resources about education opportunities and pathways to UNT to the Mexican community residing in the DFW area. This program is supported by the Government of Mexico. The Ventanilla de Educacion in Dallas is the third Education Window booth in the U.S.</p>
<table border="0" cellpadding="1" cellspacing="1" height="91" width="1091"><tbody><tr><td><a href="/engagement/education-window-ventanilla-de-educacion"><b>Education Window (Ventanilla de Educacion)</b></a></td>

<div class="animated" id="toggle-conacyt" style="display: none;">

<h6 class="toggle" id="archive"><a>Texas-Mexico Archival Initiative</a></h6>
<div class="animated" id="toggle-archive" style="display: none;">
<p>UNT Libraries is leading a collaboration between public higher education institutions in the State of Texas and Mexico’s General Archives of the Nation (AGN) to digitally archive and preserve documents related to Mexico’s and Texas’ shared histories.</p>

<h6 class="toggle" id="SPIS"><a>Sponsored Program in Information Science</a></h6>
<div class="animated" id="toggle-SPIS" style="display: none;">
<p>Mexico’s General Archives of the Nation (AGN), through the support of CONACYT, is sponsoring AGN federal employees to pursue UNT’s Graduate Academic Certificate in Archival Studies that can lead to a Master’s Degree in Information Science.</p>
