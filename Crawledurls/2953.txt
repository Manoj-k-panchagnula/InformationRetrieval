<p><strong><em>Important Information: </em></strong></p>
<p><strong>Parking Garage Use and Availability:</strong> Garage Access Cards (as indicated on card) are valid only on the 2nd through 4th Floors. 1st Floor Reserved Annual Cards are valid on the first floor with an assigned numbered space. The 5th through 7th floors are reserved for Resident Students Access Cards with a garage permit displayed. </p>
<p>Parking garages at the University of North Texas are closed during University holidays and are repurposed during campus-wide special events. Garages may also be repurposed in the event of emergency or for maintenance. Parking &amp; Transportation Services cannot guarantee that parking garage spaces will be available at all times. Notice will be issued via e-mail before any scheduled holiday or event. Please verify with the Parking &amp; Transportation office that your contact information is up to date.</p>
<p>  Replacement of Garage Access Cards: There is a $25.00 fee to replace a lost/missing/stolen access card.</p>
<p>  <strong>Sharing Garage Access Cards: </strong>Sharing of a garage access card (Pass-Back) is prohibited and is considered Theft of Service in the State of Texas. If a Pass-Back action is detected, the system will automatically terminate your access card and your parking privileges will be revoked. No refund will be issued.</p>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Visitors that are not able to pay will be issued a citation and charged the maximum daily rate.</strong></li>
  <li>Hourly  parking is available at the posted rates</li>
  <li>There  are no in and out privileges without a contract</li>
  <li>Lost  tickets will be charged the daily maximum</li>
  <li>Semester  and annual parking is available via access card only. Access cards are  available from the Parking Office. Access card registration is available  through the link on the top right of this page.</li>
  <li>Department  validations are accepted only with prior approval from the Parking Office</li>
  <li>No  storage of vehicles is allowed</li>
  <li>No  repairing of vehicles (including changing oil) is allowed in the garage</li>
  <li>No open flames are allowed within the parking  structure.</li>
  <li>No Motorcycles</li>
  <li>No Checks</li>
