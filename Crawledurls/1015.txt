  <h2 class="node__title node-title">FAQs</h2>
  
  <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"><p>Welcome to the website for the <a href="/about-human-resources" title="About Human Resources">Human Resources Department</a>. Our department supports more than 10,000 employees at locations in Dallas, Denton and Fort Worth.</p>
<ul><li>Find recent <a href="https://hr.untsystem.edu/news-human-resources">News from Human Resources</a>.</li>
<li>Who can help? <a href="https://hr.untsystem.edu/we-are-here-help-you-heres-our-human-resources-team">Find the Human Resources team</a>.</li>
<li>Questions about hours worked or time keeping? <strong>Contact your supervisor</strong> who submits time and attendance records to Payroll.</li>
<li>Questions about Payroll or W-2/W-4 forms? Contact <a href="http://bsc.untsystem.edu">Business Support Services</a>, or find your W-2 by logging in to EIS (MyUNT, et al).</li>
</ul><p>Here are answers to the questions that we are frequently asked:</p>
<div class="accButton"><button class="expand AllAcc">Expand All</button></div>
<h2>Employment</h2>
<ul class="accordian"><li>
<h2>How do I apply for a job?</h2>
<div class="info">
<p>Vacant positions for faculty, staff and student employees are posted at these websites:</p>
<ul><li><a href="https://jobs.unt.edu/applicants/jsp/shared/frameset/Frameset.jsp?time=1400435158390" target="_blank">UNT- jobs.unt.edu</a></li>
<li><a href="https://www.unthscjobs.com/applicants/jsp/shared/frameset/Frameset.jsp?time=1400262433308" title="jobs at UNTHSC">UNTHSC - unthscjobs.com</a></li>
<li><a href="https://unt-dallas.peopleadmin.com/" target="_blank">UNT Dallas, UNT System Administration, UNT Dallas College of Law - unt-dallas.peopleadmin.com</a></li>
<li><a href="http://vpaa.unt.edu/faculty-recruitment.htm" title="UNT Office of the Provost">UNT faculty recruiting - vpaa.unt.edu/faculty-recruitment.htm</a></li>
</ul><p>Human Resources Talent Acquisition and Campus HR Departments work with hiring managers to recruit and interview job applications. <a href="/job-applicants/how-apply-job">All applications must be made online</a>.</p>
</div>
</li>
<li>
<h2>How do I transfer to a different job?</h2>
<div class="info">
<p>If you are an employee and have been in your current job at least six (6) months, you may apply to transfer to a position available in another UNT System location and/or department. You may apply online:</p>
<ul><li>UNT - <a href="https://jobs.unt.edu/applicants/jsp/shared/frameset/Frameset.jsp?time=1400435158390" target="_blank">jobs.unt.edu</a></li>
<li>UNTHSC - <a href="https://www.unthscjobs.com/applicants/jsp/shared/frameset/Frameset.jsp?time=1400262433308" title="jobs at UNTHSC">unthscjobs.com</a></li>
<li>UNT Dallas, UNT System Administration and UNT Dallas College of Law - <a href="https://unt-dallas.peopleadmin.com/" target="_blank">unt-dallas.peopleadmin.com</a></li>
</ul><p>If you are joining a UNT location from another State of Texas university or agency, your prior state service, vacation and sick leave also may transfer.</p>
<ul><li><a href="https://hr.untsystem.edu/employees/prior-state-service-verification">Learn more about prior state service</a>.</li>
</ul></div>
</li>
<li>
<h2>Is a drug test or criminal background or history check required?</h2>
<div class="info">
<p>An offer of employment may be made at all system components, but it is contingent on the results of a criminal history check. All positions at UNTHSC also require a drug screen. Potential employees receive instructions about completing the criminal background check and the drug test in the offer of employment email.</p>
</div>
</li>
<li>
<h2>How do I post a job vacancy for my department?</h2>
<div class="info">
<p>Contact Talent Acquisition <a href="mailto:talentacquisiton@untsystem.edu" title="Talent Acquisition">recruiters and sourcing specialists</a> in Human Resources. Our mission is to provide support to help you identify candidates who will be successful, and to do so as quickly as possible. Talent Acquisition staff also will assist with paperwork,  interviews and <a href="https://hr.untsystem.edu/new-employee-onboarding">onboarding.</a></p>
</div>
</li>
<li>
<h2>How can I obtain <a href="https://hr.untsystem.edu/verification-employment">verification of employment</a>?</h2>
<div class="info">
<p>If you need proof of employment for financial or other personal reasons, please forward your request to Human Resources.  <a href="https://hr.untsystem.edu/verification-employment">Learn more</a>. Verification of employment will be provided in writing within 72 hours or three (3) working days.</p>
</div>
</li>
<li>
<h2>Where can I view my paycheck?</h2>
<div class="info">
<p>You can log in at <a href="https://my.unt.edu/psp/papd01/EMPLOYEE/EMPL/h/?tab=NTPA_GUEST">MyUNT</a>, <a href="https://my.unt.edu/psp/papd01/EMPLOYEE/EMPL/h/?tab=HSPA_GUEST">MyHSC</a> <a href="https://my.unt.edu/psp/papd01/EMPLOYEE/EMPL/h/?tab=DLPA_GUEST">MyUNTDallas</a>, <a href="https://my.unt.edu/psp/papd01/EMPLOYEE/EMPL/h/?tab=SYPA_GUEST">MySystem </a>or <a href="https://my.unt.edu/psp/papd01/EMPLOYEE/EMPL/h/?tab=JDPA_GUEST">MyLaw</a> with your EUID and password to view your check. After logging in, you also will find your W-2, personal information options, training opportunities, vacation and sick leave accrual and access to the Employee Assistance Program. Payroll is managed by the UNT System Controller and the department is physically located at the <a href="http://bsc.untsystem.edu">Business Service Center</a>, 1112 Dallas Drive, Suite 4000, Denton, Texas.</p>
</div>
</li>
<li>
<h2>How do I complete Form I-9 for Employment Eligibility Verification?</h2>
<div class="info">
<p>Newly hired employees receive instructions by email about the Employment Eligibility Verification (<a href="http://www.uscis.gov/sites/default/files/files/form/i-9.pdf" title="Employment Eligibility Verification Form I-9">I-9 form</a> or <a href="http://www.uscis.gov/sites/default/files/files/form/i-9.pdf" title="Employment Eligibility Verification Form I-9">Form I-9</a>) process. The form must be submitted to the hiring manager who must ensure that eligibility is verified by Human Resources and the federal <a href="http://www.uscis.gov/e-verify/what-e-verify">E-Verify</a> progam. This must be completed within three (3) days of starting work. Newly hired employees may not be added to the payroll until eligibility is verified.  Employees who work off-site will receive instructions about how to have Form I-9 documents reviewed by a notary public.</p>
<ul><li>Find explanatory information from the <a href="http://www.dhs.gov/homeland-security-forms" title="Homeland Security">Department of Homeland Security</a> and <a href="http://www.uscis.gov/sites/default/files/files/form/m-274.pdf" target="_blank" title="Guidance for Completing Form 1-9 Employment Eligibility Verification">U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services</a>.</li>
<li>Find instructions for <a href="//hr.untsystem.edu/sites/default/files/FormsForAllLocations/form_i-9-employee-training.pdf">Form I-9 completion for employees</a>.</li>
<li>Find instructions for <a href="//hr.untsystem.edu/sites/default/files/FormsForAllLocations/form_i-9-employer-training.pdf">Form I-9 completion for employers</a>.</li>
</ul></div>
</li>
</ul><h2>Benefits</h2>
<ul class="accordian"><li>
<h2>How much does health insurance cost?</h2>
<div class="info">
<p>The <a href="https://hr.untsystem.edu/employees/benefits-insurance" title="Insurance benefits">group insurance benefit plan</a> is administered by the Employees Retirement System of Texas (<a href="http://www.ers.state.tx.us/" target="_blank">ERS</a>) on behalf of the State of Texas. The cost for employees is determined by contracted negotiations and are published each year during annual enrollment, usually in July. <a href="https://www.ers.state.tx.us/Retirees/Calculate-Rates/" title="Insurance benefits rate calculation">Find the ERS cost calculator</a>.</p>
<p>Full-time employees (30 hours per week or more, or 75 percent FTE) and part-time employees (20-29 hours per week or 50-74 percent FTE) at each location are eligible for <a href="/employees/benefits-insurance" title="Insurance benefits">benefits </a>that include medical, dental, prescription drugs, optional term life insurance, short term disability and long term disability, accidental death and dismemberment and dependent term life. </p>
</div>
</li>
<li>
<h2>When is benefits enrollment?</h2>
<div class="info">
<p><a href="/employees/benefits-insurance" title="Insurance benefits">Benefits-eligible employees</a> must enroll in the optional benefits (dental, optional term life insurance, short term and long term disability, accidental death and dismemberment and dependent term life insurance) during the first 31 days of employment. Full-time employees automatically will be enrolled in Employee-Only medical coverage, but must enroll dependents under medical coverage during the first 60 days of employment. Part-time employees must enroll themselves and any dependents into the medical plan during the first 60 days of employment.</p>
<p>If the 31-day deadline or the 60-day deadline is missed, employees must wait until the next Annual Enrollment period, or may enroll if they experience a qualifying life event such as marriage, divorce, birth of child.</p>
</div>
</li>
<li>
<h2>How do I coordinate my family’s medical care?</h2>
<div class="info">
<p>Enrolled employees can access information and search for in-network providers or facilities, or get a new HealthSelect ID card on the <a href="https://www.ers.state.tx.us/Former/Health/HealthSelect/" target="_blank">ERS website</a> and <a href="http://healthselectoftexas.welcometouhc.com/" target="_blank">United Healthcare HealthSelect website</a>.</p>
<p>Create a user ID/password to access information. Enrolled employees will need their member ID number and Group/Account number, which is on the ID card sent to the enrolled employee by United Healthcare. Or, call United Healthcare at <span class="baec5a81-e4d6-4674-97f3-e9220f0136c1" style="white-space: nowrap;">866-336-9371. </span><a href="https://www.ers.state.tx.us/Retirees/Calculate-Rates/" title="Insurance benefits rate calculation">Find the ERS insurance rates calculator</a>.</p>
</div>
</li>
<li>
<h2>How do I access FMLA?</h2>
<div class="info">
<p>The <a href="http://www.dol.gov/whd/fmla/" title="FMLA facts">Family and Medical Leave Act</a> is a federal law that entitles eligible employees of covered employers to take 12 weeks of upaid, job-protected leave for specified family and medical reasons. Review <a href="/employees/family-and-medical-leave-act" title="FMLA">FMLA facts and forms</a>, and then send the request for FMLA to the Human Resources <a href="mailto:fmla@untsystem.edu" target="_blank" title="Family and Medical Leave Act">FMLA coordinator</a>, who will respond with next steps. Parental leave also is available.</p>
</div>
</li>
<li>
<h2>I need help planning my retirement.</h2>
<div class="info">
<p>Contact the Human Resources <a href="https://hr.untsystem.edu/we-are-here-help-you-heres-our-human-resources-team">Benefits Team</a> or call the Human Resources Records and Support Center at <span class="baec5a81-e4d6-4674-97f3-e9220f0136c1" style="white-space: nowrap;">1-855-878-7650 </span>to ask retirement questions.</p>
<ul><li>If you are contributing to the Teacher Retirement System of Texas (<a href="http://www.trs.state.tx.us/retirees.jsp?submenu=faqs&amp;page_id=/onac/faq_mytrs" target="_blank">TRS</a>), you can review retirement information online or call <span class="baec5a81-e4d6-4674-97f3-e9220f0136c1" style="white-space: nowrap;">1-800-223-8778</span>. (ERS manages insurance for retirees. TRS manages retirement for employees of higher education institutions. ERS manages retirement for certain state agencies - NOT for higher education.)</li>
<li>If you are contributing to the Optional Retirement Program (<a href="http://www.thecb.state.tx.us/index.cfm?objectid=08C6BE55-CC42-7676-C2D178FDFEC20EF6" title="Optional Retirement Program, Texas Higher Education Coordinating Board">ORP</a>), your investment vendor can help answer your retirement questions.</li>
<li>Access <a href="http://www.trs.state.tx.us/retirees.jsp?submenu=faqs&amp;page_id=/onac/faq_mytrs" target="_blank">MyTRS</a> or <a href="https://www.myretirementmanager.com/?unts" target="_blank">Retirement Manager</a> to learn more.</li>
<li>Learn about <a href="//hr.untsystem.edu/sites/default/files/FormsForAllLocations/texassaver-for-highered.pdf">Texas Saver 457 </a>Accounts.</li>
</ul></div>
</li>
<li>
<h2>Are there continuing education opportunities?</h2>
<div class="info">
<p>Human Resources <a href="/meet-human-resources-team" title="Talent Management">Talent Management </a>coordinates and offers a variety of classes for all employees. These include computer, software and technical skills, interpersonal skills and leadership and management techniques. A class schedule is regularly emailed to all employees and published online at a <a href="https://untranet.unt.edu/untsystem/UNT%20System%20HR/talent_management/SitePages/Home.aspx" title="Talent Management classes (SharePoint site that requires EUID.)">SharePoint website</a> which requires an EUID to log in. </p>
</div>
</li>
<li>
<h2>Is a tuition discount available for employees?</h2>
<div class="info">
<p>The <a href="http://policy.unt.edu/sites/default/files/untpolicy/2.2.14_FacultyStaffRetireeDependentScholarship_Sept2015.pdf">tuition scholarship benefit</a> is available to faculty, staff, certain dependents and retirees at UNT, UNTHSC and UNT Dallas. The scholarship pays the cost of Board of Regents-designated tuition and mandatory fees. Scholarship beneficiaries first must meet academic requirements for admission, and then may apply for the scholarship by the 12th day of class for each semester enrolled. To learn more, contact the Office of Student Accounting and University Cashiering at <a href="http://essc.unt.edu/saucs/">UNT </a>or the Office of Admissions at <a href="http://www.untdallas.edu/" title="UNT Dallas">UNT Dallas</a>, <a href="http://lawschool.untsystem.edu/" title="UNT Dallas College of Law">College of Law</a> and the <a href="http://web.unthsc.edu/" title="UNTHSC">UNT Health Science Center</a>. The State of Texas also offers various tuition exemptions through its <a href="http://www.collegeforalltexans.com/apps/financialaid/tofa.cfm?Kind=E" title="College for All Texans ">College for all Texans</a> program.</p>
