    <p>The University of North Texas (UNT) is the home of 36,000 students from all walks of life — from students who are the first in their family to earn a degree to those who transfer to the university to complete their educational journey.</p>
<p>Despite different backgrounds, UNT students have this in common — they work hard and pursue excellence in everything they do. The university has high expectations of students, but it also provides strong resources, such as its 1,500 outstanding faculty, to help them personally and academically.</p>
<p>The VPAA is committed to providing the tools and resources necessary for faculty to achieve our mission.</p>
<p>If you have questions or comments regarding faculty resources, please <a href="/about-provost-office/contact-us" title="Contact the Provost Office">contact us</a>.&nbsp; This page contains pdf documents.&nbsp; <a href="http://get.adobe.com/reader/" target="_blank" title="Adobe Reader Free Download (opens in new window)">You can download a free pdf reader here.</a></p>
