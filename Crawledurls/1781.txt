                                                              <p>The University of North Texas is accredited to award bachelor's, master's and doctoral degrees by the following:</p>

<p><strong>The Southern Association of Colleges and Schools</strong> <strong>Commission on Colleges</strong></p>

<p>The University of North Texas is accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools Commission on Colleges to award baccalaureate, masters and doctorate degrees.&#160; Contact the Commission on Colleges at 1866 Southern Lane, Decatur, Georgia 30033-4097 or call 404-679-4500 for questions about the accreditation of the University of North Texas.&#160;</p>

<p>Note: The Commission should be contacted only if there is evidence that appears to support the institution's significant non-compliance with a requirement or standard.&#160; Normal inquiries about UNT, such as admission requirements, financial aid, and educational programs, should be addressed directly to UNT and not the Commission's office.&#160;</p>

<p style="margin-left:.5in">&#160;</p>
