						<div id="page-title"><h2>Partnerships</h2></div>												<p>UNT has always been on the forefront of collaborating with industry, and with the development of Discovery Park, the resulting synergy leads to matchless incubation and innovation of discoveries inside and beyond.&nbsp; UNT&rsquo;s Discovery Park embraces collaboration and networking among academic, public, and private partners that leads to the technology-based economic vitality and development of the local and regional communities.</p>
