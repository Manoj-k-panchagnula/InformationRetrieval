    <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"><p>Founded in 1890, the University of North Texas is one of the oldest public universities in the North Texas region. In the 125 years of our history, we’re proud to say we’ve developed a culture that is all our own. As we pass our traditions from one generation of the Mean Green family to the next, we celebrate the spirit of UNT.</p>
