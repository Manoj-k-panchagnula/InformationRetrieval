    <p><strong>By <a href="mailto:Jacob.Flores@unt.edu">Jacob Flores</a>, UIT Support Services Manager</strong>&nbsp;</p>
<h3><strong>Office 365 ProPlus now available to all current students through EagleConnect!</strong></h3>
<h4><strong>Who is eligible for Office 365 ProPlus?&nbsp; How long can it be used?</strong></h4>
<p>This service is only available to current UNT students. Once you’re no longer a student, your EagleConnect ID will no longer be able to authenticate an Office 365 ProPlus application installation for use.&nbsp;</p>
<h4><strong>What is included with Office 365 ProPlus?</strong></h4>
<ul>
<li>Office 2013 for Windows 7, Windows 8, and Windows 8.1</li>
<li>Office 2011 for Mac OSX 10.5.8 and newer</li>
<li>Office Mobile for iOS 6.1 and newer</li>
<li>Office Mobile for Android 4.0 and newer</li>
</ul>
<p>You will be able to use Office on <strong>up to 5 Windows or Mac machines</strong> and use Office Mobile on <strong>up to 5 mobile devices</strong>.</p>
<h4><strong>How does this differ from EagleConnect’s OneDrive Office Web Apps that are already available?</strong></h4>
<p>The OneDrive Office Web Apps are great solution for managing your Office documents on the go, since they may be accessed and modified anywhere you have internet and a web browser.&nbsp; The applications included with Office 365 ProPlus are the full standalone applications and can be used “offline,” much like you’re used to using on your desktops and laptops.&nbsp; You simply install the suite, sign in with your EagleConnect account, and you’re up and running.</p>
<p>Check out Microsoft’s <a href="http://blogs.technet.com/b/educloud/archive/2013/10/15/student-advantage-and-office-365-proplus-faq.aspx">Office 365 ProPlus FAQ</a> for more information.</p>
<h4><strong>How do I start using Office 365 ProPlus?</strong></h4>
<p>Desktop applications</p>
<p>1)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Log into <a href="http://eagleconnect.unt.edu/login/">EagleConnect</a>.</p>
<p>2)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Click the gear icon near your name at the top right and select “Office 365 Settings.”</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<img src="/benchmarks/sites/default/files/HDFYI1_1.png" alt="Office 365 ProPlus Screen 1" width="416" height="258" /></p>
<p>3)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Select “software” from the menu on the left.</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<img src="/benchmarks/sites/default/files/HEFYI2.png" alt="Office 365 ProPlus Screen 2" width="344" height="413" /></p>
<p>4)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; It’s best to go with the <strong>“recommended”</strong> version of Office. For instance, Windows users may be using a 64-bit operating system, but most applications and plugins that integrate with Microsoft Office will be built for the 32-bit version of office. You may select an alternate version if you prefer, however, I’d suggest only advanced users venture into this territory.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<img src="/benchmarks/sites/default/files/HDFYI3.png" alt="Office 365 ProPlus Screen 3" width="635" height="404" /></p>
<p>5)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Select “install” to download the installer for your Office application.</p>
<p>6)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Open this downloaded file to start the install.</p>
<p>7)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Please do not go offline or restart your computer during this process; it is actively installing the software at this point. You’ll likely see a status indicator like this one:</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<img src="/benchmarks/sites/default/files/HDFYI4.png" alt="365 ProPlus Screen 4" width="341" height="80" /></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>8)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; While it’s installing, it will offer to go ahead and sign into your Office 365 suite.</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<img src="/benchmarks/sites/default/files/HEFYI5.png" alt="365 ProPlus Screen 5" width="640" height="358" /></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>9)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Click “Sign in” and you’ll be prompted for a username. Enter your EagleConnect ID (usually in the form of <a href="mailto:FirstLast@my.unt.edu">FirstLast@my.unt.edu</a>) and click “Next.”</p>
<p>10)&nbsp;&nbsp; The next screen will ask to verify which type of account you’re using to sign in. Since this is a service through EagleConnect from UNT, select “Organizational account.”</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<img src="/benchmarks/sites/default/files/HDFYI6.png" alt="365 ProPlus Screen 6" width="391" height="480" /></p>
<p>11)&nbsp;&nbsp; At the next page, enter your password and select “Sign in.”&nbsp; At this point, the installer will offer to give you a walkthrough of Office 365 or skip to the end where you’ll be presented with a larger progress indicating the status of your Office install.&nbsp; Once you see the screen below, you’re all set!</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<img src="/benchmarks/sites/default/files/HDFYI7.png" alt="365 ProPlus Screen 7" width="640" height="358" /></p>
<h4><strong style="font-size: 1em;">Mobile applications</strong><span style="font-size: 1em;">&nbsp;</span></h4>
<p>1)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Download Microsoft’s “Office Mobile” application from the app store.</p>
<p>2)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Upon opening the application, you’ll be prompted to read over the terms of use. You must accept these terms to use the Office Mobile applications.</p>
<p>3)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Select “Activate Office” and enter your EagleConnect ID (usually <a href="mailto:FirstLast@my.unt.edu">FirstLast@my.unt.edu</a>) in the username field.</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<img src="/benchmarks/sites/default/files/HDFYI8A.png" alt="365 ProPlus Screen 8A" width="270" height="480" /><img src="/benchmarks/sites/default/files/HDFYI8B.png" alt="365 ProPlus Screen 8B" width="270" height="480" /></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>4)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; The next screen will ask to verify which type of account you’re using to sign in.&nbsp; Since this is a service through EagleConnect from UNT, select “Organizational account.”&nbsp; At the next page, enter your password and select “Sign in.”</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<img src="/benchmarks/sites/default/files/HDFYI9A.png" alt="365 ProPlus Screen 9A" width="270" height="480" /><img src="/benchmarks/sites/default/files/HDFYI9B.png" alt="365 ProPlus Screen 9B" width="270" height="480" /></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>5)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Once you’re sign in, you should be all set!</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<img src="/benchmarks/sites/default/files/HDFYI10A.png" alt="365 ProPlus Screen 10A" width="270" height="480" /><img src="/benchmarks/sites/default/files/HDFYI10B.png" alt="365 ProPlus Screen 10B" width="270" height="480" /></p>
<h4><strong style="font-size: 1em;">Having trouble downloading or installing Office 365 ProPlus?</strong></h4>
<ul>
<li>For the desktop installation, please be sure you don’t lose internet access or restart your computer during the install.</li>
<li><a href="http://support.microsoft.com/kb/2827031">Office 365 installation error “Something went wrong”</a>.</li>
<li><a href="http://support.microsoft.com/kb/2822317">General troubleshooting for installing Office 365</a>.</li>
</ul>
<h4><strong>Having trouble logging in?</strong></h4>
<ul>
<li>See if you can log into your <a href="http://eagleconnect.unt.edu/login/">EagleConnect</a> account.</li>
<li>If you can’t log into EagleConnect, ensure you can log into the <a href="https://ams.unt.edu/">Account Management System</a> with your EUID and password. While you’re there, ensure you’re still using the correct EagleConnect ID.</li>
</ul>
<p>As always, if you have any problems, feel free to contact the <a href="http://helpdesk.unt.edu/">UIT Helpdesk</a>.&nbsp;</p>
