	<h2>
		(940) 565-2790</h2>

	<p>
		The Student Health and Wellness Center Pharmacy is a non-profit, state owned and fully licensed pharmacy.&nbsp; All eligible students may use the SHWC Pharmacy.&nbsp; If you are not currently enrolled in classes and are unsure of your eligibility, please call (940) 369-8543 to speak with a SHWC Billing representative to determine if you are still able to use this service.</p>

	<h2>
		Location and Hours</h2>

	<h2>
		Prescription Orders</h2>

	<p>
		The SHWC&nbsp;Pharmacy fills prescriptions obtained from SHWC medical providers as well as outside medical providers.&nbsp;&nbsp;Your primary physician may call your prescription in to us at (940) 565-2790 or fax us at (940) 565-3150.&nbsp; Prescriptions not picked up within 7 days will be returned to stock.</p>

	<h2>
		Prescription Refills</h2>

	<p>
		You can request refills in advance by calling (940) 565-2790.&nbsp; Please have your prescription number ready to give to the technician when calling.&nbsp; Please be aware that if your prescription is out of refills it may take up to 48 hours for additional refills to be obtained through your physician.</p>

	<h2>
		Prescription Transfers</h2>

	<p>
		Do you currently have a refill at a different pharmacy that you would like to fill here at UNT?&nbsp;&nbsp;No problem!&nbsp; Bring in your latest prescription label or receipt and we&#39;ll take care of the rest.&nbsp; If you don&#39;t have either a label or receipt just provide us with your name and date of birth, pharmacy name and phone number and prescription number or drug name.</p>

	<h2>
		Prescription&nbsp;Insurance</h2>

	<p>
		The SHWC Pharmacy accepts&nbsp;the University endorsed student insurance policy with co-pays of $10 for generic drugs,&nbsp;$35 for brand name drugs&nbsp;for up to a 30 day supply.&nbsp; As of October 1, 2012 students on the student insurance plan can receive most&nbsp;contraception, including birth control pills, at no co-pay.</p>

	<p>
		The SHWC Pharmacy also accepts some outside prescription insurance cards, like Medco, PAID, Caremark and Blue Cross, that contain one of the following&nbsp;BINs (bank identification numbers):</p>

	<p>
		<strong>004336&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;610014&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;610029&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; 610239&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; 610415&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; 610502&nbsp;&nbsp;</strong></p>

	<p>
		If unsure just present your card at the SHWC Pharmacy and we can let you know if we accept that insurance plan.&nbsp;&nbsp;If we do not accept your insurance plan, we will be happy to give you a price check as we try to keep our prescription prices affordable, even without insurance.</p>

	<p>
		At this time we are unable to accept Tricare insurance.</p>

	<h2>
		Special Orders</h2>

	<p>
		If the SHWC Pharmacy does not stock the medication you need, we may be able to special order it for you.&nbsp; Before the order is placed, we will give you an estimated price and have you sign a form that states the conditions for the special order.&nbsp; Refills for the same medication will not require you to sign a new form.&nbsp; Just call us&nbsp;a few days prior to needing the medication.</p>

	<h2>
		Over the Counter Medications (OTCs)</h2>

	<p>
		SHWC Pharmacy also offers a variety of over the counter (OTC) products, including Plan-B,&nbsp;for purchase without a prescription.&nbsp; This service is open to students, faculty and staff with a UNT identification card.&nbsp;&nbsp;<a href="/sites/default/files/healthcenter/otc_web_link.pdf" target="_self" title="OTC Products">Click here&nbsp;</a>for a list of current items.</p>

	<h2>
		Other Services</h2>

	<p>
		We also welcome any medication questions and are happy to provide you with oral and written information about any medication.</p>

	<p>
		The SHWC Pharmacy will not disclose Protected Health Information about our&nbsp;patients without prior written consent from the patient on a per request basis.&nbsp;&nbsp;For patients under 18 years of age, parents may request most medical information.&nbsp; Call for details at (940) 565-2790.</p>

	<p>
		For medical or clinical records please contact the&nbsp;Medical Records department at (940) 565-3541.</p>
