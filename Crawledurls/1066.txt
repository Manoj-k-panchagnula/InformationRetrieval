<div class="singleCol large-12 columns"><h1 id="ContentPageName">FAQs</h1>
<div class="clearBoth">&#160;</div>
<div id="ContentMiddle">
<h2>Where is the UNT Alumni Center?</h2>
<p>The alumni center and association office is located at 801 N. Texas Blvd., Ste. 170 in Denton, Texas, in the Gateway Center. We are open from 8 am-5 pm, Monday through Friday.</p>
<hr />
<h2>If I&#8217;m an alumnus/a, aren&#8217;t I already a member of the alumni association?</h2>
<p>No. You must join the association. You can join <a href="http://www.untalumni.com/alumni/pages/join-now">online</a>, by phone or in person. Prior to graduation, you can become a member of the alumni association by signing over your $10 property damage waiver. This will pay for your first year in the alumni association, after that you can renew as an annual member.</p>
<hr />
<h2>How do I become a member of the alumni association?</h2>
<p>The UNT Alumni Association has a dues based membership. You can join <a href="http://www.untalumni.com/alumni/pages/join-now">online</a>, call 940-565-2834 or stop by the Alumni Center to pay your annual dues. These dues support regional and affinity networking programs, homecoming activities and alumni scholarships.</p>
<hr />
<h2>How can I get a replacement membership card?</h2>
<p>&#160;</p>
<p>Please contact the alumni association at <a class="agreen" href="mailto:alumni@unt.edu?subject=Website%20Inquiry">alumni@unt.edu</a> or 940-565-2834 for a replacement card.</p>
<hr />
<h2>Is my membership contribution tax&#8211;deductible?</h2>
<p>Yes, your membership contribution is considered tax-deductible. Please consult your tax adviser for details.</p>
<hr />
<h2>How do I get a UNT license plate?</h2>
<p>Please visit <a href="http://www.myplates.com/landing/Passenger/91">myplates.com</a> to obtain the official license plates of UNT.</p>
<hr />
<h2>Do you offer insurance to alumni?</h2>
<p>Yes. Please visit, <a href="http://www.alumniinsuranceprogram.com/iv/index_3plans.asp?assnID=168" target="_blank">http://www.alumniinsuranceprogram.com/iv/index_3plans.asp?assnID=168</a> for more details on insurance programs.</p>
<hr />
<h2>Can I buy athletic tickets through the alumni association?</h2>
<p>To purchase athletic tickets, please call 940-565-2527 or visit <a href="http://www.meangreensports.com/" target="_blank">http://www.meangreensports.com</a>.</p>
<hr />
<h2>Why am I not receiving the UNT magazine?</h2>
<p>To ensure you are receiving your copy of <em>The North Texan</em>, please make sure we have your current address in UNT's database. You can visit <a href="http://www.unt.edu/northtexan" target="_blank">http://www.unt.edu/northtexan </a>and click on Contact Us to update your information.</p>
<hr />
<h2>How do I obtain a copy of my transcript?</h2>
<p>Please contact the registrar&#8217;s office at the University of North Texas to obtain an official copy of your transcript, <a href="http://essc.unt.edu/registrar/transcrip.htm" target="_blank">http://essc.unt.edu/registrar/transcrip.htm.</a></p>
<hr />
<h2>Do I have to be a graduate to join the alumni association?</h2>
<p>No, anyone can join the alumni association. Please visit<a href="http://www.untalumni.com/membership.php"> http://www.untalumni.com/membership</a> for more information.</p>
<hr />
<h2>Can I read <em>The North Texan</em> online?</h2>
<p>Yes. Please visit <a href="http://www.unt.edu/northtexan" target="_blank">http://www.unt.edu/northtexan</a>.</p>
<hr />
<h2>How do I make a gift to the alumni association?</h2>
<p>Click on the <a href="http://www.untalumni.com/alumni/pages/donate">&#8220;Give&#8221;</a> button here or on the home page, or contact the alumni association at 940-565-2834.</p>
<hr />
<h2>Do I have to be a member of the alumni association to join the Rec Center?</h2>
<p>In order to join the Pohl Rec Center, you must be a current student, faculty/staff member or an alumni association member. Please visit the <a href="http://recsports.unt.edu/about/rec_center">Pohl Rec Center</a>for more information.</p>
<hr />
<h2>How can I get a campus tour?</h2>
<p>Campus tours are conducted by the Eagle Ambassadors. Located inside the Eagle Student Service Center (ESSC), they can be reached online at <a href="http://tours.unt.edu/">Tours</a> or at 940-565-4104.</p>
<hr />
<h2>How can I update my address/contact information?</h2>
<p>Contact the alumni association at 940-565-2834 or at <a href="mailto:alumni@unt.edu">alumni@unt.edu</a>.</p>
<p>&#160;</p>
