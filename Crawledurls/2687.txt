				<h2>
					Parent Association Membership</h2>

				<h3>
					Why should you join the UNT Parent Association?</h3>

				<p>
					There are many benefits to joining the Parent Association, including</p>

				<ul>
					<li>
						10% discount on all school supplies, clothing, and gifts at the UNT Bookstore (excludes textbooks and computer hardware and accessories.)</li>
					<li>
						Discount on Family Weekend</li>
					<li>
						Parent Calendar outlining important dates throughout the year</li>
					<li>
						Monthly electronic newsletters with important Parent Association news as well as university updates</li>
				</ul>

				<p>
					Benefits for students include:</p>

				<ul>
					<li>
						Scholarships awarded each spring to students of Parent Association members.</li>
					<li>
						Roadside Assistance Program - Students of Parent Association members receive a booklet listing the names and phone numbers of Association members willing to be contacted if the student encounters problems while on the road. Participants in this program span the state of Texas as well other states.</li>
					<li>
						Finals goodie boxes which are distributed to students of Parent Association members at the end of the fall and spring semesters.</li>
				</ul>

				<p>
					Become a member today and complete the membership registration online form below OR download the paper form, fill it out and mail&nbsp;it back to us with payment.</p>

				<p>
					Annual memberships are good through August 31st and cost $70.00. Two-year memberships are available for $125. Lifetime memberships are available for $300 and, like the name says, lasts a lifetime. A portion of all memberships go towards scholarships.</p>

				<p>
					<a class="button" href="https://studentaffairs.unt.edu/form/parent-association-sign" target="_blank">Parent Association Membership Registration - online form</a></p>

				<p>
					<a class="button" href="http://studentaffairs.unt.edu/sites/default/files/otp/documents/PA%20Membership%20Form%202013.pdf" target="_blank">Parent Association Membership Registration - paper form</a></p>

				<h3>
					Parent Association Newsletters</h3>

				<p>
					For the current and past issues of the Parent Association Newsletter, see the&nbsp;<a href="http://studentaffairs.unt.edu/parent-association#newsletters" target="_blank">Newsletter Archive</a>.</p>

				<p>
					As a Parent Association member you are automatically signed up to receive detailed parent newsletters, university information, and other information specific only to Parent Association members.</p>

				<p>
					If you are not a Parent Association member and would like to subscribe to our newsletter, simply send an e-mail to&nbsp;<a href="mailto:parents@unt.edu">parents@unt.edu</a>. If you ever wish to be removed from our distribution list, simply send another e-mail to&nbsp;<a href="mailto:parents@unt.edu">parents@unt.edu</a>&nbsp;with the subject line, &quot;Unsubscribe.&quot;</p>

				<h3>
					Join the UNT Parent Association on Facebook!</h3>

				<p>
					Are you a parent or family member of a current or prospective UNT student? Join the conversation on the <a href="https://www.facebook.com/groups/31723060803/" target="_blank">Mean Green Family facebook page</a>.</p>

				<p>
					Parent Association members are encouraged to join the <a href="https://www.facebook.com/groups/1504421759874500/" target="_blank">closed facebook group for members only</a>.</p>

						<h1>
							Parent Association Scholarships</h1>

						<p>
							The UNT Parent Association awards scholarships in the spring semester each academic year. Two different types of scholarships are awarded&mdash;the Scholastic Scholarship and the Draw, or External Scholarship. The number of scholarships given in each category depends on the availability of funds with a minimum of five $500 scholarships awarded in the Draw category. For each recipient, the amount awarded is split evenly between the following fall and spring semesters. The scholarships are given to an undergraduate or graduate student who has attended one full semester at UNT. Summer sessions are not considered full semesters. Students may re-apply for the scholarships annually and students can receive a Parent Association scholarship multiple times during their collegiate experience at UNT. The only restriction being that students are not eligible to receive both the draw scholarship and the scholastic scholarship during the same academic year. The student may submit an application for both or just one as long as the requirements listed below are met.</p>

						<h2>
							Scholarship Requirements</h2>

						<h4>
							&quot;Draw&quot;/External Scholarship</h4>

						<ol>
							<li>
								The student&#39;s parent or legal guardian must be&nbsp;an active&nbsp;member of the UNT Parent Association.</li>
							<li>
								The student must be enrolled the following fall and spring semester at UNT.</li>
							<li>
								The student must have completed at least one full semester at UNT.</li>
							<li>
								Deadline - April 1, 2016</li>
						</ol>

						<h3>
							Scholarship Submission Information</h3>

						<h4>
							Scholastic Scholarship</h4>

						<p>
							The Scholastic Scholarship application should be submitted via the Student Financial Aid and Scholarships office. The recipients of this scholarship are selected by the university scholarship committee and notification of this scholarship usually occurs near the end of April and the first of May.</p>

						<h4>
							&nbsp;</h4>

						<h4>
							External/ Draw Scholarship</h4>

						<p>
							The Draw Scholarship application should be submitted to&nbsp;Parent Programs, in the Office of Orientation and Transition Programs,&nbsp;located in the University Union, Suite 377. Draw Scholarship winners will be drawn by the Parent Association Executive Board and/or General Board and notified at approximately the same time as the Scholastic Scholarship recipients. The deadline for both the scholarships are noted on the application forms and above.</p>
