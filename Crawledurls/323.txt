          <h2>Pursuing excellence, just like you</h2>
          <p>The Honors College at the University of North Texas creates and supports learning opportunities for talented students who seek to excel academically and thrive personally.</p> 

<p>Access to research opportunities while pursuing your bachelor’s degree, personalized attention in smaller Honors classes, two residence halls exclusively for Honors College students and a host of unique Honors-only privileges make UNT’s Honors College the right choice for talented, driven students who want more out of their university experience.</p>

<div class="faded-line"><img src="images/faded-line.png" alt=""></div>

<h3>UNT’s Honors College offers:</h3>

<div class="photo">
<img src="images/sage-hall-14_221.png" alt="Sage Hall"/>
<div class="caption"><p>Sage Hall is the home of the Honors College and serves as a hub for academic services for students.</p></div>
</div>

<ul>
<li>Stimulating discussion and personal attention in specially designed Honors-only classes, which average just 25 students each</li>
<li>Priority registration for classes, allowing you to register before most other students</li>
<li>Eligibility to live in Rawlins Hall, our brand-new campus residence exclusively for Honors College students, featuring suites with loftable beds and porches for study and recreation</li>
<li>Training in research methods and skills, and opportunities to present research at conferences</li>
<li>Tailored advising sessions with the Honors College academic counselor</li>
<li>Special library privileges</li>
<li>Tickets to cultural events, including concerts and plays</li>


</ul>
