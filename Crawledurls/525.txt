    <p title="UNT Curriculum Committee">The VPAA is responsible for the development and implementation of UNT policies and procedures relating to teaching, research and service. The VPAA acts with the advice of several councils and committees to carry out this responsibility.</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="councils-and-committees/curriculum-committee" title="UNT Curriculum Committee">Curriculum Committee</a>: Agenda and minutes, meeting dates and responsibilities of this committee.</li>

<li><a href="http://vpaa.unt.edu/councils-and-committees/list-chairs" title="List of Chairs by College">Chairs</a>: List of Chairs by College</li>
<li><a href="http://facultysenate.unt.edu/" title="UNT Faculty Senate">Faculty Senate</a>: The source for Faculty Senate information, including meeting agendas, files of past Senate meetings, and current listings of your Faculty Senators.</li>
<li><a href="http://vpaa.unt.edu/sites/default/files/UNT%20IT%20Governance%20-%20ITC%2020120720%20tjc_0.pdf" title="ITG">Information Technology Governance</a></li>
<li><a href="http://osmp.unt.edu/committee" target="_blank">Space Planning and Management Committee (SPMC)</a>: The SPMC responsibilities will include broadening the scope, review, and discussion of space issues, moves, renovations, new construction, master planning and land acquisitions to ensure alignment with the Strategic Plan.</li>
