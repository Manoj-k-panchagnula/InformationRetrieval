    <h3>Register at myUNT</h3>
<p>All students can register for classes online using<strong> <a href="http://my.unt.edu" title="MyUNT">MyUNT</a></strong>.</p>
<p>For step-by-step instructions on how to use MyUNT to register, see the <a href="http://essc.unt.edu/eis/students.htm">My Help for Students</a> website.</p>
<h3>Additional Resources</h3>
<p>For more information, contact the Registrar's Information Counter by calling 940-565-2111 or visiting Eagle Student Services Center (ESSC) Room 147.</p>
<p>For instructions on using the EIS Student Center for Registration, please go to the My Help for Students page at&nbsp;<a href="http://essc.unt.edu/eis/students.htm">essc.unt.edu/eis/students.htm</a>.</p>
