<h1 class="pagetitle">UNT Shuttle  Changes for Spring 2016:<br />
</h1>
<p>The following routes have been modified for Spring 2016: Campus Cruiser, Centre Place East, Sam Bass, Mean Green and North Texan. Please review the routes carefully.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h1 class="pagetitle">Routes and Schedules - 2015 - 2016</h1>
<hr class="clear-contentunit" />


          <h2>Click on the route name to view the map and schedule.        </h2>
        
        <div class="column1-unit">        
          <h2>2015-2016 Route: <a href="routes/meangreen.html">Mean Green</a>          </h2>
          <p><strong>Notice: Route has been updated for Spring 2016. Please review the route carefully.</strong></p>
<p>The Mean Green route serves as our signature circulator route. It provides easy and quick access to most points around campus, including the  Fouts Field, residence halls, Rec Center, and academic buildings.</p>
        <hr class="clear-contentunit" />
        </div>
        <!-- Content unit - One column -->
        <div class="column1-unit">
        <h2>2015-2016 Route: <a href="routes/northtexan.html">North Texan</a></h2>
        <p><strong>Notice: Route has been updated for Spring 2016. Please review the route carefully.</strong></p>
<p>The North Texan provides service to the University Courtyard Apartments, Uptown Apartments, the Rec Center, Environmental Science  and Technology, and the General Academic  Building.</p>
        </div>
       <hr class="clear-contentunit" />
         
         <!-- Content unit - One column -->
        <div class="column1-unit">
          <h2>2015-2016 Route: <a href="routes/centreplace.html">Centre Place</a></h2>
          <p><strong>Notice: Centre Place East Route has been updated for Spring 2016. Please review the route carefully.</strong></p>
        <p>The Centre Place route serves off-campus apartment complexes along  Bernard Street as well as the Campus Park and Forum complex. </p>
        </div>
       <hr class="clear-contentunit" />
         <!-- Content unit - One column -->
        <div class="column1-unit">
        <h2>2015-2016 Route: <a href="routes/eaglepoint.html">Eagle Point</a>        </h2>
        <p>This route serves as a connecter route between Victory Hall and the  main campus. </p>
        </div>
       <hr class="clear-contentunit" />
         <!-- Content unit - One column -->
        <div class="column1-unit">
        <h2>2015-2016 Route: <a href="routes/discoverypark.html">UNT Discovery Park</a>        </h2>
        <p align="left"><strong>Note: </strong>New service changes - please see map for details. Campus Cruiser route does not stop at Discovery Park except on Sundays. See campus cruiser map for details.</p>
        <p align="left">This route services the UNT Discovery Park on HWY 77 / N. Elm St. It picks up from the UNT campus at the GAB bus stop. </p>
        </div>
       <hr class="clear-contentunit" />
         <!-- Content unit - One column -->
        <div class="column1-unit">
        <h2>2015-2016 Route: <a href="routes/bernardstreet.html">Bernard Street</a><br />
        </h2>
        <p>The Bernard Street route serves off-campus apartment complexes. Similar  to the Centre Place route, it serves apartments located along Bernard  Street and includes The Exchange apartments. </p>
        </div>
       <hr class="clear-contentunit" />
         <!-- Content unit - One column -->
        <div class="column1-unit">
        <h2>2015-2016 Route: <a href="routes/coloradoexpress.html">Colorado Express</a><br />
        </h2>

<p><strong> The Colorado Express now stops at Medpark (A-Train)</strong></p>
<p>The Colorado Express route serves off-campus apartment complexes in the  Golden Triangle Mall area. It provides easy and quick access to and  from The Ridge at North Texas, Pace's Crossing, Golden Triangle Mall, Medpark and campus.</p>
        </div>
       <hr class="clear-contentunit" />
         <!-- Content unit - One column -->
        <div class="column1-unit">
        <h2>2015-2016 Route: <a href="routes/campuscruiser.html">Campus Cruiser</a></h2>
        <p><strong>Notice: Route has been updated for Spring 2016. Please review the route carefully.</strong></p>
        <p><strong>Note: The Campus Cruiser no longer stops at Discovery Park Monday through Saturday. There is now  Sunday Service to Discovery Park. See map for details.</strong></p>
        <p>The Campus Cruiser links the Main Campus to the Eagle Point  campus on Monday through Thursday and Saturday and Sunday. Bus will depart Victory  Hall and stop on the Main Campus. The bus will stop on the Main Campus before  returning to Victory Hall.</p>
        </div>
       <hr class="clear-contentunit" />
         <!-- Content unit - One column -->
        <div class="column1-unit">
        <h2>2015-2016 Route: <a href="routes/sambass.html">Sam Bass</a></h2>
        <p><strong>Notice: Route has been updated for Spring 2016. Please review the route carefully.</strong>This route will no longer stop at Wood Hill Square.</p>
        <p>The Sam Bass Route serves student residences on the South Side of I H 35.</p>
