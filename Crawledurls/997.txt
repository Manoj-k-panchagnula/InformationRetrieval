	<h2>FERPA and Privacy of Student Records Form</h2>

<p>The University of North Texas (&quot;UNT or &quot;university&quot;) affords all students the rights and protections relating to their education records as provided in the <em>Family Education Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA)</em>. Consistent with FERPA, students will be granted access to their education record and except in limited circumstances education records will not be disclosed without a student&#39;s consent.</p>

<ul>
	<li><a href="/sites/default/files/ferpa_consent.pdf">FERPA Authorization form </a>(PDF)</li>
	<li><a href="http://policy.unt.edu/policydesc/ferpa-policy-18-1-9">FERPA Policy of the University of North Texas</a></li>
</ul>

<h2>Free Speech and Public Assembly</h2>

<p>The University of North Texas recognizes that freedom of expression and public assembly are fundamental rights of all persons and are essential components of the educational process.</p>

<ul>
	<li><a href="http://policy.unt.edu/policydesc/free-speech-policy-0" target="_blank" title="Free Speech and Public Assembly Policy">Free Speech and Public Assembly Policy 2013</a>&nbsp;(Effective August 1, 2013)&nbsp;</li>
	<li><a href="/sites/default/files/new_sound_map.pdf">Designated Areas and Amplified Sound Form</a> (PDF)</li>
	<li><a href="/sites/default/files/free_speech_form.pdf">Request to Reserve designated areas </a>(PDF)</li>
	<li><a href="/sites/default/files/free_speech_form_parades.pdf">Request to Reserve designated areas (Parades)</a> (PDF)&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
</ul>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<h2>Prohibition of Camping on University Property</h2>

<p>The University of North Texas has an interest in controlling and maintaining university property to safeguard the health and safety of individuals and to ensure that university property is utilized for its designed purposes in support of the university&rsquo;s educational and business missions.</p>

<ul>
	<li><a href="http://policy.unt.edu/policydesc/prohibition-camping-university-property-18-1-24" target="_blank" title="Prohibition of Camping on University Property policy">Prohibition of Camping on University Property policy</a></li>
</ul>

<h2>Smoke-Free Campus</h2>

<p>In the interest of promoting the health, well-being and safety of students, faculty, staff and campus visitors, the University of North Texas prohibits smoking anywhere on university property and limits the use of smokeless tobacco products and electronic cigarettes on the UNT campus.</p>

<ul>
	<li><a href="http://www.unt.edu/features/smoke-free.htm" target="_blank" title="Smoke-Free Policy">Smoke-Free Policy Overview</a></li>
	<li><a href="http://policy.unt.edu/sites/default/files/untpolicy/10.17_Smoke%20Free%20Campus%20Policy.pdf" target="_blank" title="Smoke-Free Policy">Smoke-Free Policy</a></li>
</ul>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<h2>Student Complaint Policy</h2>

<p>The University of North Texas (&ldquo;UNT&rdquo;) provides students with a procedure for resolving complaints against UNT faculty, staff, and the university in matters where no other formal process for resolution of complaints has been established.</p>

<ul>
	<li><a href="http://policy.unt.edu/policydesc/student-complaint-policy-18-1-23" target="_blank" title="Student Complaint Policy">Student Complaint Policy</a></li>
</ul>
