<div id="title"><h1>Department of Marketing &amp; Logistics</h1></div>
<div id="main">
  <p>The Department of Marketing &amp; Logistics at the University of North Texas is dedicated to providing its students with well-rounded, state-of-the-art education in the modern principles of marketing, logistics and allied disciplines.</p>
  <p>We prepare our students for rewarding careers in marketing and logistics, as practitioners and academics, with the help of an innovative curriculum and dynamic programs.</p>
  <p>Our faculty is characterized by excellence in teaching, outstanding basic and applied research, and leadership in professional organizations.</p>
  <p> Faculty members include Honor Professors, recipients of the President's Council University Teaching Award and the J.H. Shelton Excellence in Teaching Award, and instructors who have been designated by Mortar Board, a senior honor society, as &quot;Top Profs.&quot;</p>
  <p>Students generate valuable industry contacts by participating in our student chapters of:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong><a href="http://www.ama.unt.edu/">AMA - American Marketing Association</a></strong></li>
    <li><strong><a href="http://unt.orgsync.com/org/ascend">ASCEND Pan Asian Leadership Student Chapter </a></strong></li>
    <li><a href="http://cob.unt.edu/mktg/logsa/index.html"><strong>LogSA - Logistics Student Association</strong></a></li>
    <li><strong><a href="https://orgsync.com/44114/chapter">Mu Kappa Tau</a></strong></li>
  </ul>
