        <div id="node-47" class="node">
	<p><strong><img width="892" height="245" style="vertical-align: text-top; margin: 5px;" src="/sites/default/files/outdoor%20banner.jpg" /></strong></p>
<p style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: disc; list-style-position: outside;"><strong>Not ready for a trip, but want to learn more about the outdoors? Free clinics are the perfect place to begin!</strong></p>
<p style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: disc; list-style-position: outside;">These specialty clinics and skill workshops are offered to provide on-site instruction in the fundamental skills necessary for safety and fun in the outdoors. Each clinic usually lasts one hour and helps to prepare, educate and inform participants on a wide variety of subjects. All clinics are <strong>FREE</strong> but must have registered participants to be conducted. Register at the Outdoor Pursuits Center by <strong>4pm&nbsp;Monday&nbsp;the week of&nbsp;the clinic date</strong>. Clinics are open to UNT students, faculty and staff, Rec. Center members and their guests.</p>
<h2 style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: disc; list-style-position: outside;"><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>SUMMER&nbsp;2016 CLINIC SCHEDULE</strong></span></h2>
<h2 style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: disc; list-style-position: outside;">KAYAK BASICS</h2>
<p style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: disc; list-style-position: outside;">This introduction clinic covers basic strokes, t-rescues, wet exits and more. Perfect for both beginners and experienced kayakers!</p>
<p style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: disc; list-style-position: outside;"><strong>Monday, June 13&nbsp; |&nbsp; 4:00pm&nbsp; |&nbsp; FREE</strong></p>
<p style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: disc; list-style-position: outside;">_________________________</p>
<h2 style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: disc; list-style-position: outside;">ALL ABOUT CAMPFIRES</h2>
<p style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: disc; list-style-position: outside;">Campfires are one of the best parts of camping! This fun clinic teaches the basics of fire starting including structures, techniques, and safety.</p>
<p style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: disc; list-style-position: outside;"><strong>Thursday, June 23&nbsp; |&nbsp; 7:00pm&nbsp; |&nbsp; FREE</strong></p>
<p style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: disc; list-style-position: outside;">_________________________</p>
<h2 style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: disc; list-style-position: outside;">SLACKLINING</h2>
<p style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: disc; list-style-position: outside;">Learn everything you need to know about slacklining, from setting it up to walking the line and practice your skills with guided instruction.</p>
<p style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: disc; list-style-position: outside;"><strong>Wednesday, July 13&nbsp; |&nbsp; 7:00pm&nbsp; |&nbsp; FREE</strong></p>
<p style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: disc; list-style-position: outside;">_________________________</p>
<h2 style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: disc; list-style-position: outside;">BACKCOUNTRY BASICS</h2>
<p style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: disc; list-style-position: outside;">Camping can be an expensive, complicated, and overwhelming ordeal! In this clinic you will learn how to simplify your outdoor experience and plan your trip with a limited budget. We will also survey some exciting food options you can consider when planning a meal for your next trip, be it a day hike or extended trek!</p>
<p style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: disc; list-style-position: outside;"><strong></strong><strong>Thursday, July 28&nbsp; |&nbsp; 4:00pm&nbsp; |&nbsp; FREE</strong></p>
<p style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: disc; list-style-position: outside;">_________________________</p>
<h2 style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: disc; list-style-position: outside;">CLIMBING AND BOULDERING TECHNIQUES</h2>
<p style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: disc; list-style-position: outside;">Interested in climbing but don't know where to start? Come climb with an instructor that can show you basic climbing techniques and tips while enjoying the wall all to yourself.</p>
<p style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: disc; list-style-position: outside;"><strong>Tuesday, August 9&nbsp;&nbsp;|&nbsp; 7:00pm&nbsp; |&nbsp; FREE</strong></p>
