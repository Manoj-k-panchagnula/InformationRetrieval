      <h2 class="pane-title">Why Study Materials Science and Engineering</h2>
  
  
  <div class="pane-content">
    <p>As a student in the Department of Materials Science and Engineering at the University of North Texas, you will explore the technological challenges of creating, applying and characterizing new materials for the 21st century. Your top-notch education will enable you to develop specialized skills through classroom instruction from accomplished faculty, research with modern equipment and facilities, and connections with industry leaders.</p>  </div>
