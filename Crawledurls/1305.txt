<h3>Tickets</h3>
<p>The Mean Green Sports Pass allows fans to attend every North Texas home regular-season soccer, volleyball and softball game for just $50 for adults and $30 for children 17 and under. To purchase your Mean Green SportsPass, call the North Texas Athletic Ticket Office at 1-800-UNT-2366.</p>
<p>Individual-game tickets are available on game days at Mean Green Soccer Stadium for $5 for adults and $3 for children 17 and under. All seating is general admission.</p>
<p>North Texas students are admitted free with their valid student ID.</p>
<p>Parties of ten or more are eligible for group discounts. Contact the North Texas Athletic Ticket Office at 1-800-UNT-2366 for details on how to order.</p>

<h3>Mean Green Soccer Stadium</h3>
<p>The North Texas soccer team has enjoyed a significant home-field advantage at the Mean Green Soccer Stadium, which opened in 2006 and saw North Texas win 42 of the first 50 games played at the stadium. The Soccer Stadium is located at 1500 South Bonnie Brae Street.</p>
<p>Like the Volleyball Center and Lovelace Stadium, the Mean Green Soccer Stadium was part of the second phase of development at the Mean Green Village. The stadium’s grass field measures 115 yards long and 72 yards wide, in keeping with the International Football Association’s recommended field dimensions. The stadium has permanent seating for over 1,000 fans and a press box with data, phone, and wireless internet capabilities. <a class="mainlink" href="http://www.meangreenmap.com/soccer.html" target="_blank">Click here for more information on the Mean Green Soccer Stadium</a>.</p>

<h3>Mean Green Club</h3>
<p>The Mean Green Club raises funds for North Texas athletic scholarships. Donations to the Mean Green Club help support nearly 350 North Texas student-athletes each year and provide them with the best collegiate experience possible.</p>
<p>Members receive exclusive access to North Texas athletics and year-long benefits such as invitations to member-only events, the best parking and seating available, and more.
<p>For more information or to join Mean Green Club, call us at (940) 369-7284, email us at meangreenclub@unt.edu, or <a class="mainlink" href="http://www.meangreensports.com/sports/mg-club/spec-rel/ways-to-give.html" target="_blank">click here to visit us on the web</a>.</p>
