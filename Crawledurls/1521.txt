		<h1>
			Leave your Legacy</h1>

		<p>
			Show pride in your UNT years. Commemorate friends. Remember loved ones. Congratulate new graduates.</p>

		<p>
			University of North Texas alumni and friends now have the opportunity to add their names and graduation years to the Highland Street entrance of the new UNT University Union.&nbsp; The walkway and entrance will feature&nbsp;engraved&nbsp;stone pavers, available for $100 (4-inch by 8-inch paver) and $150 (8-inch by 8-inch paver).</p>

		<p>
			Become a part of UNT&rsquo;s lasting legacy; become etched in Mean Green tradition.&nbsp;</p>

		<h1>
			About the Pavers</h1>

		<h4>
			What will my gift support?</h4>

		<p>
			Proceeds will benefit&nbsp;the Union Excellence Fund.</p>

		<h4>
			Is my gift tax deductible and how will I receive a receipt?</h4>

		<p>
			Yes, your gift is <strong>100% tax-deductible</strong>. You will receive a gift receipt at the address submitted with your order form.</p>

		<h4>
			Who owns the brick?</h4>

		<p>
			The University of North Texas is proud to offer the opportunity to install your personalized paver at the University Union. These pavers are meant to showcase the honor, support and memories of alumni and friends of UNT. Because of this, your purchased paver will become the property of the University of North Texas and cannot be removed by the purchaser for any reason.</p>

		<h4>
			Can I request a specific location?</h4>

		<p>
			Due to the nature of the installation process, we cannot accept specific requests of this type.</p>

		<h4>
			When will my paver be installed?</h4>

		<p>
			Paver orders received by January 1, 2016&nbsp;will be&nbsp;installed for the Union Grand Opening&nbsp;in April&nbsp;2016. Orders placed after January 1, 2016 will be installed in the fall of 2016. The final deadline for all paver orders is <strong>June 30, 2016</strong>.</p>

		<h1>
			Donate a Paver</h1>

		<p>
			Due to the limited number of available pavers and to ensure your order is received accurately, we encourage online ordering below. To ensure each paver is properly engraved, each paver must be ordered separately.</p>

		<p>
			If you have questions or concerns regarding online credit card ordering or have special order needs, please call Blair Condon 940-369-7474.</p>

			Paver Options</h4>

			<p>
				All text will be capitalized and all lines will be centered both vertically and horizontally. If you wish, you are able to include Greek letters and ampersands. Images and other symbols are not allowed.</p>

University Advancement and the Division of Student Affairs will review all submissions and possible errors will be brought to your attention.</p>
