	<h2><img width="600" height="245" style="margin: 5px;" src="/sites/default/files/sport%20clubs%20banner.jpg" /></h2>
<h2 style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>HOW TO START A SPORT CLUB</strong></span></h2>
<ol style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
<li style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">Contact the Recreational Sports Office for assistance.</li>
<li style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">Complete the Student Activity Organization Registration form on-line through Orgsync: <a href="https://orgsync.com/login/university-of-north-texas">https://orgsync.com/login/university-of-north-texas</a>. Contact Student Activities in the Union Room 345 or by phone: 940-565.4446 for assistance.</li>
<li style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">Once a student organization, publicize the club on campus. Hold an organizational meeting for interested students to join the club. Designate officers, a student leader, and faculty sponsor to represent the club.</li>
<li style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">Registered clubs, with a sports orientation, may then apply into the Sport Club Program.</li>
<li style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">An application, risk management questionaire and a projected budget is required and must be submitted. Each club is required to provide a schedule of proposed activities for the year and a&nbsp;group roster. Each club must abide by the rules, regulations and guidelines of the Recreational Sports Office and University of North Texas.</li>
<li style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">The student leader must appear and present&nbsp;before the Sport Clubs Council who will then vote on a recommendation to the Sport Club Staff on the approval of the club.</li>
<li style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">Sport Clubs authorization and a club budget will be approved or disapproved by the Director of Recreational Sports, Assistant Director and the Sport Club Council.</li>
</ol>
<h2 style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">&nbsp;<span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>MANAGEMENT</strong></span></h2>
<p style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">Student leadership is emphasized, and the club members plan their own schedule of activities. A club's growth is determined by active student involvement and participation. The Student Leader is the communicator with the Recreational Sports Office, and all information concerning sport club policies and procedures are directed to this person.</p>
<h2 style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>SPORT CLUBS COUNCIL</strong></span></h2>
<p style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">Each sport club shall have a Sport Club Council Representative as a member of the Sport Club Council.&nbsp; Each representative must be a currently enrolled student member of a recognized sport club.&nbsp; The Sport Club Council shall advise the Sport Club Staff in administering the Sport Club Program.&nbsp; Its three main functions are as follows:&nbsp;</p>
<p style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">1.&nbsp; &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; To promote participation in the Sport Club Program</p>
<p style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">2.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; To provide a medium for the exchange of information regarding Sport Club policies and procedures contained in the Sport Club Manual.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">3.&nbsp; &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; To listen to all new Sport Club Recognition Requests and vote on a recommendation to the Sport Club Staff on the approval of a new club.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<h2 style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>EXECUTIVE BOARD</strong></span></h2>
<p style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">The Sport Club Council will have an Executive Board, which shall consist of five student members. Each year four Sport Club Council Representatives will be elected from among their peers at the last Sport Club Council Meeting in the spring semester. All board members shall serve a term, which runs from August through July. Executive Board members can also represent their club at each Sport Club Council meeting as the Sport Club Representative.</p>
<h2 style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;">SPORT CLUBS OBJECTIVES</span></strong></h2>
<ul style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
<li style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">To offer students an opportunity to develop their skills and knowledge in a particular sport.</li>
<li style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">To provide students with leadership opportunities in managing a club and scheduling activities.</li>
<li style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">To provide partial funding for travel, competition, and equipment purchases.</li>
<li style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">To provide assistance in scheduling areas and facilities for club meetings, practices, and games.</li>
<li style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">To provide administrative support for the clubs.</li>
</ul>
<h2 style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>SPORT CLUBS POLICIES</strong></span></h2>
<ul style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
<li style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
<p style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">The deadline for new clubs to apply for funding from the Recreational Sports Office is&nbsp;January for the spring semester.</p>
</li>
<li style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
<p style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">There is a maximum $500 allocation for the first semester for all new Sport Clubs.</p>
</li>
<li style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
<p style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">Clubs should drive no more than five hundred (500) miles in a twenty-four (24) hour period. Clubs may not drive between the hours of 10pm and 6am.</p>
</li>
<li style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
<p style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">Clubs are limited to certain allotments for their travel and competitions. Club members finance a large portion of their participation expenses from personal savings.</p>
</li>
<li style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
<p style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">Members of any club, within the Sport Clubs Program, must be registered for at least one hour of course work with the University of North Texas.</p>
</li>
<li style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
<p style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">Fund raising projects must be approved at least two weeks prior to the start of the project, by the Assistant Director of Sport Clubs.</p>
</li>
<li style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
<p style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">Any policy, regarding the internal operation of a club, may be implemented providing that the majority of club members and the Recreational Sport Office agree on the policy.</p>
</li>
<li style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
<p style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">Clubs will not be funded for tournaments which award scholarships to the individual, unless the award can be used to benefit the entire club.</p>
</li>
</ul>
