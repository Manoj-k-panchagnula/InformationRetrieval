<div id="greentext">
<h2>Route to Green Parking Lot</h2>
<p class="route">Access is from the 35E frontage road or Bonnie Brae.</p>
<p class="route">From northbound 35W, take Exit 85B to West Oak. Right on West Oak, right on Bonnie Brae, then cross under 35E (see below).</p>
<p class="route">From southbound 35, take Exit 468 (not shown on this map) to West Oak. Left on West Oak, right on Bonnie Brae, then cross under 35E (see below).</p>
<p class="route">From northbound 35E, take Exit 467 to Bonnie Brae and stay right when the exit ramp splits. Left on Bonnie Brae, then cross under 35E (see below).</p>
<p class="route">From downtown Denton, go west on West Oak, left on Bonnie Brae, then cross under 35E (see below).</p>
<p class="route"><b>AFTER CROSSING UNDER 35E</b>, two routes are available: 1. left onto the 35E frontage road, right at the parking-lot entrance, and proceed through the Black Lot to the Green lot; or 2. continue south on Bonnie Brae, turn left at the parking-lot entrance, and left again into the Green Lot.</p>
</div>
<div id="blacktext">
<h2>Route to Black Parking Lot</h2>
<p class="route">Access is from the 35E frontage road.
<p class="route">From northbound 35W, take Exit 85B to West Oak. Right on West Oak then right on Bonnie Brae. After crossing under 35E, left onto the 35E frontage road then right into the Black Lot.</p>
<p class="route">From southbound 35, take Exit 468 (not shown on this map) to West Oak. Left on West Oak, right on Bonnie Brae, left onto the 35E frontage road then right into the Black Lot.</p>
<p class="route">From northbound 35E, take Exit 467 to Bonnie Brae and stay right when the exit ramp splits. Left on Bonnie Brae, left onto the 35E frontage road then right into the Black Lot.</p>
<p class="route">From downtown Denton, go west on West Oak then left on Bonnie Brae. Left onto the 35E frontage road and then right into the Black Lot.</p>
</div>
<div id="whitetext">
<h2>Route to White Parking Lot</h2>
<p class="route">Access is from Bonnie Brae.
<p class="route">From northbound 35W, take Exit 85B to West Oak. Right on West Oak, then right on Bonnie Brae. Cross under 35E, turn left at the split to Airport Road to continue south on Bonnie Brae, then turn left into the White Lot.</p>
<p class="route">From southbound 35, take Exit 468 (not shown on this map) to West Oak. Left on West Oak, then right on Bonnie Brae. Cross under 35E, turn left at the split to Airport Road to continue south on Bonnie Brae, then turn left into the White Lot.</p>
<p class="route">From northbound 35E, take Exit 467 to Bonnie Brae and stay right when the exit ramp splits. Turn left on Bonnie Brae. Cross under 35E, turn left at the split to Airport Road to continue south on Bonnie Brae, then turn left into the White Lot.</p>
<p class="route">From downtown Denton, go west on West Oak then left on Bonnie Brae.  Cross under 35E, turn left at the split to Airport Road to continue south on Bonnie Brae, then turn left into the White Lot.</p>
</div>
<div id="redtext">
<h2>Route to Red Parking Lot</h2>
<p class="route">Access is from North Texas Blvd. The lots next to Victory Hall are for red and blue parking passes.</p>
<p class="route">From northbound 35W, take Exit 85A to southbound 35E. From southbound 35E, take Exit 466B to North Texas Blvd. Right on North Texas Blvd., then an immediate right into the Red Lot.</p>
<p class="route">From southbound 35, take Exit 466B to North Texas Blvd. Right on North Texas Blvd., then an immediate right into the Red Lot.</p>
<p class="route">From northbound 35E, take Exit 467 to Bonnie Brae and stay right when the exit ramp splits. Left on Bonnie Brae, then left the 35E frontage road to North Texas Blvd. Right on North Texas Blvd., then an immediate right into the Red Lot.</p>
<p class="route">From downtown Denton, take West Oak to Bonnie Brae. South on Bonnie Brae under 35E, then left on the 35E frontage road. Right on North Texas Blvd., then an immediate right into the Red Lot.</p>
</div>
<div id="bluetext">
<h2>Route to Blue Parking Lot</h2>
<p class="route">Access is from Bonnie Brae via Walt Parker Drive. The lots next to Victory Hall are for blue and red parking passes.</p>
<p class="route">From northbound 35W, take Exit 85B to West Oak. Right on West Oak then right on Bonnie Brae. Left on Walt Parker to the Blue Lot.</p>
<p class="route">From southbound 35, take Exit 468 to West Oak. Left on West Oak then right on Bonnie Brae. Left on Walt Parker to the Blue Lot.</p>
<p class="route">From northbound 35E, take Exit 467 to Bonnie Brae and stay right when the exit ramp splits. Left on Bonnie Brae, then left on Walt Parker to the Blue Lot.</p>
<p class="route">From downtown Denton, go west on West Oak, left on Bonnie Brae, then left on Walt Parker to the Blue Lot.</p>
</div>
<div id="orangetext">
<h2>Route to Orange Parking Lot</h2>
<p class="route">Access is from North Texas Blvd. or the 35E Northbound frontage road.</p>
<p class="route">From northbound 35W, take Exit 85B to West Oak. Right on West Oak then continue east on West Hickory. Right on North Texas Blvd. and right into the Orange Lot.</p>
<p class="route">From southbound 35, take Exit 468 (not shown on this map) to West Oak, left on West Oak, then continue east on West Hickory. Right on North Texas Blvd. and right into the Orange Lot.</p>
<p class="route">From northbound 35E, take Exit 466B to North Texas Blvd. Stay on the 35E frontage road past North Texas Blvd. Go under the Pedestrian Bridge, then turn right into the Orange Lot.</p>
<p class="route">From downtown Denton, go west on West Oak, left on North Texas Blvd., and right into the Orange Lot.</p>
</div>
<div id="adatext">
<h2>Route to ADA Parking Lot</h2>
<p class="route">Access is from North Texas Blvd. or the 35E Northbound frontage road, and the ADA-dedicated area is in the southwest portion of the lot.</p>
<p class="route">From northbound 35W, take Exit 85B to West Oak. Right on West Oak then continue east on West Hickory. Right on North Texas Blvd. and right into the Orange Lot.</p>
<p class="route">From southbound 35, take Exit 468 (not shown on this map) to West Oak, left on West Oak, then continue east on West Hickory. Right on North Texas Blvd. and right into the Orange Lot.</p>
<p class="route">From northbound 35E, take Exit 466B to North Texas Blvd. Stay on the 35E frontage road past North Texas Blvd. Go under the Pedestrian Bridge, then turn right into the Orange Lot.</p>
<p class="route">From downtown Denton, go west on West Oak, left on North Texas Blvd., and right into the Orange Lot.</p>
<p class="route"><img class="left-float" src="adalogo.jpg" width="32" height="33" />This logo marks pick-up and drop-off points for the shuttle service between the ADA lot and Apogee Stadium.</p>
</div>
<div id="yellowtext">
<h2>Route to Yellow Parking Lot</h2>
<p class="route">Access is from Bonnie Brae. </p>
<p class="route">From northbound 35W, take Exit 85B to West Oak. Right on West Oak, right on Bonnie Brae and right into the Yellow Lot.</p>
<p class="route">From southbound 35, take Exit 468 (not shown on this map) to West Oak, left on West Oak then right on Bonnie Brae. Turn right into the Yellow Lot.</p>
<p class="route">From northbound 35E, take Exit 467 to Bonnie Brae and stay right when the exit ramp splits. Turn left on Bonnie Brae, then right into the Yellow Lot.</p>
<p class="route">From downtown Denton, go west on West Oak, left on Bonnie Brae and right into the Yellow Lot.</p>
</div>
<div id="closingstext">
<h2>Pre-Game Road and Exit Closings</h2>
<p class="route">Three to four hours prior to North Texas home football games, one exit off I-35 will be closed: Exit 84 to Bonnie Brae from northbound 35W.</p>
<p class="route">A stretch of Airport Road will also be closed, as will the North Texas Blvd. bridge over 35E and a short portion of North Texas Blvd. south of the Red Lot entrance immediately north of Underwood Street.</p>
<p class="route">The following I-35 exits involved in Apogee Stadium game-day traffic flow will remain open:
<ul><li>468 to West Oak from southbound 35</li>
<li>85A to 35E from northbound 35W</li>
<li>85B to West Oak from northbound 35W</li>
<li>466B to North Texas Blvd. from northbound 35E</li>
<li>466B to North Texas Blvd. from southbound 35E</li>
<li>467 to Bonnie Brae from northbound 35E</li></ul></p>
</div>
<div id="shuttletext">
<h2>Eagle Ambassador Shuttle Stops</h2>
<p class="route">A shuttle runs between Apogee Stadium Gate 2, Gate 3, the Victory Hall entrance and the Victory Hall bus stop on home game days. The shuttle begins two hours prior to kickoff and runs approximately every 15 minutes until the end of the first quarter, then resumes at the beginning of the fourth quarter until approximately 30 minutes after the end of the game.</p>
