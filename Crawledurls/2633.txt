  <h2 class="node__title node-title">Compensation</h2>
  
  <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"><p class="p1"><span class="s1">Compensation, managed by the Total Rewards department, includes:</span></p>
<ul><li class="p1"><span class="s1">Salary</span></li>
<li class="p1"><span class="s1">Job classifications</span></li>
<li class="p1"><span class="s1">Job grading and evaluation</span></li>
<li class="p1"><span class="s1">Equity reviews</span></li>
<li class="p1"><span class="s1">Market analysis</span></li>
<li class="p1"><span class="s1">Pay bands and plans</span></li>
</ul><p class="p1"><span class="s1">Salary and compensation programs are designed to:</span></p>
<ul><li><span style="line-height: 1.538em;">Ensure fair pay that is tied to current market levels.</span></li>
<li><span style="line-height: 1.538em;">Establish clear career paths so team members know how they may advance in their organization.</span></li>
<li><span style="line-height: 1.538em;">Enhance transparency and consistency.</span></li>
<li><span style="line-height: 1.538em;">Help recruit, retain and motivate talented team members.</span></li>
</ul><p><span style="line-height: 1.538em;">Find assistance from <a href="https://hr.untsystem.edu/we-are-here-help-you-heres-our-human-resources-team">Total Rewards staff, including Compensation, Benefits, Records and Information Services</a>.</span></p>
<div class="accButton"><button class="expand AllAcc">Expand All</button></div>
<ul class="accordian" style="clear: both;"><li>
<h2>Compensation for Staff, Pay Plan, Pay Band</h2>
<div class="info">
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">The Total Rewards staff monitors salaries in the Dallas-Denton-Fort Worth area, the region and at peer organizations to help establish pay plans. Data resources include: </span></p>
<ul><li><span style="line-height: 1.538em;">Respected consultants with expertise in compensation and human resources</span></li>
<li><span style="line-height: 1.538em;">The College and University Professional Association for Human Resources (</span><a href="http://www.cupahr.org/" style="line-height: 1.538em;"><span class="s2">CUPA-HR</span></a><span style="line-height: 1.538em;">), a national association that monitors human resources and employment trends in higher education</span></li>
<li><span style="line-height: 1.538em;">CompAnalyst, Payscale and other analytical programs</span><span style="line-height: 1.538em;"> </span></li>
</ul><p class="p1"><span class="s1">Employees may be eligible for compensatory, overtime, longevity and <a href="https://fmx.cpa.state.tx.us/fm/pubs/paypol/agency_provisions/index.php?section=hazardous&amp;page=hazardous" title="State of Texas hazadous duty pay">hazardous duty</a> pay, depending on their classification and job description.  </span></p>
<p class="p1"><strong><span class="s1">UNT Pay Plans</span></strong></p>

<h2>Compensation for Faculty</h2>
<div class="info">
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">The Office of the Provost at each campus monitors and manages faculty salaries to support recruitment and retention of academic experts for teaching and research. The provost also oversees policies and procedures for tenure and promotion. Supplemental pay is available, but must be approved by Human Resources and the appropriate executive prior to assignment.</span></p>
<ul><li><span style="line-height: 1.538em;">Office of the Provost and Vice President for Academic Affairs - </span><a href="http://vpaa.unt.edu/" style="line-height: 1.538em;"><span class="s2">UNT</span></a></li>

<li><span style="line-height: 1.538em;">Office of the Provost - </span><a href="http://www.untdallas.edu/provost" style="line-height: 1.538em;"><span class="s2">UNT Dallas</span></a></li>
<li><span class="s4" style="line-height: 1.538em;"><a href="http://lawschool.untsystem.edu/faculty">Faculty</a></span><span class="s3" style="line-height: 1.538em;"> - UNT Dallas <a href="http://lawschool.untsystem.edu/"><span class="s4">College of Law</span></a></span></li>

<li><span style="line-height: 1.538em;">Find the </span><a href="//hr.untsystem.edu/sites/default/files/FormsForAllLocations/fringe-benefi-chart-fy16.pdf"><span class="s4">FY16 Fringe Benefits Chart</span></a><span style="line-height: 1.538em;">.</span></li>
<li><span style="line-height: 1.538em;">Find <a href="//hr.untsystem.edu/sites/default/files/FormsForAllLocations/flsa-poster-english2009.pdf">FLSA guidelines in English</a> and <a href="//hr.untsystem.edu/sites/default/files/FormsForAllLocations/flsa-poster-spanish2009.pdf">FLSA guidelines in Spanish</a>. </span></li>

</div>

<li>
<h2>Benefits Administration</h2>
<div class="info">
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">Benefit costs are deducted from employee pay. <a href="http://bsc.untsystem.edu/payroll-home"><span class="s2">Payroll</span></a> is managed by the system's unified controller function, located at <a href="mailto:bsc@untsystem.edu"><span class="s2">Business Support Services</span></a>, 1112 Dallas Drive, Denton, Texas 76205,<span class="baec5a81-e4d6-4674-97f3-e9220f0136c1" style="white-space: nowrap;">1-855-868-4357 or </span><a href="mailto:bsc.untsystem.edu"><span class="s2">bsc@untsystem.edu</span></a><span class="s2">.</span></span></p>
<ul><li class="p1"><span class="s1">The UNT System’s group <a href="/employees/benefits-insurance"><span class="s2">insurance benefit plan</span></a> is administered by the Employees Retirement System of Texas (<a href="http://www.ers.state.tx.us/"><span class="s2">ERS</span></a>) and by United Healthcare <a href="http://healthselectoftexas.welcometouhc.com/">Health Select</a>. </span></li>
<li class="p1"><span class="s1">T</span><span class="s1">he retirement plan is administered by the Teacher Retirement System (<a href="http://www.trs.state.tx.us/retirees.jsp?submenu=faqs&amp;page_id=/onac/faq_mytrs"><span class="s2">TRS</span></a>). A voluntary 403(b) plan is available for savings beyond TRS automatic contributions.</span></li>
<li class="p1"><span class="s1">An Optional Retirement Plan (<a href="http://www.thecb.state.tx.us/index.cfm?objectid=08C6BE55-CC42-7676-C2D178FDFEC20EF6"><span class="s2">ORP</span></a>), a 403(b), is available within 90 days of hiring to certain llibrarian and facultyemployees. </span></li>
<li class="p1"><span class="s1">Texas$aver (<a href="http://www.fascore.com/PDF/Texas/compare_401k_457.pdf">Texas Saver</a>) 457 plan is an additional savings option.</span></li>
</ul><p class="p1"><span class="s1"><span class="s2"><em>Note that ERS administers a retirement plan for some State of Texas agencies (not institutions of higher education) that is separate from ERS insurance administration.</em></span></span></p>
