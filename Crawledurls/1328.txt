                	<h2>
	Our Mission</h2>

<div>
	<div>
		<div>
			The Substance Abuse Resource Center at UNT will provide evidence-based individual, group and environmental level education regarding alcohol and other drug use through services and strategies that encourage healthy decision making, in an effort to reduce the harmful effects of substance use.</div>
	</div>
</div>

<div>
	<h2>
		Vision</h2>

	<p>
		The Substance Abuse Resource Center will create a culture of personal responsibility, thereby encouraging responsible behaviors related to alcohol and other drug use, thus reducing and eliminating negative consequences and the barriers to personal, professional and academic success.</p>

	<h2>
		Values and Goals</h2>

	<ul>
		<li>
			Harm reduction</li>
		<li>
			Data driven intervention delivery</li>
		<li>
			Research</li>
		<li>
			Personal responsibility</li>
		<li>
			Ethical practice</li>
		<li>
			Confidentiality</li>
		<li>
			Continuing education and staff development</li>
		<li>
			Diversity</li>
		<li>
			Systemic change</li>
	</ul>

	<p>
		&nbsp;</p>

	<h2>
		Resources</h2>

	<ul>
		<li>
			<strong><a href="http://recovery.unt.edu/recovery-meetings-campus">On-campus resources for recovery</a></strong></li>
		<li>
			<strong>Meet with a friendly SARC professional to get more information or help &ndash; 940.565.3177</strong></li>
		<li>
			<a href="http://www.aadallas.org/non_cms/meetings.php?bSearch=1" target="_blank" title="Alcohol Anonymous Support Group">Find an Alcohol Anonymous support group</a></li>
		<li>
			<a href="http://www.dallasal-anon.org/meetings.php" target="_blank" title="Family members dealing with alcohol or drugs">For family members of individuals dealing with alcohol or drugs</a></li>
		<li>
			<a href="https://ncadd.org/for-the-media/alcohol-a-drug-information" target="_blank" title="Additional Information">Additional alcohol and drug information</a></li>
		<li>
			<a href="/quick-tips">Quick Tips</a></li>
	</ul>

	<div>
		<h2>
			Services</h2>

		<ul>
			<li>
				psychoeducation for students dealing with substance dependency or violations</li>
			<li>
				support for students in recovery</li>
			<li>
				assistance to alcohol-free students and students who are looking to cut back</li>
			<li>
				a walk-in resource room with books, brochures and realistic alcohol and other drug information</li>
			<li>
				scheduling presentations for residence halls, classrooms or student organizations</li>
			<li>
				host responsibility training</li>
			<li>
				tailored drug and alcohol education</li>
			<li>
				co-sponsorship opportunities, with organizations or with special events planned by this office</li>
		</ul>

		<p>
			UNT SARC will keep all conversations confidential, except in cases defined by law where further assistance is required: suicidal, homicidal or psychotic. SARC&nbsp;keeps a list of solid resources that can help students with&nbsp;many issues.</p>
	</div>
