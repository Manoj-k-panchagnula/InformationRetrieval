						<h4>OneDrive and Office Web Apps</h4>
						<p>OneDrive is available as part of EagleConnect to all current UNT students, offering 50 GB of cloud based storage.</p>
						<p>Office Web Apps are available as part of EagleConnect to all current UNT students, offering a free cloud based version of the Microsoft Office Suite.</p>
							<ul>
								<li><a href="../skydrive">Overview of OneDrive and Office Web Apps</a></li>
								<li><a href="../skydrive/skydrive_old.html">I previously had a SkyDriveDrive account through EagleConnect</a></li>
								<li><a href="../skydrive/desktop.html">Overview of the OneDrive desktop client</a></li>
							</ul><h4 id="retirees">EagleConnect for retirees</h4>	<p>Retirees may request an EagleConnect account as an alternative to keeping their UNT Exchange account upon retirement.  This option is found within the exit paperwork filed with HR or may be requested after retirement following <a href="https://untsitsm.saasit.com/alp.aspx?ProviderName=UNT&Scope=SelfService&role=Anonymous%20Access&CommandId=ShowKnowledgeArticle&Id=D1D27A6E4AB64D83B13BA6180F2355B1" target="ec_request">these instructions</a>.	<br><br>There are a few important things to note:		<ul>			<li>A new email address in the form of FirstLast@my.unt.edu will be created. Upon request, the existing UNT Exchange alias (ususally First.Last@unt.edu) can deliver mail to the new EagleConnect address.</li>			<li>No mail from the UNT Exchange account will be transferred to the EagleConnect email address.  Retirees will need to forward any existing mail to the new address if they wish to keep it.</li>			<li>OneDrive will not be included; OneDrive is a service only available to current students and is explained in the FAQ above.  The options below are available to the general public and may provide as alternatives for retirees.  UNT does not support these services should trouble arise.</li>			<ul>				<li>Any free <a href="https://onedrive.live.com/about/en-us/">Microsoft Live account</a> comes with 7GB of OneDrive cloud storage and basic Office Web Apps.  This includes Word, Excel, PowerPoint, OneNote, and the OneDrive desktop application for syncing files to your computers.</li>				<li><a href="http://office.microsoft.com/en-us/home-premium/">Microsoft Office 365 Home Premium</a> is a paid-subscription based service that comes with 20GB of OneDrive cloud storage, access to Office Web Apps, and the Office desktop applications you're used to using.</li>				<li><a href="http://office.microsoft.com/en-us/buy/">Microsoft Office 2013</a> can be purchased for use on a single computer and includes the Office desktop applications you're used to using.</li>				<li><a href="http://www.google.com/drive/about.html">Google Drive</a> with Google Docs comes with 15GB of free storage and may serve as an alternative to Microsoft OneDrive with Office Web Apps.</li>			</ul>		</ul>

<h4>Is there a mobile version of EagleConnect?</h4>
<ul>
<li>Your mobile device should automatically redirect you to the mobile site.</li>
<li>The address for the mobile version of EagleConnect is <span id="important"> <a href="http://eagleconnect.unt.edu/mobile">eagleconnect.unt.edu/mobile</a></span>.</li>
<li>iPhones display a mobile version of your EagleConnect Account.</li>
<li>Android devices and Windows Phones will most likely show a "light" version of your mailbox. You can see the standard desktop view by using your mobile browser's "request desktop view" feature. </li>
</ul>
