<div class="singleCol large-12 columns"><h1 id="ContentPageName">Alumni Travel</h1>
<p class="clearBoth">&#160;</p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><strong><span style="font-size: 12pt;">REGAL ROUTES of Northern Europe &#8211; OCEANIA CRUISES** </span></strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;"><em>London to Copenhagen (10 nights)</em><strong><br /><br /></strong></span><strong>Date:&#160;&#160;&#160; June 13-24, 2016</strong>&#160; Marina</span><br /><span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif;">Price:&#160;&#160;&#160; From $3,799 per person, double occupancy (airfare included from select cities)</span><br /><br /><span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif;">Embark on an odyssey to Northern Europe, where beautiful medieval buildings reflect upon tranquil canals, fairy-tale turrets rise above marvelous cities, and quaint cobbled lanes lead to ornate cathedrals and regal monuments. From London, travel to Southampton and begin your voyage aboard Oceania Cruises&#8217; majestic Marina, stopping first in Le Havre, a jumping-off point for adventures in magical Paris. Admire Bruges&#8217; lovely gabled buildings and arched stone bridges, and take in the stylish art galleries of Antwerp. Explore the fine museums and canals of Amsterdam, Europe&#8217;s captivating cultural hub, and behold the sprawling parks and gardens of Hamburg, Germany&#8217;s &#8220;Green City.&#8221; Continue to Kristiansund, a gateway to Norway&#8217;s welcoming villages and gorgeous fjords, followed by Scandinavia&#8217;s oldest capital, Oslo, home to the imposing Akershus Fortress and the whimsical world of sculptures at Vigeland Park. Before your voyage concludes in Copenhagen, discover the charming boutiques, cozy cafes and elegant structures that grace Gothenburg, Sweden&#8217;s cultural heart. </span><br /><span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><strong><br /><br />**Date, price and itinerary are subject to change.<br /><br /><br /><br /><span style="font-size: 12pt;">EUROPEAN EMPIRES OF ARTISTRY &#8211; OCEANIA CRUISES ** &#160;&#160;&#160; &#160;&#160;&#160; </span></strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;"><em><img alt="MonteCarlo_EuropeanEmpires_051115" height="247" src="http://www.untalumni.com/image/alumni/2015-images/Monte-Carlo-Monaco_EuropeanEmpires.jpg" width="330" /></em></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;"><em>Barcelona to Rome (7 nights)</em><strong><br /><br /></strong></span><strong>Date:&#160;&#160;&#160; October 14-22, 2016</strong>&#160;&#160; Nautica </span><br /><span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif;">Price:&#160;&#160;&#160; From $2,899 per person, double occupancy (airfare included from select cities)</span><br /><br /><span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif;">Trace the remnants of ancient empires and revel in striking Mediterranean beauty as you sail to Spain, France, Monaco and Italy aboard Oceania Cruises&#8217; graceful Nautica. Depart Barcelona for the glorious island of Minorca, where Mah&#243;n beckons with a lovely waterfront, elegant Georgian architecture and idyllic coves. Admire the picturesque markets and canals of S&#232;te, France, and take in the colorful French city of Marseille, a gateway to the quaint villages and medieval towns of Provence. Sail to Italy and visit the fishing village turned resort town, Portofino, a modern playground of villas and yachts. Then stop in glamorous Monte Carlo, a small, affluent enclave famous for its casino and its stunning locale on the Riviera&#8217;s winding Grand Corniche. Explore the magnificent Renaissance architecture of legendary Florence, stand before Pisa&#8217;s famous Leaning Tower, or relax amid the rolling hills of Tuscany, before disembarking in Civitavecchia, the port of Rome.</span><br /><br /><span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><strong><br />**Date, price and itinerary are subject to change.<br /><br /></strong></span></p>
<p class="clearBoth">&#160;</p>
