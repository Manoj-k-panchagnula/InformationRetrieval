<h2>Route to Mean Green Club Parking Lot</h2>
<p class="route">Access is from North Texas Blvd. Mean Green Club members may park in spaces on Highland Street, in the top portion of the Fouts Field lot, or in the general section (shown in blue) of the Fouts Field lot.</p>
<p class="route">From northbound 35W, take Exit 85A to 35E, then Exit 466B to North Texas Blvd. Left on North Texas Blvd.</p>
<p class="route">From southbound 35, take Exit 468 (not shown on this map) to West Oak. Left on West Oak and continue east on Hickory. Right on North Texas Blvd.</p>
<p class="route">From northbound 35E, take Exit 4666B to North Texas Blvd. Right on North Texas Blvd.</p>
<p class="route">From downtown Denton, go west on West Oak then left on North Texas Blvd/Ave E.</p>
</div>
<div id="gentext">
<h2>Route to General Parking Lot</h2>
<p class="route">Access is from North Texas Blvd. Parking is free.</p>
<p class="route">From northbound 35W, take Exit 85A to 35E, then Exit 466B to North Texas Blvd. Left on North Texas Blvd.</p>
<p class="route">From southbound 35, take Exit 468 (not shown on this map) to West Oak. Left on West Oak and continue east on Hickory. Right on North Texas Blvd.</p>
<p class="route">From northbound 35E, take Exit 4666B to North Texas Blvd. Right on North Texas Blvd.</p>
<p class="route">From downtown Denton, go west on West Oak then left on North Texas Blvd/Ave E.</p>
