<div class="singleCol large-12 columns"><h1 id="ContentPageName">Board of Directors</h1>
<p class="clearBoth">The University of North Texas Alumni Association&#8217;s Board of Directors provide counsel to the university and serve as the official liaison between the university and its 216,000+ graduates. These individuals serve as university ambassadors in their communities and work to establish and maintain life-long relationships.</p>
