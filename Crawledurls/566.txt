<h3>VISION</h3>
<p>Be the global thought leader in digital retailing research, innovation and knowledge transfer.<br />
 </p>
<h3>MISSION</h3>
<p>Advance global digital retailing knowledge through academic – industry collaborations in discovery, innovation, knowledge transfer, talent development, and excellence awards.</p>
