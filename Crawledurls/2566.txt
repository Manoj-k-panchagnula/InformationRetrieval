<th colspan="2">Important Dates for Summer 2016 3W1, 8W1 and SUM Sessions</th>
</tr>
<tr>
<td width="15%"><strong>April 15<br /></strong></td>
<td width="85%">
<p>Last day for all graduate students new to UNT and all former graduate students (those who have not been enrolled at least once during the 12 months prior to Summer and/or who have received a degree from UNT since their last enrollment) to submit graduate admission applications for 3W1, 8W1, and SUM session regular registration. Graduate applications received after this deadline must be accompanied by all official transcripts.</p>
<p>*Graduate programs have varied deadlines.&nbsp; Please refer to college/departmental information in the <a href="http://catalog.unt.edu/content.php?navoid=608&amp;catoid=10">Graduate Catalog</a> for additional admission deadlines.&nbsp; For timely application processing, submit your application to the Toulouse Graduate school by this date.</p>
<p>Graduate students who submit applications after this date, if accepted, will register during late registration and pay a $50 late registration fee.</p>
<p>No applications for graduate admission for the 3W1, 8W1, and SUM sessions will be accepted after May 6.</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td width="15%"><strong>May 11</strong></td>
<td width="85%">
<p>Last day for graduate students to request change of major in the <a href="http://tsgs.unt.edu" target="_blank" style="font-size: 12px; line-height: 1.5;">Toulouse Graduate School</a> prior to 3W1, 8W1, and SUM session registration.</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td width="15%"><strong>May 12</strong></td>
<td width="85%">
<p><span style="font-family: Verdana, Geneva, sans-serif; font-size: 12px; font-style: normal; line-height: 1.5;">Last day for all undergraduate students new to UNT to submit undergraduate admission applications for 3W1, 8W1, and SUM session regular registration. Any undergraduate applications received after this deadline must be accompanied by all official transcripts and test scores. The student must meet the stated admission requirements and will register during late registration and pay a $50 late registration fee.</span></p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><strong>May 13</strong></td>
<td>
<p>Payment deadline for registration that has occurred between 7:00 a.m., March 11 through 4:30 p.m., May 13.</p>
<p>Payment arrangements must be completed by 5:00 p.m., or your registration request will be canceled. To pay your bill, go to <a href="http://my.unt.edu/" target="_blank" style="font-size: 12px; line-height: 1.5;">my.unt.edu</a>.</p>
<p style="font-style: normal; font-size: 12px; font-family: Verdana, Geneva, sans-serif;">No applications for undergraduate admission for the 3W1, 8W1, and SUM sessions will be accepted after this date.</p>
<p style="font-style: normal; font-size: 12px; font-family: Verdana, Geneva, sans-serif;">Individual exceptions to the stated undergraduate admissions requirements cannot be considered after this date.</p>
<p style="font-style: normal; font-size: 12px; font-family: Verdana, Geneva, sans-serif;"><strong>NOTE:&nbsp;</strong>Students who hold a bachelor’s degree or higher must apply to the&nbsp;<a href="http://tsgs.unt.edu" target="_blank" style="font-size: 12px; line-height: 1.5;">Toulouse Graduate School</a>&nbsp;before they may continue enrollment.</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><strong>May 16</strong></td>
<td>
<p>Payment deadline for late registration that has occurred between 7:00 a.m., May 14 through 4:30 p.m., May 16.</p>
<p>Payment arrangements must be completed by 5:00 p.m., or your registration request will be canceled. To pay your bill, go to <a href="http://my.unt.edu/" target="_blank" style="font-size: 12px; line-height: 1.5;">my.unt.edu</a>.</p>
</td>
</tr>

<p><span style="color: #339e35; font-family: Calibri, Candara, Segoe, 'Segoe UI', Optima, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 1.61111em; font-weight: bold; letter-spacing: 0.045em; line-height: 1.65517em;"><a name="Fee_Payment"></a>Fee Payment</span></p>
<p>The fee payment deadline is 5:00 p.m., May 13 for regular registration that has occurred from 7:00 a.m., March 11 through 4:30 p.m., May 13.</p>
<p>The fee payment deadline is 5:00 p.m., May 16 for late registration that has occurred from 7:00 a.m., May 14 through 4:30 p.m., May 16.</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12px; line-height: 1.5;">Bills will not be mailed for Summer sessions. Go to EIS self-service online at </span><a href="http://my.unt.edu/" target="_blank" style="font-size: 12px; line-height: 1.5;">my.unt.edu</a><span style="font-size: 12px; line-height: 1.5;">&nbsp;for your account balance and schedule information. Account balance information will be available at&nbsp;</span><a href="http://my.unt.edu/" target="_blank" style="font-size: 12px; line-height: 1.5;">my.unt.edu</a><span style="font-size: 12px; line-height: 1.5;"> once you complete registration. Go to EIS Student Center to view your bill.</span></p>
<p>Fee payment arrangements must be completed by the deadlines stated above, or your registration requests will be canceled and you will not be registered. Students must pay their total due amount or the first payment of the installment plan if they select the installment plan option. Students receiving financial aid or scholarships must have enough anticipated aid to cover their total due amount or the first payment of the installment plan posted to their account by 5:00 p.m., May 13.</p>
<p>For information about making payments, please visit <a href="http://studentaccounting.unt.edu" target="_blank" style="font-size: 12px; line-height: 1.5;">studentaccounting.unt.edu</a>.</p>
<p>For information about Financial Aid and Scholarships, visit <a href="http://financialaid.unt.edu/" target="_blank" style="font-size: 12px; line-height: 1.5;">financialaid.unt.edu</a>.</p>
<h3><a name="AdditionalResources" id="AdditionalResources"></a>Additional Resources</h3>
<p>For more information, contact the Registrar's Information Counter by calling 940-565-2111 or visiting Eagle Student Services Center (ESSC) Room 147.</p>
<p>For instructions on using the EIS Student Center for Registration, please go to the <a href="http://essc.unt.edu/eis/students.htm" target="_blank" style="font-size: 12px; line-height: 1.5;">EIS My Help for Students page</a>.</p>
