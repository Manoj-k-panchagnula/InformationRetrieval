	<h2><strong>16th Annual SECC Golf Tournament <img width="150" height="150" style="float: left;" src="/sites/default/files/images/facilities/secc-logo.jpg" /></strong></h2>
<h2>October 12, 2015&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <br />Oakmont Country Club&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <br />Corinth, TX<strong><br /></strong></h2>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>____________________________________</p>
<h2><strong>GENERAL INFORMATION</strong></h2>
<p><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>Date:</strong> </span><br />October 12, 2015<br /><strong></strong></p>
<p><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>Time:</strong> </span><br />1:00pm shotgun start<br /><strong></strong></p>
<p><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>Location:</strong> </span><br />Oakmont Country Club, Corinth TX<br /><strong></strong></p>
<p><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>Format:</strong> </span><br />4-person scramble<br /><strong></strong></p>
<p><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>Anticipated Schedule of Events:</strong></span><br />Registration: 11:30am - 12:45pm<br />Putting and Pitching Contests: 11:30am - 12:45pm<br />Shotgun Start: 1:00pm</p>
<p>Trophies will be given for 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place team finishes. Prizes for contest winners and door prizes will be given out at the awards ceremony.</p>
<p><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>Downloadable Forms:</strong></span><br /><a href="/sites/default/files/images/facilities/SECC%20Golf%20Flyer_2015.pdf" target="_blank">SECC Flyer and Registration Form</a><br /><a href="/sites/default/files/images/facilities/SECC%20golf%20titleSponsor%20letter_2015_editable.pdf" target="_blank">SECC Sponsor Letter and Form</a><br /><a href="/sites/default/files/images/facilities/Door%20Prize%20Letter%202015.pdf" target="_blank">SECC Door Prize Letter and Form</a></p>
<h2><strong>Get on the links, have fun, and give to a great cause!</strong></h2>
<p>This tournament helps our community through the State Employee Charitable Campaign (SECC), and will benefit the United Way of Denton County and many of its partner agencies such as the American Red Cross-Denton County, Big Brothers Big Sisters, CASA, and Denton County Friends of the Family.</p>
<p>______________________________</p>

Funds raised through these sponsorships benefit agencies that improve the quality of life for many community members. Additionally, this sponsorship provides your organization an opportunity to be publicly acknowledged for a worthwhile charitable contribution and helping continue a great tradition.
