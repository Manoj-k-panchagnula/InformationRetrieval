    <p>You can find classes using the MyUNT online class search or by browsing the schedule of classes by department using the PDF documents provided on this page.&nbsp;</p>
<h3>Option 1: Use MyUNT to Find Classes</h3>
<p>An online class search is available at <a href="http://my.unt.edu/">myUNT</a>. Sign in, or select Guest, then click on 'Class Search' link.</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/how-to-view-schedule-of-classes-online-with-myunt" title="How to View the Schedule of Classes Online with MyUNT">How to View the Schedule of Classes Online with MyUNT</a></li>

<li><a href="/how-to-view-web-based-classes-online-with-myunt" title="How to View Web-Based Classes Online with MyUNT">How to View Web-Based Classes Online with MyUNT</a></li>

<li><a href="/how-to-view-schedule-of-evening-and-weekend-classes-online-with-myunt" title="How to View the Schedule of Evening and Weekend Classes Online with MyUNT">How to View the Schedule of Evening and Weekend Classes Online with MyUNT</a></li>
<li><a href="/list-of-major-codes-and-advising-locations" title="List of Major Codes and Advising Locations">Subject codes, majors and advising locations</a></li>
</ul>
<p>Listings for Distance Education and Web-Based Courses are also available at <a href="http://www.untecampus.com/">untecampus.com</a>. Click on the Search Courses of Browse Courses links, then select the semester.</p>
<h3>Option 2: Use Visual Schedule Builder</h3>
<p><a href="http://vsb.unt.edu">Visual Schedule Builder</a><span style="font-size: 12px; line-height: 1.5;"> is a tool designed to assist students in planning their academic schedule. It allows you to graphically view schedule options based on the courses and offering times that works best for you. For additional information please read the <a href="/sites/default/files/UNT_Visual_Schedule_Builder_help.pdf">help documentation</a>. </span></p>
<h3>Option 3: Browse Course Listings</h3>
<p>To search for courses, select one of the links below to view a list courses by term in a PDF file that is updated daily. You can download a free copy of Acrobat Reader from <a href="http://get.adobe.com/reader">http://get.adobe.com/reader</a>.</p>
