<h2 style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><strong>ABOUT THE FITNESS PROGRAM</strong></h2>
<p style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">The UNT Fitness program oversees the weightroom, personal training, faculty/staff fitness, and group exercise&nbsp;classes and programs. These classes&nbsp;are designed to increase your fitness level through safe and fun classes - at your own pace - and are often&nbsp;facilitated by one of our&nbsp;many certified instructors&nbsp;and trainers.</p>
<p style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">These activities are a great way to meet new friends, stay in shape, and keep a regular exercise routine. Taking classes (group exercise or specialty fitness programs) is also a great way to take your workout to the next level, or break the rut of your workout routine.</p>
<p style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">Free group exercise classes are available to all currently enrolled UNT students and to all Rec Center members. Free classes are indicated on the <a href="group_ex/groupex">Group Exercise Schedule</a> with an asterisk (*). All other classes are available with the purchase of a group exercise pass.</p>
<p class="notice" style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">For all fitness programs, please register in the Rec Sports office Monday through Friday, 8am-5pm. For more information, please call (940) 565-2275 or (940) 369-8347.</p>
<p style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">For more specific information about any of the areas listed above you can click on the program specific link below:</p>
<p style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><a href="https://recsports.unt.edu/group_ex/groupex" target="_blank">Group Exercise</a>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <a href="https://recsports.unt.edu/group_ex/programs" target="_blank">Fitness Programs</a>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <a href="https://recsports.unt.edu/group_ex/personal_training" target="_blank">Personal Training</a>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <a href="https://recsports.unt.edu/group_ex/fs/index" target="_blank">Faculty/Staff Fitness</a></p>
<p style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">_________________________</p>
<h2 style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>SPRING 2016 SPECIAL FITNESS EVENTS</strong></span></h2>
<h2 style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">GROUP EXERCISE INSTRUCTOR TRAINING PROGRAM</h2>
<p style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">Ever wonder what it takes to become a group exercise instructor? Interested in learning more? This 6 week course will provide you with the foundational theories and practical application of skills needed to lead others in their fitness journey. Whether your interest is cardio-based, mind-body, or circuit training this class will provide the opportunity to explore your interests while helping to prepare you to teach a group exercise class.</p>
<p style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">**Register for either the Tuesday classes OR the Wednesday classes.**</p>
<p style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">**This class will not meet during Spring Break.**</p>
