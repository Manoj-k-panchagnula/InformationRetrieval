					<h2>
	Mission Statement</h2>

<p>
	<i>&quot;The University Union enhances the educational process and builds community by providing an inclusive environment where individuals are valued through quality programs and support services.&quot;</i></p>

<h2>
	Values</h2>

<p>
	The University Union values:</p>

<ul>
	<li>
		Being Student-Centered:&nbsp; We value student involvement, employment, and leadership opportunities that promote learning and personal growth beyond the classroom;</li>
	<li>
		Being Service-Oriented:&nbsp; We value great customer service, overall quality, and adaptability;</li>
	<li>
		Being Technology-Driven:&nbsp; We value technology and advancements that increase our efficiency in delivering programs and services;</li>
	<li>
		Being Environmentally-Conscious:&nbsp; We value environmentally responsible practices and sustainable initiatives;</li>
	<li>
		Being Diverse:&nbsp; We value individual uniqueness represented through race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, socioeconomic background, age, ability, national origin, religion, and thought; and</li>
	<li>
		Being Ethical:&nbsp; We value integrity as demonstrated through honesty, civility, and fairness.</li>
</ul>
