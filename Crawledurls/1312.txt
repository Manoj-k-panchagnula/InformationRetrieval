<h3>Mean Green Club</h3>
<p>The Mean Green Club raises funds for North Texas athletic scholarships. Donations to the Mean Green Club help support nearly 350 North Texas student-athletes each year and provide them with the best collegiate experience possible.</p>
<p>Members receive exclusive access to North Texas athletics and year-long benefits such as invitations to member-only events, the best parking and seating available, and more.
<p>For more information or to join Mean Green Club, call us at (940) 369-7284, email us at meangreenclub@unt.edu, or <a class="mainlink" href="http://www.meangreensports.com/sports/mg-club/spec-rel/ways-to-give.html" target="_blank">click here to visit us on the web</a>.</p>

<h3>Beth Marie's Junior Mean Green Club</h3>
<p>The Beth Marie's Junior Mean Green Club is perfect for young fans to experience special opportunities and join all the excitement of North Texas athletics. Children 15 years of age or younger can join. Annual membership is only $20 per child. <a class="mainlink" href="http://www.meangreensports.com/ot/junior-mgc-main.html" target="_blank">Click here for details on how to sign up your child</a>.</p>
