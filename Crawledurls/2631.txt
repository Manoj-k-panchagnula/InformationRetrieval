          <h3 class="title" style="margin-bottom: 1em;">Welcome!</h3>
          <div class="container-12">
            <div class="grid-9"><div class="inside">
              
              <p>You&rsquo;ll find information, forms and links for HR and State of Texas services here on the <a href="https://hr.untsystem.edu">Human Resources website</a>.</p>
              <p>If you need assistance on the Denton campus, please call us at <strong>940-565-2281</strong> or visit our office in <strong>Sycamore Hall, Room 119</strong>. We also can assist with HR information related to the Frisco campus.</p>
              
<p>We're available to help faculty and staff who have questions about employee relations, policies and procedures and benefits. We&rsquo;ll help you find support for recruiting and hiring and leadership and management training from Talent Acquisition, Talent Management and Total Rewards, the Human Resources departments that serve UNT and all system locations.<strong><em><br />
- Brandi Renton, PHR, director, UNT Campus HR</em></strong>
</p>
