  <div class="content">
    <h4>All University management&nbsp;is responsible for:</h4>
<ul>
<li>being knowledgeable of federal and state laws that impact on their business units.</li>
<li>informing their employees of the specific regulatory requirements that apply to their job functions.</li>
<li>recommending or providing training that employees need to remain in compliance with the law.</li>
<li>participating in the responsibility for compliance implementation and enforcement within their units.</li>
</ul>
