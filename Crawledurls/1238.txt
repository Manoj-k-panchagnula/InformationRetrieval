<p>Are you a senior experiencing course scheduling roadblocks?</p>
<p>The Class Concierge is here to help!</p>
</div>
</div>
<h3>How we can help you:</h3>
<p>If you’re a senior (90 or more hours) experiencing course scheduling roadblocks that may impact your timely graduation, then the Class Concierge service is here for you.</p>
<p>The Class Concierge will work on your behalf with your academic advisor, UNT departments and your college or school on matters such as time conflicts between required courses or waitlisted required courses.</p>
<p>UNT is dedicated to helping you earn your bachelor’s degree.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3>Before requesting assistance:</h3>
<ol>
<li>Complete all required prerequisite courses</li>
<li><strong>Meet with your academic advisor to discuss your situation</strong></li>
<li>Complete the <a href="/sites/default/files/u3/Application_for_assistance.pdf">Application for Assistance</a>&nbsp;and email it to the Class Concierge</li>
</ol>
<p class="p1">You'll be contacted by phone or email within three business days.</p>
<p>If you know other seniors who could benefit from this service, please spread the word. We want our students to achieve their dreams with a UNT degree.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3>Contact information:</h3>
<p><a href="mailto:ClassConcierge@unt.edu">ClassConcierge@unt.edu</a><br />Sage Hall, room 315</p>
