  <h2 class="node__title node-title">How to Apply for a Job</h2>
  
  <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"><p><a href="https://jobs.unt.edu/applicants/jsp/shared/frameset/Frameset.jsp?time=1415831062801unt-dallas.peopleadmin.com/"><img alt="" src="/sites/default/files/TA_UNT-Jobs-button.jpg" style="width: 119px; height: 119px; margin-left: 20px; margin-right: 20px; float: left;" /></a></p>
<p><a href="https://facultyjobs.unt.edu/applicants/jsp/shared/Welcome_css.jsp"><img alt="" src="/sites/default/files/TA_UNT-Faculty-Recruitment.jpg" style="width: 120px; height: 120px; margin-left: 20px; margin-right: 20px; float: left;" /></a></p>
<p><a href="https://www.unthscjobs.com/applicants/jsp/shared/frameset/Frameset.jsp?time=1414515212989" title="Apply for Job at UNTHSC"><img alt="Jobs at UNTHSC" src="/sites/default/files/Jobs%20at%20UNTHSC2.jpg" style="float: left; margin: 0px 10px; width: 119px; height: 119px;" /></a></p>
<p> </p>
<p> </p>
<p> </p>
<p> </p>
<p><a href="https://unt-dallas.peopleadmin.com/"><img alt="" src="/sites/default/files/FormsForAllLocations/Jobs%20at%20UNTDallas.jpg" style="width: 120px; height: 120px; margin: 0px 20px;" /></a><a href="http://unt-dallas.peopleadmin.com/"><img alt="" src="//hr.untsystem.edu/sites/default/files/ta_untd-lawjobs-button.jpg" style="width: 120px; height: 120px; margin-left: 20px; margin-right: 20px;" /></a><a href="https://hr.untsystem.edu/apply-job-unt-system-administration"><img alt="" src="//hr.untsystem.edu/sites/default/files/ta-green-button-system_admin.jpg" style="width: 120px; height: 120px; margin-left: 10px; margin-right: 10px;" /></a></p>
<p>Click the button above to find job vacancies at those locations: <a href="http://jobs.unt.edu/applicants/jsp/shared/frameset/Frameset.jsp?time=1415831062801unt-dallas.peopleadmin.com/">UNT</a> (includes <a href="https://hr.untsystem.edu/employment-opportunities-unt-new-college-frisco">Frisco</a>), <a href="http://www.unthscjobs.com/applicants/jsp/shared/frameset/Frameset.jsp?time=1414515212989">UNTHSC</a> and <a href="http://unt-dallas.peopleadmin.com/">UNT Dallas</a>, <a href="http://unt-dallas.peopleadmin.com/">UNT Dallas College of Law</a> and <a href="https://hr.untsystem.edu/apply-job-unt-system-administration">UNT System Administration</a>.</p>
<ul><li>The <a href="http://facultyjobs.unt.edu/applicants/jsp/shared/Welcome_css.jsp">UNT Office of the Provost</a> manages the UNT Faculty Career site for regular faculty, librarians, academic administrators and research faculty positions.</li>
<li>The Division of Student Affairs manages the <a href="http://studentaffairs.unt.edu/career-center">UNT Career Center</a>.</li>
<li>Job listings are on a separate website; these links take users there.</li>
</ul><p>Please note:</p>
<ul><li>All applications must be submitted online. Links to job listings, above, take applicants to the online application site. (ADA accommodations are available upon request.)</li>
<li>When an application is submitted, it is a final document that cannot be changed.</li>
<li>Follow instructions in the application links above to return to the job listing.</li>
<li>If you have technical problems or questions, contact <a href="mailto:talentacquisition@untsystem.edu?subject=Application%20problem%20or%20question">Talent Acquisition</a> or the Human Resources Support Center, 1-855-878-7650, weekdays, 8 a.m. to 5 p.m.</li>
<li>Applicants are subject to a criminal background check (and a drug screen at UNT Health Science Center) before an employment offer is confirmed.</li>
<li>The UNT<a href="https://hr.untsystem.edu/policies-and-information"><img alt="" src="//hr.untsystem.edu/sites/default/files/e-verify_banner.png" style="width: 150px; margin: 5px 10px; float: right; height: 45px;" /></a> System participates in E-Verify, the federal program that verifies eligibility for employment using Form I-9.</li>
<li>If you have been an employee at any UNT System location for a minimum of six (6) months, you may apply online to transfer to another UNT System location or department.</li>
<li> <a href="https://hr.untsystem.edu/living-denton-dallas-and-fort-worth">Learn about living in Denton, Dallas and Fort Worth</a>.</li>
</ul><p>Managers who need help with recruiting and hiring should contact <a href="mailto:talentacquisition@untsystem.edu">Talent Acquisition</a>.</p>
<p>Here are answers to frequently asked questions about applying for a position.</p>
<div class="accButton"><button class="expand AllAcc">Expand All</button></div>
<ul class="accordian" style="clear: both;"><li>
<h2>Where are job vacancies posted?</h2>
<div class="info">
<ul><li>Click the green button above for the location where you wish to work to find job vacancies there.</li>
<li>Find positions that match your skills and experience.</li>
<li><strong>Note that only online applications will be considered</strong>.</li>
<li>If you need internet access, find free or low-cost computers at each <a href="https://hr.untsystem.edu/meet-human-resources-team" title="UNT System Human Resources">Campus HR</a> location, any <a href="http://www.twc.state.tx.us/customers/jsemp/job-search.html" target="_blank">Texas Workforce Commission</a> office, most public libraries, some community centers and at commercial locations. Free computer kiosks also are available in <a href="http://bsc.untsystem.edu/" target="_blank" title="UNT System Business Service Center">Business Support Services</a>, 1112 Dallas Drive, Suite 4000, Denton.</li>
<li>The <a href="http://studentaffairs.unt.edu/explore?category=30">UNT Career Center</a> provides services for students and alumni at UNT in Denton. </li>
<li>Positions at <a href="https://hr.untsystem.edu/employment-opportunities-unt-new-college-frisco">UNT New College at Frisco</a> are listed with UNT and the UNT Office of the Provost.</li>
</ul></div>
</li>
<li>
<h2>Do I have to complete an application for each position?</h2>
<div class="info">
<p>No, you may create one online application and can apply it to multiple positions. You may edit your application as needed to show the experience most relevant to the position for which you are applying. You may not submit a résumé for consideration without completing the online application process. You may attach a résumé and other relevant documents during the online application process.</p>
</div>
</li>
<li>
<h2>Do I have to complete all sections if I submit a resume?</h2>
<div class="info">
<p>Yes. A resume may not be substituted for application fields. <strong><em>Complete all application fields</em></strong>. When you click Submit, you cannot make changes or additions. When you receive a confirmation number, your application has been sent to the hiring department and changes cannot be made. If you have technical problems or questions, contact <a href="mailto:talentacquisition@untsystem.edu?subject=Application%20problem%20or%20question">Talent Acquisition</a> or the Human Resources Support Center, 1-855-878-7650, weekdays, 8 a.m. to 5 p.m.</p>
</div>
</li>
<li>
<h2>Can I save my application without finishing?</h2>
<div class="info">
<p>Yes. Click on the Save and Stay on this Page button. This allows you to return to the application and see data through the last page that was saved, even if you have not completed all required fields. You have the option to save changes before leaving the page. The online session will time out after 60 minutes of inactivity. If your session times out, your information will be lost if you have not saved it. If you have technical problems or questions, contact <a href="mailto:talentacquisition@untsystem.edu?subject=Application%20problem%20or%20question">Talent Acquisition</a> or the Human Resources Support Center, 1-855-878-7650, weekdays, 8 a.m. to 5 p.m.</p>
</div>
</li>
<li>
<h2>Can I use the Back button to get to a prior page?</h2>
<div class="info">
<p>Do not use your browser's back, forward or refresh buttons to navigate. This may cause your application to time out or lose data. Please use only navigational buttons within the site.</p>
</div>
</li>
<li>
<h2>What if I forget my username and password?</h2>
<div class="info">
<p>Contact the Human Resources Support Center, 1-855-878-7650 or <a href="mailto:hrrecords@untsystem.edu">hrrecords@untsystem.edu</a>, from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m., Monday through Friday.</p>
</div>
</li>
<li>
<h2>How do I submit veteran’s preference information?</h2>
<div class="info">
<p>Complete the <a href="//hr.untsystem.edu/sites/default/files/FormsForAllLocations/veterans_self-identification_form_2015.docx">Veterans Self-Identification form</a> and submit a copy of your service record (<a href="http://www.archives.gov/veterans/military-service-records/" target="_blank">DD214</a>) as an attachment to your application.</p>
</div>
</li>
<li>
<h2>Can I use an alternate social security number?</h2>
<div class="info">
<p>No. You must register for Social Security and use a legal number to apply. If you are hired, you must present an official Social Security card or a receipt for a Social Security number of the legal card. If you are hired, you cannot begin work without a legal social security number.</p>
</div>
</li>
<li>
<h2>Can my college hours be substituted for experience and vice versa?</h2>
<div class="info">
<p>Hiring departments sometimes will accept substitutions for experience and education. Every 30 hours of college can be substituted for one year of experience. Every year of experience can be substituted for one year of college. Please review the minimum qualifications for each position.</p>
</div>
</li>
<li>
<h2>Do I have to provide an official transcript?</h2>
<div class="info">
<p>If the position requires a college degree or if college education is a qualifying factor, an official transcript will be required upon employment.</p>
</div>
</li>
<li>
<h2>How do I print my application?</h2>
<div class="info">
<p>Before submitting your application, you will have the option to view it. When your application opens in a new browser window, use the tool bar at the top of your computer screen, click on File and then on Print, and follow the prompts. If you have technical problems or questions, contact <a href="mailto:talentacquisition@untsystem.edu?subject=Application%20problem%20or%20question">Talent Acquisition</a> or the Human Resources Support Center, 1-855-878-7650, weekdays, 8 a.m. to 5 p.m.</p>
</div>
</li>
<li>
<h2>How long will my application remain active?</h2>
<div class="info">
<p>Your application will be active for as long as you continue to apply for positions.</p>
</div>
</li>
<li>
<h2>Will Human Resources refer my application to other managers?</h2>
<div class="info">
<p>No. Applicants must review new postings and apply for each.</p>
</div>
</li>
<li>
<h2>May I withdraw my application?</h2>
<div class="info">
<p>Yes, but please note that when your application has been withdrawn, you will no longer be considered and you cannot apply for the same position. You may apply for other positions.</p>
</div>
</li>
<li>
<h2>Will I be contacted by the hiring department?</h2>
<div class="info">
<p>After you apply and receive a requisition number, your application will be submitted to the hiring department. The hiring department will review applications and Talent Acquisition will assist with arranging interviews.</p>
</div>
</li>
<li>
<h2>How can I check the status of my application?</h2>
<div class="info">
<p>After logging in to the job application site, click on <em>Positions for Which I Have Applied</em> in the left menu bar. You will see a notation that says:</p>
<ul><li>In-Process - Position remains open and based on your skills/qualifications, you may or may not be contacted for an interview.</li>
<li>Position Filled - Another candidate has been hired.</li>
</ul></div>
</li>
<li>
<h2>Questions?</h2>
<div class="info">
<p>Email Human Resources Records and Support Center at <a href="mailto:hrrecords@untsystem.edu" title="Human Resources Employment">hrrecords@untsystem.edu</a>, or call 1-855-878-7650, from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m., Monday through Friday.</p>
</div>
</li>
</ul><p> </p>
</div></div></div>
