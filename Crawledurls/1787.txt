              <div class="breadcrumb"><a href="/">Home</a></div><h2>Free and Legal Entertainment</h2><div id="node-32" class="node clear-block">



  <div class="meta">
  
    </div>

  <div class="content">
    <p><img src="/sites/default/files/free_music.png" alt="free music" width="214" height="140" /></p>
<div>
<h3 style="margin: 0px 0px 12px; padding: 0px 0px 2px; border-bottom-width: 2px; border-bottom-style: solid; border-bottom-color: #006633; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', tahoma, arial, 'lucida sans', helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 19px;">Music, Videos and More</h3>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 6px; font-size: 11px; line-height: 15px; font-family: verdana, arial, 'lucida sans', helvetica, sans-serif;">Trying to stay up-to-date on the availability of music and video resources online and otherwise is pretty much impossible. The list of websites and resources below is just a jumping-off point. Remember, Google (or insert your favorite search engine name here) is your friend. Looking for something? Search the Web for it, it is probably out there! Whatever you do, always try to be a "legal eagle." The&nbsp;<a style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;" href="http://www.riaa.com/">Recording Industry Association of America®, RIAA,</a>&nbsp;and/or the&nbsp;<a style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;" href="http://www.mpaa.org/contentprotection/public-service-announcements">the Motion Picture Association of America, MPAA,</a>&nbsp;may be watching.&nbsp;<a style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;" href="http://arstechnica.com/tech-policy/news/2011/01/copy-some-web-pages-owe-more-than-the-national-debt.ars">Seriously</a>.</p>
<p><br /></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 6px; font-size: 11px; line-height: 15px; font-family: verdana, arial, 'lucida sans', helvetica, sans-serif;">UNT takes a hard stance against the illegal sharing of copyright protected material, for more information please read&nbsp;<a style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;" href="http://security.unt.edu/sites/all/files/approach_to_copyright.pdf">UNT's Approach to Addressing Copyright Infringement</a>.</p>
<p><br /></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 6px; font-size: 11px; line-height: 15px; font-family: verdana, arial, 'lucida sans', helvetica, sans-serif;">The RIAA has set up this&nbsp;<a style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;" href="http://www.riaa.com/toolsforparents.php?content_selector=legal-music-services">page</a>&nbsp;containing "some of the more popular legal online music sources" to help you access music online legally.</p>
<h4 style="margin: 6px 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', tahoma, arial, 'lucida sans', helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 15px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">ResNet and the dorms</strong></h4>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 6px; font-size: 11px; line-height: 15px; font-family: verdana, arial, 'lucida sans', helvetica, sans-serif;">&nbsp;</p>
<ul style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 8px 14px; font-size: 11px; list-style-type: square; font-family: verdana, arial, 'lucida sans', helvetica, sans-serif;">
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 8px;">The University of North Texas has partnered with Apogee in order to provide Residential Network (ResNet) service. You can view Apogee’s end user agreement at<a style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;" href="https://www.myresnet.com/pdf/eua.pdf">https://www.myresnet.com/pdf/eua.pdf&nbsp;</a>. EDUCAUSE has created a&nbsp;<a style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;" href="http://www.campusdownloading.com/">page&nbsp;</a>that provides resources for&nbsp;<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">legal</strong>&nbsp;campus downloading. Some of the sites they list for legal&nbsp;<a style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;" href="http://www.campusdownloading.com/legal.htm">music downloads&nbsp;</a>are also listed below. You can:</li>
</ul>
<ul style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 8px 14px; font-size: 11px; list-style-type: square; font-family: verdana, arial, 'lucida sans', helvetica, sans-serif;">
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 8px;">Buy Music -- For example:<br />
<ul style="margin: 0px 0px -6px; padding: 6px 0px 0px 14px; list-style-type: square;">
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 8px;"><a style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;" href="http://www.apple.com/itunes/">iTunes.com</a></li>
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 8px;"><a style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;" href="http://www.buy.com/dept/Music_CDs_/109.html">Buy.com</a></li>
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 8px;"><a style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;" href="http://music.msn.com/">MSN Music</a></li>
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 8px;"><a style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;" href="http://www.walmart.com/music">WalMart Music</a></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 8px;">Purchase music subscriptions from places such as:<br />
<ul style="margin: 0px 0px -6px; padding: 6px 0px 0px 14px; list-style-type: square;">
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 8px;"><a style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;" href="http://www.rhapsody.com/home.html">Rhapsody</a></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 8px;">Listen to music online:<br />
<ul style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 8px 14px; list-style-type: square;">
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 8px;"><a style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;" href="https://www.spotify.com/">Spotify</a></li>
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 8px;"><a style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;" href="http://pandora.com/">Pandora</a></li>
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 8px;"><a style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;" href="http://music.aol.com/">AOL Music</a></li>
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 8px;"><a style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;" href="http://www.mp3.com/">MP3.com</a></li>
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 8px;"><a style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;" href="http://www.last.fm/">Last.fm</a></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 8px;">Get music for free:<br />
<ul style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 8px 14px; list-style-type: square;">
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 8px;"><a style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;" href="http://www.amazon.com/Free-Songs-Music/b?ie=UTF8&amp;node=334897011">Amazon.com:Free</a></li>
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 8px;"><a style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;" href="http://www.last.fm/music/+free-music-downloads">Free Music Downloads on Last.fm</a></li>
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 8px;"><a style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;" href="http://www.bearshare.com/">BearShare</a></li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<h4 style="margin: 6px 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', tahoma, arial, 'lucida sans', helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 15px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">On the Internet</strong></h4>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 6px; font-size: 11px; line-height: 15px; font-family: verdana, arial, 'lucida sans', helvetica, sans-serif;">Many websites offer a combination of music and videos for sale and, at times, free.</p>
<ul style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 8px 14px; font-size: 11px; list-style-type: square; font-family: verdana, arial, 'lucida sans', helvetica, sans-serif;">
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 8px;"><a style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;" href="http://www.apple.com/itunes/whats-on/">iTunes</a>, for example, offers music, movies, TV shows, books and podcasts. Additionally, iTunes U -- "Free lectures from top institutions."</li>
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 8px;">Besides providing music and videos - for sale and for free --<a style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;" href="http://music.aol.com/">AOL Music&nbsp;</a>has an "Online Radio" area that offers&nbsp;<a style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;" href="http://music.aol.com/radioguide/bb">Free Internet Radio Stations</a>.</li>
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 8px;"><a style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;" href="http://new.music.yahoo.com/">Yahoo! Music&nbsp;</a>has been the "go to" place for listening to music online for a long time. It continues to offer free access to Internet radio, music videos, artist interviews, and lots more.</li>
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 8px;"><a style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;" href="http://www.mtv.com/music/">MTV&nbsp;</a>has all kinds of free music, videos, and music videos. About.com gives you the scoop on how to&nbsp;<a style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;" href="http://desktopvideo.about.com/od/watchingonlinevideo/p/mtv.htm">Watch Free Music Videos From MTV&nbsp;</a>and more.</li>
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 8px;">Also available from About.com,&nbsp;<a style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;" href="http://websearch.about.com/od/searchtipoftheday/qt/freemovie.htm?nl=1">A Comprehensive Guide to the Best Sites for Free Movie Downloads&nbsp;</a>.</li>
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 8px;"><a style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;" href="http://www.vh1.com/music/">VH1&nbsp;</a>has a site with a similar look and feel of the MTV website.</li>
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 8px;"><a style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;" href="http://www.magnatune.com/">Magnatune&nbsp;</a>-- offers "independent music to listen, download or license."</li>
</ul>
<p><strong><br />
<h4 style="margin: 6px 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', tahoma, arial, 'lucida sans', helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 15px;">On Campus</h4>
<p></p></strong></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 6px; font-size: 11px; line-height: 15px; font-family: verdana, arial, 'lucida sans', helvetica, sans-serif;">&nbsp;</p>
<ul style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 8px 14px; font-size: 11px; list-style-type: square; font-family: verdana, arial, 'lucida sans', helvetica, sans-serif;">
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 8px;">A great source of free music and videos on campus is the&nbsp;<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">University of North Texas Libraries</strong>. Specifically, the&nbsp;<a style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;" href="http://www.library.unt.edu/media">Media Library</a>&nbsp;and the&nbsp;<a style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;" href="http://www.library.unt.edu/music/music-library">Music Library</a>. Also worth noting is the Media Library's&nbsp;<a style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;" href="http://www.library.unt.edu/media/news-events">Film Series</a>. More sources for movies, both on-campus and for download can be found at the Libraries'<a style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;" href="http://www.library.unt.edu/media/collection/online/">Online Media</a>&nbsp;page. You can read more about the UNT Libraries and all their assets&nbsp;<a style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;" href="http://www.library.unt.edu/about">here&nbsp;</a>.</li>
</ul>
<ul style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 8px 14px; font-size: 11px; list-style-type: square; font-family: verdana, arial, 'lucida sans', helvetica, sans-serif;">
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 8px;">The&nbsp;<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">University Program Council</strong>&nbsp;(<a style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;" href="http://www.unt.edu/union/upc.htm">UPC</a>) hosts movies and events, most of them free. Check out the&nbsp;<a style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;" href="http://web3.unt.edu/calendar/calendar.cfm">campus calendar</a>&nbsp;(you can filter for the type of events you are interested in).</li>
</ul>
