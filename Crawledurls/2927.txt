  <div class="content">
    <p>The Office of Data, Analytics, &amp; Institutional Research at the University of North Texas provides an abundance of timely data in support of planning, policymaking, academic assessment and program reviews, in accordance with the institution's mission and strategic goals.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Data, Analytics, &amp; Institutional Research collects data on a large breadth of topics, including enrollment, financial aid, research, faculty and staff, and more.&nbsp; Reports are generated each year based on the findings and can be viewed in <a title="IR&amp;E Facts &amp; Figures" href="/facts-figures">Facts &amp; Figures</a>.</p>
