<div class="sidebar">
<h2>North Texas Athletic Ticket Office<br />and Mean Green Team Store</h2>
<ul><li>Hours: Monday-Friday, 8 a.m.-5 p.m.</li>
<li>Sports ticketed: football, basketball, soccer, volleyball, softball and track and field</li>
<li>Phone: 940-565-2527 or 800-868-2366</li>
<li><a class="toplinks" href="http://www.northtexasfanguide.com" target="_new">North Texas Fan Guide</a></li>
<li><a class="toplinks" href="http://shop.meangreensports.com" title="Mean Green Team Store" target="_new">Mean Green Team Store online</a></li></ul>
</div>
<p>The North Texas Athletic Ticket Office and the Mean Green Team Store are located on the west side of Apogee Stadium, and, in addition to hours on home game days, are open Monday-Friday from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. throughout the year.</p>
<a href="http://shop.meangreensports.com" title="Mean Green Team Store online" target="_new"><img class="floatleft" src="jersey.jpg" width="110" height="150" border="0" /></a>
<p>The Mean Green Team Store is an 1,800-square-foot store packed with Mean Green apparel, gifts, tailgating gear and collectibles. You can also order your green gear <a class="para" href="http://shop.meangreensports.com" target="_new">online</a>.</p> 
<p>On home-football game days, the ticket windows open four hours prior to kickoff and remains open until halftime. Tickets for other sports can also be purchased at the Athletic ticket office up to four hours prior to the game on weekdays. After that time and on weekends, tickets are available at the venue. The ticket office also sells tickets to away games.</p>
