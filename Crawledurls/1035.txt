<h1 class="title">Around Denton</h1>
<div id="node-5" class="node node-type-page build-mode-full clearfix">
<div class="content">
<p>The City of Denton is the quintessential North Texas county seat complete with the Downtown Courthouse and shopping district. A favorite spot among Dentonites, the Denton County Courthouse lawn is a place where locals and visitors alike stop to picnic, people watch, or play a game of Frisbee. Downtown, more commonly known as “The Square,” has many shops and restaurants to explore. Denton is also known for its live music scene. <em><a href="http://travel.nytimes.com/2008/05/11/travel/11cultured.html">The New York Times</a></em> has featured Denton for its “Indie scene with a Texas twang”.</p>
<p>For more information about Denton, please visit the <a href="http://www.discoverdenton.com/">Discover Denton</a> website.</p>

<h3>Events On Campus</h3>
<p>We encourage you to explore the Denton community during your UNT Preview  weekend – after all, you won't spend all of the next four years solely  on-campus! The City of Denton is the quintessential North Texas county seat  complete with the Downtown Courthouse and shopping district. A favorite spot  among Dentonites, the Denton County Courthouse lawn is a place where locals and  visitors alike stop to picnic, people watch, or play a game of Frisbee.  Downtown, more commonly known as "The Square," has many shops and restaurants  to explore. Denton is also known for its live music scene. The New York Times  has featured Denton for its "Indie scene with a Texas twang."</p>
<p><a href="http://www.discoverdenton.com/">Discover Denton</a> and learn more  about this vibrant North Texas community. Here are just a few of the events  happening around town during the UNT Preview Weekend:</p>
<p> <strong>Friday,  February 19th </strong><br /><br /><a href="http://www.discoverdenton.com/event/9th-annual-thin-line-film-festival/?instance_id=97946">9th Annual Thin  Line Film Festival</a> @ The Campus Theatre <br /><br />
