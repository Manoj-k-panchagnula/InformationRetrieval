    <section class="block block-block block-18 block-block-18 odd" id="block-block-18">
  <div class="block-inner clearfix">
              <h3 class="block-title">We are part of the community.</h3>
            
    <div class="content clearfix">
      <p>Every day, UNT reaches out to the community to create partnerships and offer helpful services, making us the university <em>for</em> North Texas. UNT enriches, inspires and helps the communities of the region in many ways. A cultural hot spot in <a href="http://www.discoverdenton.com">Denton</a> — which was named “<a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zXa_RfAKgHo">the best college town in Texas</a>” — UNT is home to world-class performance facilities, esteemed faculty artists and internationally recognized guests. By blazing the trail as a leader in the arts, UNT has produced renowned alumni such as Grammy-winning singer Norah Jones and National Medal of Arts recipient and sculptor Jesús Moroles.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>  </div>
</div><div class="grid-12 region region-content-single" id="region-content-single">
  <div class="region-inner region-content-single-inner">
    <section class="block block-block block-37 block-block-37 odd" id="block-block-37">
  <div class="block-inner clearfix">
              <h3 class="block-title">In the community</h3>
            
    <div class="content clearfix">
      <p><a href="http://untonthesquare.unt.edu">UNT on the Square</a>, home of the Institute for the Advancement of the Arts, presents exhibitions, concerts, poetry readings and more in downtown Denton. The institute hosts respected artists-in-residence such as alumnus Nick Cave, who debuted an eye-catching performance piece called <a href="http://northtexan.unt.edu/content/nick-caves-heard-performed-university-north-texas"><em>Heard</em></a> at UNT, and composer Jake Heggie, whose Moby-Dick-inspired <a href="http://www.unt.edu/features/2013_jake-heggie"><em>Ahab Symphony</em></a> premiered at UNT.</p>

<h4>Events</h4>

<p>From French opera to blackout poetry, UNT presents 1,500 performances, exhibitions and lectures each year.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</section><div class="block block-block block-41 block-block-41 even block-without-title" id="block-block-41">
  <div class="block-inner clearfix">
                
    <div class="content clearfix">
      <p><img alt="" class="media-image img__fid__31 img__view_mode__media_original attr__format__media_original" height="306" src="/sites/default/files/planting_0.jpg" width="632" /></p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div><div class="block block-block block-39 block-block-39 odd block-without-title" id="block-block-39">
  <div class="block-inner clearfix">
                
    <div class="content clearfix">
      <h4>Notable Speakers</h4>

<p>Through the <a href="http://studentaffairs.unt.edu/distinguished-lecture-series">Distinguished Lecture Series</a>, the <a href="http://union.unt.edu/fas">Fine Arts Series</a> and more, UNT attracts high-caliber speakers such as former Secretary of State Condoleezza Rice, <em>World War Z</em> author Max Brooks and Apple co-founder Steve Wozniak.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div><div class="block block-block block-38 block-block-38 even block-without-title" id="block-block-38">
  <div class="block-inner clearfix">
                
    <div class="content clearfix">
      <h4>Outreach</h4>

<p>UNT’s experienced staff and students offer help to the public through a number of facilities such as the <a href="http://cpt.unt.edu">Center for Play Therapy</a>, the <a href="http://autism.unt.edu">Kristin Farmer Autism Center</a> and the <a href="http://speechandhearing.unt.edu/sphs-clinic">Speech and Hearing Center</a>, among others.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div><section class="block block-block block-40 block-block-40 odd" id="block-block-40">
  <div class="block-inner clearfix">
              <h3 class="block-title">Artistic Excellence</h3>
            
    <div class="content clearfix">
      <p>Celebrated artists, musicians, faculty and students perform and exhibit their work in UNT’s numerous state-of-the-art facilities, where community members from across the North Texas region and beyond experience UNT’s artistic excellence.</p>

<h4>Finest</h4>

<p>The UNT <a href="http://music.unt.edu">College of Music</a> stands as one of the nation’s best, largest and most comprehensive music schools — with the nation’s first jazz studies program.</p>

<h4>Best</h4>

<p><em>U.S. News &amp; World Report</em> ranks the <a href="http://art.unt.edu">College of Visual Arts and Design</a> 62nd nationally among top graduate schools for fine arts. The college is considered one of the best art schools in the South and Southwest.</p>

<h4>Prestigious</h4>

<p>An educational and cultural resource, the <a href="http://www.tfc.unt.edu">Texas Fashion Collection</a> holds 18,000 historic items from renowned designers.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
