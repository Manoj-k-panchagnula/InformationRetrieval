          <a href="http://blog.orgsync.com/2012/orgsync-benefits-students-video" class="side-callout orange left">
            January 16, 2012            <span class="arrow"></span>
          </a>
          <br class="clearboth">
          <p>As students begin to arrive back on campus, we know that many of our new campus partners are preparing to launch OrgSync this spring. To explain how OrgSync can benefit your students, we created this short interactive video. Students are the catalyst that makes OrgSync a success on every campus we work with, and we encourage you to share this with your students to help spread the word about OrgSync across campus.</p>
<p><center><iframe src="http://player.vimeo.com/video/34485479?title=0&amp;byline=0&amp;portrait=0&amp;color=c9ff23" width="450" height="250" frameborder="0" webkitAllowFullScreen mozallowfullscreen allowFullScreen></iframe>
<p><a href="http://vimeo.com/34485479">OrgSync for Students</a></center></p>
<p>Thanks to our designer, <a href="http://www.orgsync.com/about-orgsync/our-team/design#jeff_jurica" target="blank">Jeff Jurica</a>, for the hard-work and creativity on this project. Let us know what you think in the comments section below.</p>
