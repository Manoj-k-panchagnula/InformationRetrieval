<h2>Time to get Started</h2>

<div class="intro">
<p>Choosing where to go to college is a big decision, but it doesn’t have to be a stressful one! This website will help you figure out what to keep in mind as you begin your college search, how to navigate the application process and how to pay for school once you decide. The information here will help you find the right college for you and help you be prepared before you step foot on campus.</p>
