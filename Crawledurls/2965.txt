                <h2 class="title" id="page-title">Celebrating the accomplishments of UNT graduates </h2>
                        <div class="block block-system block-main block-system-main odd block-without-title" id="block-system-main">
  <div class="block-inner clearfix">
                
    <div class="content clearfix">
      <div class="node node-page view-mode-front node-not-promoted node-not-sticky author-1 odd clearfix clearfix">
    
  <div class="container-12">
  	    <div class="grid-3 content-area sidebar-content sidebar-right top-important"><div class="inside">
	      <div class="field field-name-updates field-type-ds field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even"> <div class="color-box bg-unt-green">
<h4 class="boxed">Updates</h4>

<ul>
	<li><a href="http://inhouse.unt.edu/spring-2016-commencement">Read more about 2016 Commencement</a></li>
</ul>
</div>
 </div></div></div>	    </div></div>
	    
  
  
	  	    <div class="grid-9 content-area main-content top-important"><div class="inside">
	      <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"> <p>The Mean Green family comes together each year at commencement to honor the hard work and celebrate the success of our graduates. These are stories of pride, spirit and support -- the University of North Texas way.</p>
<iframe frameborder="0" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/4L5lqV1nPo4?rel=0" style=" margin:0 20px 20px 0px;"></iframe><h2>Congratulations, graduate!</h2>
<p>You’ve done it. All the hard work, late nights and sacrifice are paying off. Soon you’ll be a college graduate, ready to conquer the world with a UNT degree in hand and all of the power and knowledge that comes with it. <strong>It’s time to celebrate!</strong></p>
<p>Commencement marks a passage, the transformation from student to graduate. It’s the beginning of a new journey of discovery and the start of a lifelong affiliation with UNT as an alumnus alumni.</p>
<p>We're celebrating this milestone with you. For you. And with all of the fanfare and grandeur you deserve.</p>
<p>We want you to attend all of the graduation events because there are unique features for each one. It also gives you more opportunities to celebrate with your loved ones and your UNT family.</p>
<h3>Spring Ceremonies</h3>
<ul><li><strong>Every May, UNT's University-wide Commencement honors UNT's annual graduating class</strong> with pomp and circumstance, featuring an inspirational speaker and degree conferral at Apogee Stadium.</li>
<li><strong>Also in May, UNT's Undergraduate College Recognition Ceremonies</strong> enables graduates to enjoy the more personal and traditional moments of graduation with fellow graduates from their program. Graduation is about individual recognition. Graduates names being called. Walking the stage in their cap and gown. Shaking hands with their university administration. Undergraduate College Recognition Ceremonies give graduates the opportunity to celebrate graduation with these traditional features in a more intimate setting<strong>. </strong></li>
<li><strong>A Doctoral and Master's Convocation is held every May</strong> to recognize the accomplishments of students who completed post-graduate studies by that semester. Doctoral and M.F.A. candidates (recipients of terminal degrees) are hooded during this ceremony by their faculty mentor(s).</li>
<li>Those who graduate in May are encouraged to attend both the University-wide Commencement, at which their degrees are conferred, and their college recognition ceremony or doctoral/master’s convocation. </li>
</ul><h3>Fall Ceremonies</h3>
<ul><li><strong>In December, UNT holds Undergraduate College Commencement Ceremonies</strong> for summer and fall graduates. These ceremonies feature the traditional moments of graduation, including degree conferral and individual recognition.</li>
<li><strong>A Doctoral and Master's Commencement is held every December</strong> to recognize doctoral and M.F.A. candidates (recipients of terminal degrees) who completed post-graduate studies by that semester. Fall degree candidates and summer degree recipients are hooded by their faculty mentor(s) and their degrees are conferred during this ceremony.</li>
<li>Those who graduate in December are encouraged return to UNT to attend the University-wide Commencement the following May.</li>
</ul><p><strong>Congratulations, UNT graduate!</strong> Savor this time and be a part of the sea of green pride at UNT’s commencement events.</p>
<p><strong>Learn more about the different commencement events for </strong><a href="../spring-commencement-overview"><strong>Spring</strong></a><strong> and </strong><a href="../fall-commencement-overview"><strong>Fall.</strong></a></p>
