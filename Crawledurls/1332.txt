<h2>
	Hours</h2>

<p style="line-height:18.0pt">
	The fastest way to meet with the attorney is to come to our Open Legal Clinic on Wednesdays. Open Legal Clinic hours are from 8:00am-12:00pm, and then from 1:00pm to 3:30pm&nbsp;every Wednesday. No need to schedule - just stop by!</p>

<p style="line-height:18.0pt">
	<u><strong>Please note:</strong></u></p>

<ul>
	<li style="line-height: 18pt;">
		Open Legal Clinic hours can sometimes change with little notice. We are currently operating with only a part time attorney who is actively advising and representing clients while we await the appointment of Student Legal Service&#39;s new director and full time&nbsp;attorney. We recommend calling the office on the day that you plan to stop by for the clinic if you are traveling&nbsp;a far distance. We appreciate your patience while we adjust to staffing changes.</li>
</ul>

<p style="line-height:18.0pt">
	Appointments can also be scheduled for Tuesdays and Thursdays between 8:00am and 3:30pm. Please note that the wait for non-open legal clinic appointments can sometimes be a week or longer. Please call the office if you wish to schedule an appointment.</p>

<p>
	If you need immediate assistance, we suggest contacting the Lawyer Referral Information Service for the State Bar of Texas. Through this service, you can receive a 30 minute consultation for just $20.00. If you and the attorney agree to keep working after the first 30 minutes, their rates will apply.
