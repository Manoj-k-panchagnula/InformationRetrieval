  <div class="content">
    <p>The
mission of the Research and Statistical Support (RSS) group at the
University of North Texas (UNT) is to facilitate access to current
research tools and statistical methodologies and to promote these
methods to the research, instructional, and administrative communities
at UNT; to encourage a collaborative research environment for
researchers through the development and use of innovative computing
technologies; to provide training and consultation in the appropriate
use of&nbsp; statistical methodologies and computer software; and to
facilitate access to data collection and data management
technologies.&nbsp; RSS accomplishes these goals by:<br /><br /></p><ul><li>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
Collaborating with students, faculty, and staff on research projects
through appointments, telephone, and email.&nbsp; For further details
on our consulting policy, please read this and the consulting and FAQ
links to the right.</li><li>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Providing assistance with statistical theory and techniques, software, data management, and research design.</li><li>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
Keeping current on new statistical computing methodologies and making
these technologies available to the UNT community.</li><li>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Providing training to the UNT community through short course workshops.</li><li>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Providing opportunities for the professional development of the RSS staff.</li></ul>Research and Statistical Support (RSS) is a division of Academic
Computing&nbsp;and User Services (<a href="http://it.unt.edu/ACUS">ACUS</a>); which&nbsp;is a division of
University Information Technology (<a href="http://it.unt.edu/">UIT</a>). RSS is located in&nbsp;<a class="ext" href="http://maps.unt.edu/places/1010/view">Sage Hall</a>,
