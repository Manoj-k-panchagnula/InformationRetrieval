<p>Join us for a UNT signature green tie formal event&nbsp;honoring excellence in teaching, student success, notable alumni and generous donors.</p>
<p><strong>Saturday, April 16, 2016</strong></p>
<p><strong>New University Union</strong></p>
<p>1155 Union Circle, Denton, Texas -- Complimentary valet parking</p>
<p><a href="/sites/default/files/16_490_WINGSPAN_GALA_UNION-MAP.jpg" style="line-height: 20.8px;">Download a map of the University Union</a></p>
<p><strong>6:30 p.m. Reception - Level 3</strong></p>
<p>Highlights include the orchestral premiere of Richard DeRosa's <em>Suite for an Anniversary, </em>composed in honor of UNT's 125th anniversary, performed at 6:50 p.m. and 7:10 p.m. by the UNT Concert Orchestra; UNT jazz entertainment and fashion display; and UNT's archive exhibit <em>From Normal to Now</em>.</p>
<p><strong>7:30 p.m. Dinner and Program - Ballroom, Level 3</strong></p>
<p>Emcee: Dr. Shirley Cothran Barret ('73, '74 M.Ed.), Miss America 1975</p>
<p>Highlights include performances by UNT's Four Tenors, One O' Clock Lab Band and more; special presentations recognizing UNT excellence; and after-dinner dancing to the music of the Emerald City Band All Stars.</p>
<p>For event information, please contact: Mary Howard at <a href="mailto:special.events@unt.edu">special.events@unt.edu</a> or 940-565-3480.</p>
<p>For sponsorship information, please contact: David Broughton at <a href="mailto:david.broughton@unt.edu">david.broughton@unt.edu</a> or 940-565-3641.</p>
