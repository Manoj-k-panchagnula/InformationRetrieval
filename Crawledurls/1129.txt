                                <h1 class="title">Child and Family Resource Clinic </h1>                                                                <div id="content-content" class="content-content">
                                  
<div id="node-254" class="node odd full-node node-type-page">
  <div class="inner">
    
    
    
    
    <div class="content clearfix">
      <p style="text-align: center;"><img style="margin-left: 40px; margin-right: 40px;" src="/sites/default/files/254/CFRClogo.JPG" alt="" height="206" width="193" /> <img style="margin-left: 40px; margin-right: 40px;" src="/sites/default/files/254/DSC03088.JPG" alt="" height="204" width="272" /></p>
<p style="text-align: left;">The Child and Family Resource Clinic (CFRC) is a counseling and training clinic that serves the Denton County community. The CFRC offers counseling to children, adolescents, adults, couples, and families for issues including learning, emotional, and adjustment difficulties. The CFRC's purpose is twofold: to provide professional and responsible services to clients who request them and professional, competent training for graduate students. Click <a title="Frequently asked questions" href="https://www.coe.unt.edu/child-and-family-resource-clinic/frequently-asked-questions-about-counseling">here</a> for frequently asked questions about our services. Click <a title="Schedule an Appointment" href="https://www.coe.unt.edu/child-and-family-resource-clinic/scheduling-counseling-appointment">here</a> to learn how to schedule an appointment.</p>
<h3 style="text-align: left;">Counseling Services</h3>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: medium;">Adult Counseling</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: medium;">Adolescent Counseling</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: medium;">Couples Counseling</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: medium;">Play Therapy</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: medium;">Sandtray Therapy</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: medium;">Group Counseling</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: medium;">Psychoeducational Assessment</span></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: left;"><strong>Adult &amp; Adolescent Counseling: </strong>Services include support counseling for personal growth as well as emotional challenges such as life transition, depression, anxiety, anger, relationship, self-esteem, and other concerns.</p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><strong>Child Counseling:</strong> Services include individual and group play therapy. <a title="Play Therapy" href="https://www.coe.unt.edu/child-and-family-resource-clinic/play-therapy">Learn more about play therapy...</a></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><strong>Parent Education:</strong> Services include parent education classes and individual parent education for purposes of improvement of parenting skills as well as the parent-child relationship. <a title="Parent Education" href="https://www.coe.unt.edu/child-and-family-resource-clinic/parent-education">Learn more about parent education services...</a></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><strong>Psychoeducational Assessment:</strong> Services include interdisciplinary assessment for children between the ages of 6 and 16 years old. Assessments evaluate a child's intellectual and learning abilities, speech and language concerns, and emotional or psychological challenges. <a title="Psychoeducational Assessment" href="https://www.coe.unt.edu/child-and-family-resource-clinic/psychoeducational-assessment">Learn more about psychoeducational assessment services...</a></p>
