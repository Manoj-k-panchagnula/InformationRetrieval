<h1>Apogee Stadium Alumni Pavilion</h1>

<p>The Alumni Pavilion, located near the northeast entrance to Apogee Stadium, is a pre-game meeting place for North Texas Alumni Association members and guests.</p>
<p>The pavilion was designed and constructed by the same people who built Apogee Stadium, award-winning architect HKS Inc. Architects and Manhattan Construction, using the same bricks used in the stadium. The 2,000-square-foot pavilion has room inside for 150, is equipped with big-screen televisions and has a 3,200-square-foot outdoor deck.</p>
