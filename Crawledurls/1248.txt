<!-- <a class="blue-button" href="/current">Current student?</a> --><!-- <a class="blue-button" href="/current">Current student?</a> --><h3>Know your college costs now</h3>
<p>The traditional tuition plan lets you know your tuition costs for four years so you can plan ahead.</p>
<ul>
<li>With the traditional plan, you will pay a 3.9 percent tuition increase from the 2014-15 tuition rate and then a 3.9 percent increase each year for the next three years.</li>
<li>The traditional plan does not offer the incentive of up to $3,000 for graduating on time but you may still be eligible for the <a href="https://registrar.unt.edu/graduation-and-diplomas/1000-tuition-rebate">statewide&nbsp;on-time $1,000 tuition rebate</a>.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>If you do not meet the <a href="https://registrar.unt.edu/transcripts-and-records/residency-information">Texas residency requirements</a>, you will be automatically enrolled in the traditional plan.</strong></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Eagle Express Plan vs. Traditional Plan</strong><br /> For New Freshmen<br /> (Total Academic Costs* by Year)</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">&nbsp;</p>
<div style="float: right; width: 300px; font-size: 13px; line-height: 15px; margin-left: 20px;">
<p style="margin-bottom: 0 !important;">*Total Academic Cost is based on taking 15 semester hours in the fall semester and 15 semester hours in the spring semester.</p>
<p> This includes:</p>
<ul>
<li>Board-designated tuition</li>
<li>State-mandated tuition</li>
<li>Mandatory fees</li>
<li>Course/instructional fees (averaged for traditional plan)</li>
</ul>
<div style="font-size: 11px;">
<p>**In your final 15 semester credit hours, the Eagle Express plan will save you $3,000. With the $1,000 on-time tuition rebate, you could save up to $4,000.</p>
</div>
</div>
<p><img src="/sites/default/files/u4/ee-traditional-line-graph.png" alt="Traditional Plan vs Eagle Express plan" width="574" height="398" /></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><em>In the traditional tuition plan, course and instructional fees are not included in the fixed increase. For comparison purposes, the chart above shows average course/instructional fees.</em></p>
<p>Students in the traditional tuition plan may be eligible for the&nbsp;<a href="https://registrar.unt.edu/graduation-and-diplomas/1000-tuition-rebate">statewide $1,000 on-time tuition rebate</a>.</p>
