              <div class="breadcrumb"><a href="/">Home</a> » <a href="/transcripts-and-records">Transcripts and Records</a> » <a href="/transcripts-and-records/order-transcript" class="active">Order a Transcript</a></div><h2>Order a Transcript</h2><div id="node-45" class="node clear-block">



  <div class="meta">
  
    </div>

  <div class="content">
    <p><strong>UNT offers three convenient ways for students to obtain or send official transcripts.</strong></p>
<h3>Introducing Online Transcript Ordering:</h3>
<p>&nbsp;<span style="color: #339966;"><em><strong>NEW!!</strong> </em></span>The University of North Texas has partnered with Credentials Inc. to offer online transcript ordering services. Current and former students may order transcripts 24/7 through this encrypted and secure process. Select the electronic transcript option to send your official record to any receiver with a valid email address! Receive real-time updates via email or text as your document reaches its destination in moments! Upload documents to accompany your official transcript through the TranscriptsPlus interface.&nbsp; Even select the mail option if your intended recipient requires a hard copy of your transcript.</p>
<p>This method is fast, secure and the most reliable way to order an official transcript. <span style="color: #339966;">This service has a charge of $10.00 per transcript ordered.</span></p>
<p>For more information on this new service, click <a href="UNT-order-transcript-online" title="UNT Ordering Transcripts Online">here</a>.</p>
<h3>Request Official Transcripts via form:</h3>
<p>Current and former students may complete a Transcript Request Form and submit it to the Office of the Registrar. Requests can be submitted via email, fax, or mail. These are processed during normal operating hours of the Office of the Registrar. Please allow seven business days for processing.</p>
<p><em><strong>PLEASE NOTE: Beginning September 1, 2016, all requests must be made online or in person.</strong> </em></p>
<p>For more information on this requesting via a form, click <a href="UNT-order-transcript-by-form" title="UNT Ordering Transcripts via Form">here</a>.</p>
<h3>Request Official Transcripts in person:</h3>
<p>Current and former students may visit the ESSC to pick an official transcript.&nbsp; This is a pickup service only, transcripts requested in person will not be mailed.</p>
<p>For more information on this service, including office hours, click <a href="UNT-order-transcript-in-person" title="UNT Ordering Transcripts In Person">here</a>&nbsp;or<strong>&nbsp;</strong>visit the <a href="transcripts-faq" target="_blank" title="UNT Transcript  - FAQ"><u>FAQ</u></a> page.</p>
<h3>Additional Resources</h3>
<p>For more information, contact the Registrar's Information Counter by calling 940-565-2111 or visiting Eagle Student Services Center (ESSC) Room 147.</p>
<p>For more information about transcripts, please see the Academics section of the <a href="http://catalog.unt.edu">UNT Catalog</a>.</p>
