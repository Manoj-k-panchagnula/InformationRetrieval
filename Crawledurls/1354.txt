<h2 style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;">GENERAL RESERVATION INFORMATION</span></strong></h2>
<h2 style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">UNT DEPARTMENTS (Campus)</h2>
<p style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">Facility reservations may be made in person in room 103&nbsp;of the Pohl Recreation Center (Rec Sports Main Office) or emailed to <a href="mailto:recres@unt.edu">recres@unt.edu</a>. The facility request form is available on-line at <a href="http://www.unt.edu/recsports/resources">www.unt.edu/recsports/resources</a>.</p>
<p style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><strong>**Requests must be submitted at least 1 week in advance**</strong></p>
<p></p>
<h2 style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">UNT STUDENT ORGANIZATIONS (Students)</h2>
<p style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">All student organizations must complete a campus event planner through their organization's OrgSync account by visiting the Student Organizations website at <a href="http://www.studentaffairs.unt.edu/student-organizations">www.studentaffairs.unt.edu/student-organizations</a>.</p>
<p style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><strong>**Requests should be made a minimum of 15 business days in advance**</strong></p>
<p style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"></p>
<h2 style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">NON-UNIVERSITY GROUPS (Off-campus)</h2>
<p style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">Non-university affiliated groups who wish to reserve space must call (940) 565-2656 to make the reservation. Reservations remain tentative pending receipt of deposit or receipt of a University account number.</p>
