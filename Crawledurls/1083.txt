<div class="singleCol large-12 columns"><h1 id="ContentPageName">Homecoming</h1>
<p>Homecoming is filled with tradition and excitement about the University of North Texas. Alumni, faculty, staff, students and friends come together to celebrate old friendships, make new memories and cheer on the Mean Green!</p>
<p>This year&#8217;s Homecoming is scheduled for the week of October 5 - 10, 2015, and will culminate with the Homecoming football game on October 10 versus Portland State. This year's theme,&#160; selected by students, is "Forever Mean Green: A Journey Through Time."&#160; </p>
<p>Highlight of the weeks activities for alumni include:</p>
<h4><strong>Thursday, October 8<br /></strong></h4>
<p><strong>Distinguished Alumni Achievement Awards<br /></strong>This an annual event that recognizes the outstanding achievement, service and support of UNT's alumni and friends.</p>
<h4><strong>&#160;</strong></h4>
<h4><strong>Friday, October 9<br /></strong></h4>
<p><strong>Alumni at the Bonfire<br /></strong>Join us for the best seats in the house! Watch Bonfire from the UNT Alumni Pavilion from 7:30-9:30 pm.</p>
<h4><strong>&#160;</strong></h4>
<h4><strong>Saturday, October 10<br /></strong></h4>
<p><strong><a href="http://www.untalumni.com/alumni/pages/alumni-events-golden-eagles-2015">Golden Eagles Reunion Breakfast</a> - Class of 1965<br /></strong>Gateway Ballroom<br />Celebrate your 50 year reunion! For more information, contact <a href="mailto:annie.carter@unt.edu">annie.carter@unt.edu</a> or 940-369-7001.</p>
<p><strong>Alumni GameDay Grille<br /></strong>For Homecoming, the Alumni Association encourages all alums to &#8220;come home&#8221; to UNT and join us at the Alumni Pavilion prior to the game. This weekend admission to the Pavilion is FREE for all. We will be open two hours prior to kickoff</p>
<p>&#160;</p>
<p>Some other helpful links for Homecoming specific activities include:</p>
<p>Tent and Tailgating Packages<br /><a href="http://www.meangreensports.com/homecomingtailgate/">http://www.meangreensports.com/homecomingtailgate/</a></p>
<p>Student Affairs Homecoming site<br /><a href="http://studentaffairs.unt.edu/homecoming">http://studentaffairs.unt.edu/homecoming#</a></p>
<p>&#160;</p>
