    <h4>Important Distinction between Graduating and Participating in Commencement Events</h4>
<p>Commencement events are the congratulatory ceremonies hosted by university officials in which students are publically and formally recognized. Graduation is the process of being awarded a degree after completion of credit hours and other degree requirements. Actual diplomas are not awarded at commencement ceremonies and participation in the ceremonies is not a guarantee of degree completion.</p>
<p>To officially graduate from UNT, a student must apply for graduation by the posted deadline. After receipt of final grades, the student's degree audit or degree plan must show the student satisfied all university, college and departmental requirements for the specific degree. A degree becomes official when it is posted to a student's transcript -- typically two to three weeks after the completion of their final term -- not by their participation in commencement exercises.&nbsp;</p>
<p>For more information about commencement events, please visit <a href="http://www.unt.edu/commencement">www.unt.edu/commencement</a>.&nbsp;</p>
<h3>Apply to Graduate</h3>
<p>All students must apply for graduation by the deadline. During the semester in which you intend to graduate, please submit your application through MyUNT by the deadline listed in the chart below.</p>
<p>In an effort to "go green" and streamline the application process for our graduates, the application for graduation is now online. To proceed with your graduation application <a href="http://registrar.unt.edu/sites/default/files/Online-Graduation-Flyer.pdf" target="_blank" title="Online Graduation Flyer">follow these instructions</a>:</p>
<ol>
<li><strong>Login to MyUNT and from the Student Center click on the "Apply for Graduation" link in the Other Academic dropdown.</strong> The "Apply for Graduation" link will be visible, even if you may not be eligible for graduation at this time. Please see your academic advising office for more information.</li>
<li><strong>Verify your program, degree, and major.</strong> If your specific program, degree, and/or major is not listed in MyUNT, contact your academic advising office.</li>
<li><strong>Select your anticipated graduation term.</strong> Only terms for which we are accepting applications will be displayed.</li>
<li><strong>Enter your name as you would like it to appear on your diploma and in the commencement program.</strong> Please double check for typos - your name will be printed exactly as you entered it.</li>
<li><strong>Verify/update your mailing address. </strong>This is the address that will be used to send you important graduation/commencement updates (pre-graduation), as well as to send your diploma (6-8 weeks after graduation).</li>
<li><strong>Check your UNT email for a confirmation that your application has been received.<br /></strong></li>
<li><strong>Check your UNT email frequently for updates as your application is reviewed and processed.</strong></li>
</ol>
<p>For more detailed instructions, <a href="https://registrar.unt.edu/sites/default/files/Online-Graduation-Student-Documentation_1.pdf" target="_blank">click here</a>.</p>
<h4></h4>
<h3>Important Deadlines</h3>
<p><a href="http://www.unt.edu/commencement">Click here&nbsp;for information about the ceremonies.</a></p>
<p>Please review the below information to determine when you should apply for graduation.</p>
<h4>Undergraduate students</h4>
<ul>
<li><b>If you anticipate graduating in Spring 2016 with an undergraduate degree and this is your last semester at UNT. </b>If you plan to graduate in Spring 2016, the deadline to apply online for Spring 2016 graduation is Monday, <b><u>February 15, 2016</u></b>.&nbsp;</li>
<li><b>If you anticipate graduating in Summer 2016 with an undergraduate degree and will enroll in all your remaining courses in Summer 2016</b>.&nbsp; If you are planning to graduate in Summer 2016 and wish to participate in the Spring 2016 commencement ceremony, please apply for Summer 2016 graduation by the above deadline <b><u>&nbsp;(Monday, February 15, 2016).</u></b></li>
</ul>
<h4>Graduate students</h4>

<ul>
<li><b>If you anticipate graduating Spring 2016 with&nbsp;a Doctoral&nbsp;degree. </b>Doctoral candidates must have successfully defended their dissertation no later than April 15<sup>th</sup>. Please note that paper submissions are due ealier than this date. Check for specific deadlines related to the completion of your graduate degree at <a href="http://tsgs.unt.edu/new-current-students/graduation-information">http://tsgs.unt.edu/new-current-students/graduation-information.</a></li>
<li><b>If you anticipate graduating Spring 2016 with&nbsp;a&nbsp;Masters&nbsp;degree</b>. Master’s students must have completed their programs by the end of the Spring 2016 semester.</li>
<li>Graduation applications for master's and doctoral degrees are processed through Toulouse Graduate School. For further information regarding graduation applications and processes call <strong>940-565-2383</strong>&nbsp;or visit&nbsp;<a href="http://www.tsgs.unt.edu/" style="font-size: 12px;">tsgs.unt.edu</a>.</li>
</ul>
