<h3>Mission</h3>
<p>The University of North Texas System is committed to supporting the Historically<br />
Underutilized Business (HUB) Program established by the State of Texas.</p>
<p>The UNT System HUB Area is always available and ready to assist department representatives with locating a qualified and responsive HUB vendor or to assist a HUB-eligible vendor to acquire HUB certification.</p>
