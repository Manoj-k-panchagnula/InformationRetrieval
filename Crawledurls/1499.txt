              <h3 class="block-title">We are big and bold.</h3>
            
    <div class="content clearfix">
      <p>UNT is big in every way, from our size to our impact. Founded in 1890, today we are one of the nation’s largest universities. We are the choice of 37,000 students, and we give them the knowledge and know-how to shape their lives, their workplaces and their communities for the better. Our students are at the heart of a growing public research university that fuels the intellectual and economic vitality of one of the most dynamic, fastest-growing regions in the United States. With their UNT education and all of the excellence that comes with it, our students and alumni are the leaders, doers and creative thinkers of today and tomorrow.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>  </div>
</div><div class="grid-12 region region-content-single" id="region-content-single">

    <section class="block block-block block-55 block-block-55 odd" id="block-block-55">
  <div class="block-inner clearfix">
              <h3 class="block-title">Big size, big impact.</h3>
            
    <div class="content clearfix">
      <p>We thrive through the diversity of our campus community, which includes people of different social, ethnic, cultural and religious backgrounds. UNT is home to talented students of every kind, from the first-time college student to the working parent to the military veteran.</p>

<ul><li>UNT has been named a <a href="http://news.unt.edu/news-releases/unt-again-ranked-best-west-princeton-review">Best in the West</a> college by <em>The Princeton Review</em> for six years, offering excellent academic programs and campus experiences.</li>
</ul><h4>Alumni Strong</h4>

<p>UNT’s <a href="http://www.untalumni.com">alumni network</a> includes 361,000 members, with 237,000 alumni who live in the Dallas-Fort Worth area. And last year, 8,700 students graduated from UNT with the power to turn their Mean Green educations into golden opportunities.</p>

<h4>Economic Driver</h4>

<p>The North Texas region includes one of the nation’s largest metropolitan areas and most vibrant economies. UNT, the flagship of the <a href="http://untsystem.edu">UNT System</a>, boosts the region’s economic activity by $1.7 billion annually.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</section><div class="block block-block block-56 block-block-56 even block-without-title" id="block-block-56">
  <div class="block-inner clearfix">
                
    <div class="content clearfix">
      <div class="equal-height-element">
<h4>Student Focused</h4>

<p>We have 4,000 dedicated faculty and staff members focused on helping students succeed. Our faculty include distinguished researchers, scholars and artists who are mentors, innovators and leaders.</p>
</div>
    </div>
  </div>

      <div class="equal-height-element">
<h4>Diverse</h4>

<p>UNT is one of the nation’s most diverse universities with 7,100 Hispanic students and 5,000 African American students.</p>

<h4>Global Reach</h4>

<p>We host about 2,500 <a href="http://international.unt.edu">international students</a>, who come from 129 countries.</p>

      <h4>Big and Bright</h4>

<p>The average SAT score of an entering freshman in Fall 2014 was 1107, higher than the Texas average of 971 and the national average of 1010.</p>

<p>And with 2,000 students, UNT’s <a href="http://honors.unt.edu">Honors College</a> is the largest in the North Texas region.</p>
