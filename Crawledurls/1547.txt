	<h1>
	<strong>Union Fest 2016: </strong>Welcome Home!</h1>

<hr style="color: rgb(90, 90, 90); font-size: 14px;" />
<h2>
	Join us at the Union on Thursday, April 14<span style="top: -0.5em; line-height: 0; font-size: 15px; vertical-align: baseline; position: relative;">th</span><span style="top: -0.5em; line-height: 0; font-size: 15px; vertical-align: baseline; position: relative;">&nbsp;</span>for giveaways, fun and games, music, food, and more!</h2>

<p style="margin: 0px 20px; color: rgb(90, 90, 90); font-size: 14px;">
	On April&nbsp;14, the University Union is hosting the annual celebration of the Union, Union Fest. The tradition of Union Fest began in 1976 with the completion of the renovated Union. Staff and employees spend weeks planning the celebration. They line up vendors, activities and giveaways to make sure it&rsquo;s an epic celebration of the Union that the entire campus can enjoy. This year marks the 40th celebration and is special as it is the first&nbsp;Union Fest in the brand new building!</p>

<p style="color: rgb(90, 90, 90); font-size: 14px;">
