              <h3 class="block-title">We are excellence realized.</h3>
            
    <div class="content clearfix">
      <p><span style="line-height: 1.6em;">As a public research university growing toward the top tier, we are focused on creating tomorrow’s knowledge. Our students excel as teachers, artists, engineers and everything in between. They learn in creative environments that combine academic excellence, hands-on experience and thought-provoking research to spark their imaginations and fill their minds. We give students an educational experience that challenges and enriches them, ensuring they have the power to compete with anyone, anywhere.</span></p>

	<li>LEADER: One of the largest providers of <a href="http://www.unt.edu/distance-learning.htm">online credit courses</a> among Texas public universities, UNT is a leader in high-quality online education.</li>
