  <div class="meta">
  
    </div>

  <div class="content">
    <p>The Summer semester consists of seven sessions included within one term. Summer sessions are grouped around three common beginning dates that fall within three common registration periods and payment deadline and grade reporting periods.</p>
<p><strong>View academic dates sorted by session:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/registration/summer-3w1-8w1-and-sum-sessions">Summer 3W1, 8W1, and SUM Sessions</a></li>
<li><a href="/registration/summer-5w1-10w-and-AOP-sessions">Summer 5W1, 10W, and 8W2 Sessions</a></li>
<li><a href="/registration/summer-5w2-session">Summer 5W2 Session</a></li>
</ul>
<p style="font-style: normal; font-size: 12px; font-family: Verdana, Geneva, sans-serif;"><strong>Bill Distribution/Payment Arrangements:&nbsp;</strong><span style="font-style: normal; font-size: 12px; font-family: Verdana, Geneva, sans-serif;">Pay fees through EIS online self-service at&nbsp;</span><a href="http://my.unt.edu" target="_blank" style="font-style: normal; font-size: 12px; font-family: Verdana, Geneva, sans-serif; line-height: normal;">my.unt.edu</a><span style="font-style: normal; font-size: 12px; font-family: Verdana, Geneva, sans-serif;">&nbsp;or go to the Eagle Student Services Center for schedule/bill print.</span></p>
<p><a href="/registration/schedule-of-classes">Schedule of Classes</a> information is available online. Departments may begin advising and issuing advising clearance forms for Summer term.</p>
<p><strong>Now Available</strong>: <a href="http://vsb.unt.edu">Visual Schedule Builder</a>&nbsp;- A&nbsp;<span style="font-size: 12px; line-height: 1.5;">tool designed to assist students in planning their academic schedule. Visual Schedule Builder allows students to graphically view schedule options based on the courses and offering times that works best for them. For additional information please read the <a href="/sites/default/files/UNT_Visual_Schedule_Builder_help.pdf">help documentation</a>.</span></p>
