    <p><span style="font-size: large; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><strong>Welcome to IITS!</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: small;">Instructional Information Technology Services is a division of University Information Technology and provides a variety of computing services to the academic community.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><strong><span style="font-size: medium;">Team</span></strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><a href="mailto:ehinkle@unt.edu">Elizabeth Hinkle-Turner</a>, D.M.A., director,&nbsp;940-565-4808</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><a href="mailto:cdts@unt.edu">Ray Banks</a>, manager, <a href="http://it.unt.edu/ctds">Classroom and Testing Desktop Services</a>,&nbsp;940-369-6051</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><a href="mailto:joann.luksich@unt.edu">JoAnn Luksich</a>, manager, <a href="http://it.unt.edu/datamanage" title="Link to Data Management Services Office page">Data Management Services</a>,&nbsp;940-369-7416</span></p>
<h3><strong><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: medium;">Divisions</span></strong></h3>
<h3><strong style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: small;">Classroom and Testing Desktop Services</strong></h3>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><a href="http://it.unt.edu/ctds">Classroom and Testing Desktop Services</a>&nbsp;team members support the goals of the university by facilitating a technical environment that enhances learning, research and testing through the use of technology. The CTDS team members provide technical management for the <a href="http://it.unt.edu/test">Sage Hall Testing Center</a> and manage four student-computing classrooms located at Discovery Park.&nbsp;</span></p>
<h3><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: small;"><strong>Data Management Services</strong></span></h3>
<p><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"><a href="http://it.unt.edu/datamanage">Data Management Services</a>&nbsp;is responsible for research data entry and automated scanning services for test scoring and course evaluations.</span>&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: medium; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><strong>Mission</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"></span><span style="font-size: small; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">The mission of Instructional Information Technology Services is to deliver IT services and infrastructure in support of instruction and learning within a framework of communication, integrity and accountability.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">The IITS Division team members serve students, faculty and staff</span></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">as a first point of contact for computing use on campus;</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">provide an enterprise IT help desk service;</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">operate the General Access and Adaptive Technology Lab located in Sage Hall, Room 167;</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">furnish considerable documentation and instruction on information technology and educational applications;</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">offer exam processing and grading and research data compilation services to faculty;</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">administer computer classroom facilities, computer-based testing facilities, resource management services, and strategic research and instructional software licensing and access;&nbsp;and</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">support a superior level of high performance research computing infrastructure and data visualization technologies.&nbsp;</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">To accomplish this mission, members of the IITS team employ knowledgeable and professionally active research and technical consultants who have an effective combination of academic experience and technical expertise.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">Please address your questions about this page to: </span><a href="mailto:baczewski@unt.edu" style="font-size: small; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">Philip Baczewski</a><span style="font-size: small;">, senior director, University Information Technology, email:&nbsp;</span><a href="mailto:baczewski@unt.edu" style="font-size: small; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">baczewski@unt.edu</a><span style="font-size: small;">, 940-565-3886.</span></span></p>
