<div class="singleCol large-12 columns"><h1>Regional Alumni Receptions</h1>
<p><img alt="" height="17" src="https://www.untalumni.com/image/alumni/bkgd_pagetitle.gif" width="730" /></p>
<p>Whether you graduated forty years ago or yesterday, you will always be an integral part of the Mean Green family. Stay connected to your alma mater by joining us at one of the upcoming UNT Alumni Regional Receptions. The programs listed below are the first of several upcoming alumni events in the North Texas area. Select the event that best fits your schedule and location. These events were made to give you the opportunity to reconnect with other UNT alumni in your area for networking, University updates and fun.<br /><br /><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>Dallas County</strong></span><br /><strong>October 1, 2015 from 6:30-8:30 pm</strong><br />Belo Mansion &amp; Pavilion, Dallas, TX<br /><br />With more than 36,000 alumni in Dallas county, we will be painting the town Green! Plan to attend our UNT Alumni Fall Reception - Dallas County at the Belo Mansion &amp; Pavilion on Thursday, October 1, 2015.&#160; President Smatresk will give a brief update on the University as we dine on heavy hors d&#8217;oeurves and drinks in the elegant ballroom at the Belo Mansion.<br /><br /><strong>RSVP below by September 28</strong></p>
<p><br /><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>Collin County</strong></span><br /><strong>October 13, 2015 from 6:30-8:30 pm</strong><br />Gleneagles County Club, Plano, TX<br /><br />Collin county boasts more than 20,000 proud UNT alumni. Join us for a night of networking at the picturesque Gleneagles County Club on Tuesday, October 13, 2015. A brief University update will be given by President Smatresk as we nosh on plentiful appetizers and drinks.<br /><br /><strong>RSVP below by October 11</strong><br /><br /></p>
</div>
