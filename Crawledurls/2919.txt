      <h4 class="green"><strong>OVerview</strong></h4>
      <p>UNT is engaged in a comprehensive master plan update effort, the aim of which is to refine/refresh the vision for the future of our campus.&nbsp; The update to the 2005 master plan will provide a direction for supporting the vision, mission and goals for the University while accommodating the projected increases in enrollment. As we move through this process, we ask that all members of the extended university community share their best ideas for the future of our campus.</p>
