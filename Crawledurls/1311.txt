<h3>Tickets</h3>
<p>Admission to North Texas swimming and diving meets is free and open to the public.</p>

<h3>Pohl Recreation Center and PEB Natatorium</h3>
<p>The home of the Mean Green swimming and diving team is located north of the Super Pit on Chestnut Street, spanning the block from North Texas Boulevard to Avenue D, and its distinctive design has been the subject of articles in Texas Architect and Mondo Track & Field.</p>
<p>The massive 138,000-square-foot, two-story building has an eight-lane pool with bleachers for fans and an electronic scoreboard. The facility is also equipped with a six-lane 25-meter pool for training and warm-ups. Adjadent to the Recreation Center is the PEB Natatorium, which houses the diving facilities: two one-meter springboards and a three-meter springboard. From May to October, North Texas also uses the outdoor long-course pool at TWU, three miles from campus. <a class="mainlink" href="http://www.meangreenmap.com/pohl.html" target="_blank">Click here for more information on the Pohl Recreation Center and PEB Natatorium</a>.</p>

<h3>Mean Green Club</h3>
<p>The Mean Green Club raises funds for North Texas athletic scholarships. Donations to the Mean Green Club help support nearly 350 North Texas student-athletes each year and provide them with the best collegiate experience possible.</p>
<p>Members receive exclusive access to North Texas athletics and year-long benefits such as invitations to member-only events, the best parking and seating available, and more.
<p>For more information or to join Mean Green Club, call us at (940) 369-7284, email us at meangreenclub@unt.edu, or <a class="mainlink" href="http://www.meangreensports.com/sports/mg-club/spec-rel/ways-to-give.html" target="_blank">click here to visit us on the web</a>.</p>
