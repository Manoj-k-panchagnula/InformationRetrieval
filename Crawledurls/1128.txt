<h3>Welcome!</h3>
<p><strong>EPSY Office of Family Science </strong>facilitates collaborative and interdisciplinary family science research and training projects by faculty and students across the UNT community. &nbsp;The goal of the OFSERP is to meet the needs of students, professionals and families through research, training, consultation and policy development resources.​&nbsp;</p>
<p>OFS is a unit of the <a href="http://www.coe.unt.edu/educational-psychology" target="_blank">Department of Educational Psychology</a> (EPSY) in the UNT <a href="http://www.coe.unt.edu/" target="_blank">College of Education</a>.</p>
<p>OFSERP assists in the establishment of partnerships with parents, schools, and agencies through research and training. Some of the Office's ongoing activities include:</p>
<ul class="welcome">
<li><a href="https://parenteducation.unt.edu/conferences">International Conference on Parent Education and Parenting</a></li>
<li><a href="https://parenteducation.unt.edu/online-learning">Continuing Education through Online Training</a></li>
<li><a href="https://parenteducation.unt.edu/roper/parent-educator-database">Database Registry for Parent Educators</a></li>
<li><a href="https://parenteducation.unt.edu/recognition-system">Professional Development Recognition System</a></li>
<li><a href="http://www.facebook.com/pages/UNT-Center-For-Parent-Education">Resource Library</a></li>
</ul>
