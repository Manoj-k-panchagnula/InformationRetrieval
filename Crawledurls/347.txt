			<h2 class="title" id="page-title">I&#039;m accepted! Now what do I do?</h2>
			
			        
                <div class="block block-system block-main block-system-main odd block-without-title" id="block-system-main">
  <div class="block-inner clearfix">
                
    <div class="content clearfix">
      <div  about="/freshman/next-steps-for-admitted-students" typeof="sioc:Item foaf:Document" id="node-page-218" class="ds-1col node node-page view-mode-full node-not-promoted node-not-sticky author-wdc odd clearfix clearfix">

  
  <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"><h3 class="sub-title">Next steps for admitted freshmen</h3>

<h4>Congratulations on being accepted to UNT!</h4>

<p>If you have questions about what you should do between receiving your acceptance letter and attending your first day of classes, don't worry, we have you covered.</p>

<p>The first day of class for the Fall 2016 semester is <strong>August 29</strong>.</p>

<p>Don't forget to:</p>

<ul>
  <li><a href="/freshman/how-to-apply#transcripts">Send us your final transcript.</a></li>
  <li>Accept your admission by logging into <a href="https://my.unt.edu" target="_blank" title="MyUNT (opens in a new window)">my.unt.edu</a> and clicking on the "accept" link in your Student Center. </li>
  <li>Let us know as soon as possible which semester you're planning on attending UNT to avoid delays in processing your financial aid awards. <a href="http://financialaid.unt.edu/howto/How-to-complete-Expected-Enrollment" target="_blank" title="Expected enrollment verification instructions (opens in a new window)">Learn how to complete the Expected Enrollment Form.</a></li>

  <li>Tell us if you have a disability for which you will need assistance or accommodations. <a href="http://disability.unt.edu/services/apply" target="_blank" title="Apply for disability services (opens in a new window)">Request assistance for special needs.</a></li>

</ul>

<hr />

<a id="orientation"></a>

<h3>Attend New Student Orientation</h3>

<p>Once you are accepted, you will be provided with information about orientation for new students. Orientation is your first step in successfully transitioning to UNT. All new undergraduate students are required to attend an orientation session.</p>

<p>Orientation sessions for new students enrolling in the Fall semester are scheduled throughout the Summer. Freshmen Summer orientation is a 3-day experience. Sessions for new students enrolling in the Spring semester are 1-day sessions scheduled in December and early January.</p>

<p>At orientation, you will:</p>

<ul>

  <li>meet with your academic advisor, who will help you prepare to register for classes</li>

  <li>get with a class schedule for the upcoming semester</li>

  <li>learn about academic and campus resources</li>

  <li>meet with faculty and staff from your college or school</li>

  <li>start building relationships with other students</li>

  <li>learn about UNT's traditions</li>

</ul>

<p>Register for orientation through your <a href="https://my.unt.edu/" target="_blank" title="MyUNT student account portal">MyUNT account</a>. Sessions fill up quickly, so register as early as you can.</p>

<p><a href="http://studentaffairs.unt.edu/orientation" target="_blank" title="UNT Orientation website (opens in a new window)">Learn more about orientation and how to register.</a></p>

<hr />

<h3>Participate in New Student Events</h3>

<p>Connect with other students, prepare for life at UNT and have fun with welcome events designed just for you.</p>

<h4>Eagle Camp</h4>

<p><a href="http://studentaffairs.unt.edu/eagle-camp" target="_blank" title="UNT Eagle Camp (opens in a new window)">Eagle Camp</a> is an optional next step that welcomes the entering students to UNT each year with the purpose of giving them an opportunity to have fun, make friends, and learn more about life at UNT. Eagle Camp is open to any new students who will be attending UNT in the fall.</p>
<h4>First Flight Week and Spring Soar</h4>

<p><a href="http://studentaffairs.unt.edu/first-flight-week" target="_blank" title="First Flight Week (opens in a new window)">First Flight Week</a> is UNT's official welcome program for incoming students. It takes place before the fall semester. Spring Soar is an extension of First Flight that takes place before to the spring semester. This week is designed to help new students begin their college experience on the right foot. From programs that prepare you for the upcoming educational opportunities to programs where you get to let loose and meet fellow peers, there is something for everyone! First Flight is mandatory for all new freshmen.</p>

<h4>UNT Premiere Weekend</h4>

<p><a href="http://premiere.unt.edu/" target="_blank" title="UNT Premiere (opens in a new window)">UNT Premiere Weekend</a> is a chance for incoming freshmen to get an insider's view of college life on campus and in Denton. Hands-on activities will expose you to new areas of UNT's campus while also providing opportunities to meet other new students in small group settings.</p>

<hr />

<h3>Tour Campus</h3>

<p>If you haven't visited UNT yet, now's the perfect time to take a student-led tour of our beautiful campus. Let UNT's knowledgeable Eagle Ambassadors show you around campus, then take an individualized tour of one of our colleges or schools. </p>

<p><a href="http://tours.unt.edu/" target="_blank" title="UNT Tours (opens in a new window)">Sign up for a tour.</a></p>

<hr />
<a id="tsi"></a>

<h3>Take the TSI Assessment</h3>

<p>UNT requires all new students to take the Texas Success Initiative (TSI) Assessment before registering for classes, unless they qualify for an exemption. This assessment measures college-level readiness in reading, mathematics and writing. The TSI Assessment is not used in admissions decisions at UNT, but is necessary for adequate preregistration advising. For  more information on the TSI Assessment, please visit <a href="http://learningcenter.unt.edu/TexasSuccessInitiative" target="_blank" title="The UNT Learning Center's Texas Success Initiative (TSI) information page (opens in a new window)">UNT Learning Center's TSI web page</a> or call at 940-369-7006.</p>


<hr />

<h3>Sign Up for Housing and Meal Plans</h3>

<p><strong>Freshman On-Campus Living Requirement</strong> - Most incoming freshman students are required to reside in university operated residence halls. <a href="http://housing.unt.edu/oncampus_housing_requirement" target="_blank" title="UNT's freshman on-campus living requirement information">Read more about the freshman on-campus living requirement.</a></p>

<p><a href="http://housing.unt.edu/housing_guide" title="UNT Housing Guide (opens in a new window)" target="_blank">See the UNT housing guide for  information about residence halls, move-in dates and how to apply for housing.</a></p>

<p><a href="https://offcampushousing.unt.edu/" title="UNT Off-Campus Housing website (opens in a new window)" target="_blank">Find off-campus housing.</a></p>

<p><a href="http://www.dining.unt.edu/" title="UNT Dining Services website (opens in a new window)" target="_blank">See campus dining options.</a></p>

<p><a href="http://www.dining.unt.edu/residentmealplans" title="UNT Dining Services resident student meal plan information (opens in a new window)" target="_blank">Get information about meal plans for campus residents</a>.</p>


<hr />

<h3>Meet UNT's Health and Immunization Requirements</h3>

<p>All new students under the age of 22 years old must show an immunization record with proof of receiving a recent bacterial meningitis vaccination dose or booster, unless an exemption is sought and granted.</p>

<p>Also, incoming international students may be required to have a recent tuberculosis (TB) screening.</p>

<p><a href="http://studentaffairs.unt.edu/immunization-requirements" target="_blank" title="UNT immunization requirements (opens in a new window)">Learn more about meningitis immunization requirements, recommended vaccinations, tuberculosis screenings and how to submit medical documents to UNT.</a></p>

<p>Students needing immunizations or health screenings may schedule an appointment with the <a href="http://studentaffairs.unt.edu/student-health-and-wellness-center">UNT Student Health and Wellness Center</a> by calling 940-565-2333. </p>

<hr />

<h3>Apply to the Honors College</h3>

<p>The Honors College at UNT provides unique learning and living opportunities for talented, driven students who want more out of their university experience. <a href="http://honors.unt.edu/eligibility-and-membership-requirements" title="UNT Honors College eligibility requirements (opens in a new window)" target="_blank">See the Honors eligibility requirements</a>.</p>

<p><a href="http://honors.unt.edu/advantage" title="UNT Honors College information (opens in a new window)" target="_blank">Learn the benefits of UNT Honors College membership and how to apply.</a></p>



<hr />

<h3>Connect With Us</h3>

<p>Join the conversation on <a href="http://facebook.com/northtexas" title="UNT on Facebook (opens in a new window)" target="_blank">UNT's Facebook</a> or one of our other <a href="http://social.unt.edu/" title="UNT social media channels (opens in a new window)" target="_blank">social media channels</a>.</p>

<p>Once you&rsquo;re admitted to UNT, <a href="http://unt.schoolsapp.com/" title="UNT's private social network (opens in a new window)" target="_blank">join UNT&rsquo;s private social network</a> so you can make friends before setting foot on campus. Connect to others by major, hometown, residence hall or organization.</p>

<hr />

<h3>Know Where to Go</h3>

<p>It's a good idea to find out where your classes are so you make a great first impression with your professors.</p>

<p><a href="http://maps.unt.edu/" title="UNT Maps (opens in a new window)" target="_blank">See a map of campus.</a></p>


<hr />

<h3>Learn More About...</h3>

<ul>

<li>The <a href="http://success.unt.edu/" title="Succeed at UNT(opens in a new window)" target="_blank">Succeed at UNT program</a> designed to help you define your future and take control of your success</a></li>

<li><a href="http://studentaffairs.unt.edu/off-campus-student-services" title="Student services for off-campus, commuter and non-traditional students (opens in a new window)" target="_blank">UNT services for off-campus, commuter and non-traditional students</a></li>

<li><a href="https://studyabroad.admin.unt.edu/" title="UNT Study Abroad (opens in a new window)" target="_blank">Study Abroad</a> for global learning and experience opportunities</li>

<li>Ordering textbooks and gear from the <a href="http://unt.bncollege.com/" title="UNT Bookstore (opens in a new window)" target="_blank">UNT Bookstore</a></li>

<li><a href="http://www.discoverdenton.com/" title="DiscoverDenton.com (opens in a new window)" target="_blank">Denton, Texas</a></li>


</ul></div></div></div></div>
