              <div class="breadcrumb"><a href="/">Home</a> » <a href="/veterans" class="active">Veterans and Military</a></div><h2>Information for Veterans and Military</h2><div id="node-71" class="node clear-block">



  <div class="meta">
  
    </div>

  <div class="content">
    <p><img src="/sites/default/files/2011-best-for-vets.gif" alt="UNT Named a Best for Vets College in 2011" title="UNT Named a Best for Vets College in 2011" align="right" width="99" height="99" />The UNT Veterans’ Certification-Registrar’s Office proudly serves over 1100 service members, Veterans, and their families. The services we provide ensure that eligible UNT students receive their Federal educational benefits by submitting certifications of enrollment to the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs on behalf of the veterans. We have combined forces with the UNT Veterans’ Center and the Hazlewood representatives to create a new office: UNT Student Veteran Services (SVS). The goal of the SVS is to provide our military veterans with a “one-stop shop” for getting help with achieving their educational goals. Veterans will be able to apply for benefits, request enrollment certification, apply for Hazlewood, and interact with other veterans at the SVS.</p>
<p>The SVS is located in Sage Hall, Room 123 and will handle all of the Veterans educational benefits:</p>
<ul>
<li>Montgomery GI Bill® – Chapter 30</li>
<li>Vocational Rehabilitation and Employment Program – Chapter 31</li>
<li>Post 9/11 GI Bill<span>®</span> – Chapter 33</li>
<li>Survivors and dependents education – Chapter 35</li>
<li>Selected Reserve GI Bill<span>®</span> – Chapter 1606</li>
<li>Reserve Educational Assistance Program – Chapter 1607</li>
<li>Hazlewood Exemption</li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: 12px; line-height: 1.5;">The Registrar's Office is also responsible for enrollment certification and notifying the regional VA office of student schedule changes. To speak with someone in the UNT Veteran's Program Office please call 940-369-8021. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12px; line-height: 1.5;"><span style="font-size: 12px; line-height: 1.5;">For more informaton about specific services, p</span>lease visit the <strong><a href="http://registrar.unt.edu/veterans/faq">Frequently Asked Questions</a></strong> page and visit the UNT Veterans Services website: </span><a href="http://www.unt.edu/veterans" style="font-size: 12px; line-height: 1.5;">www.unt.edu/veterans</a><span style="font-size: 12px; line-height: 1.5;">.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12px; line-height: 1.5;">You can contact the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs Office by calling 1-888-GIBILL-1 (1-888-442-4551). Their website is </span><a href="http://www.gibill.va.gov/" style="font-size: 12px; line-height: 1.5;">www.gibill.va.gov</a><span style="font-size: 12px; line-height: 1.5;">.</span></p>
<h3>Students Called to Active Duty</h3>
<p>For policy information concerning students called to active duty, please see the Academics section of the <a href="http://catalog.unt.edu">UNT Catalog</a>.</p>
<h3>Additional Resources</h3>
<p>For questions about financial aid and scholarships for veterans, contact the <strong>UNT Office of Financial Aid and Scholarships</strong> by calling 940-565-2302, dropping by their offices in the Eagle Student Services Center (ESSC) or visiting their website at <a href="http://veteranscenter.unt.edu">veteranscenter.unt.edu</a>.</p>
