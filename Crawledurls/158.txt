    <h2 id="exploreCareerOpportunities"><a href="http://www.texascaresonline.com/wowmenu.asp">Explore Career Opportunities</a></h2><div class="field-section-body"><p>Explore opportunities based on your interests and work values, match characteristics of your current or most recent occupation with similar occupations, and find specific information such as the fastest growing jobs, levels of education and training requirements, and average salaries.</p>
<ul class="links"><li><a href="http://www.texascaresonline.com/wowmenu.asp">Explore careers using Texas <acronym title="Career Alternatives Resource Evaluation System">CARES</acronym></a></li>
</ul></div></div>
<div class="content-section-primary entity entity-field-collection-item field-collection-item-field-content-section even" about="/field-collection/field-content-section/131" typeof="">
    <h2 id="evaluateExpensesAgainstCareers"><a href="http://www.texasrealitycheck.com/">Evaluate Expenses Against Careers</a></h2><div class="field-section-body"><p>Estimate how much money you need for housing, utilities, transportation, clothes, health care, entertainment and other expenses, and see which careers will pay for these needs.</p>
<ul class="links"><li><a href="http://www.texasrealitycheck.com/">Compare careers &amp; expenses using Reality Check</a></li>
</ul></div></div>

</ul></div></div>
