              <h2>West Hall</h2><div id="node-363" class="node clear-block">



  <div class="meta">
      <span class="submitted">Submitted by Jonathan Piott on Tue, 2011-11-15 16:46</span>
  
    </div>

  <div class="content">
    <h3>Overview</h3><p><img alt="West Hall" src="/sites/default/files/pdf/West.jpg" width="665" height="250" /></p><p></p><p>Located at the intersection of North Texas Boulevard and Chestnut Street, West Hall is one of UNT’s “economy halls” that houses freshmen students — men in north tower and women in south tower. West is known for its unique mixture of residents who have an abundance of hall pride.</p><p>Offering both double and triple rooms, residents at West enjoy spacious living quarters with lofted beds and couches. There is also a newly renovated large lobby with pool tables and big screen television that is a popular hangout, as well as an on-site cafeteria.</p><h3>Why We Love This Hall!</h3><ul><li>Double occupancy&nbsp;rooms and triple occupancy rooms</li><li>Community baths—students don’t have to clean!</li><li>Ping pong and pool tables</li><li>Computer lab, wireless internet available building-wide (including courtyard)</li><li>Large lobby and study room</li><li>Kitchen for student use</li><li>Centralized laundry facility</li><li>On-site cafeteria - <a href="http://www.dining.unt.edu/westhall">West Cafeteria</a></li><li>Close proximity to Pohl Recreation Center and PEB</li></ul><h3>Room Interior</h3><p><img alt="West interior" src="/sites/default/files/images/West%20Interior.png" width="433" height="289" /></p><h3>Suite Floor Plan</h3><p><img src="/sites/default/files/pdf/West-Suites.jpg" alt="floor plan" width="372" height="263" /></p><p></p><p></p><!--<h3 style="text-align: center;">West 360 Degree Double Room View&nbsp;</h3><div align="center" style="border: solid 2px #453f26; width: 520; height: 380; margin: auto; background-color: #e2e2bf; color: #453f26;">Click the mouse and drag to view the room.<br /><embed controller="true" width="500" height="350" src="/sites/default/files/pdf/c.westdouble.mov" /></div><p></p><h3 style="text-align: center;">West 360 Degree Triple Room View&nbsp;</h3><div align="center" style="border: solid 2px #453f26; width: 520; height: 380; margin: auto; background-color: #e2e2bf; color: #453f26;">Click the mouse and drag to view the room.<br /><embed controller="true" width="500" height="350" src="/sites/default/files/pdf/c.westtriple.mov" />-->  </div>
