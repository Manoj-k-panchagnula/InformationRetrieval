	<h2>
		<br />
		<br />
		<span style="line-height: 1.1; font-size: 18px;">Large Format Printing</span></h2>

	<p>
		Our printers are HP Design Jets - 600dpi capability. We print with UV ink. Design Works has several different options for large format printing.</p>

	<h4>
		Paper Options and Maximum Sizes</h4>

	<ul>
		<li>
			Regular paper - Heavy weight paper (35lb weight) up to 59&rdquo; wide</li>
		<li>
			Semi Gloss Paper - Heavy weight paper with a satin finish up to 41&rdquo; wide</li>
		<li>
			Glossy Paper - Heavy weight paper with a high gloss up to 41&rdquo; wide</li>
		<li>
			Outdoor Material &ndash; up to 41&ldquo; wide vinyl scrim, waterproof, tear resistant, fade resistant</li>
	</ul>

	<p>
		&nbsp;</p>

	<p>
		*Samples of each paper are available at the Design Works office.</p>

	<p>
		Large format printing is charged by the square inch.</p>

	<h4>
		Setting up the print job yourself?</h4>

	<p>
		Design Works is not responsible for spelling errors or color matching from outside files. A one inch color test strip is recommended. Design Works can print from many different graphics and word processing programs - please call ahead to check on compatibility.</p>

	<p>
		Remember files downloaded from the web, when enlarged, will become fuzzy or blurred. We do not recommend their use in large format printing.</p>

	<p>
		Design Works&#39;s turnaround time depends on work load. Design Works requires at least one week (five working days) on large print orders or print order requiring setup. Please let us know when you need the job done when you are placing the order.</p>

	<p>
		<a href="#">back to top</a></p>

	<h2 id="b">
		Balloons</h2>

	<h4>
		Latex Balloons</h4>

	<p>
		We have 11&quot; latex balloons in the primary (red, yellow, blue) and secondary (green, orange, purple) colors - also available in black, white, lime green and pastel colors (please call for pastel color availability). Latex balloons have a life span of 8-12 hours.</p>

	<h4>
		Mylar Balloons</h4>

	<p>
		We have mylar balloons containing various designs for special occasions (birthday, get well, congratulations, we&#39;ll miss you, good luck, we appreciate you, I love you, etc.). We also have mylar balloons in several solid colors (please call for availability). Mylar balloons have a life span of 2-4 days.</p>

	<h4>
		Specialty Balloons</h4>

	<p>
		We have a selection of specialty mylar balloons that have various designs and shapes.</p>

	<p>
		Please come by Design Works office to see different balloon choices.</p>

	<p>
		Banners are printed from our large format 600 dpi printers. The typical banner size is 8&#39; x 3&#39;. The price of one banner includes 3 grommets; additional grommets may be added for an extra charge.</p>

		Posters and Flyers</h4>

	<p>
		Some standard poster sizes are 8.5&quot;x11&quot;, 11&quot;x17&quot;, 12&rdquo;x18&rdquo; 17&quot;x23&quot;, 22&quot;x28&quot;, and 24&quot;x36&quot;. We can adjust to print any poster size, although size limit may apply. Posters can be emailed to Design Works, brought in on a disk or flash drive, or can&nbsp;</p>

	<h2>
		<span style="line-height: 1.1; font-family: WebGotham, Georgia; font-size: 18px;">Lawn Signs and Laminating</span></h2>

	<h4>
		Lawn Signs</h4>

	<p>
		Lawn signs are available from Design Works. The lawn sign will be 12&quot;x18&quot;, double sided, laminated, and will include metal frame.&nbsp;</p>

	<p>
		&nbsp;</p>

	<h4>
		Manual Press Laminate</h4>

	<p>
		Design Works uses a heat press laminate that has the ability to laminate thicker signage. Laminating with the manual press can be single or double sided. Maximum size is 32&quot;x40&quot;.</p>

	<h4>
		Roll Laminate</h4>

	<p>
		The roll laminating machine can laminate only double sided up to 3&#39; wide. The roll laminate does have a tendency to crease or wrinkle larger items pieces, please&nbsp;order at your own risk.</p>

	<h2>
		<font face="WebGotham, Georgia"><span style="line-height: 22px; font-size: 17.77px;">Mounting</span></font></h2>

	<h4>
		Drymount</h4>

		See also,&nbsp;<a href="http://union.unt.edu/retail/design_works#a">Printing and Paper Options</a></p>-->

	<p>
		Drymount is a heat process using a permanent bonding tissue. Drymount can also be used with matboard, or foamboard. Maximum size is 32&quot; x 40&quot;.</p>

	<h4>
		Boards For Mounting</h4>

	<ul>
		<li>
			Posterboard &mdash; Thin board in variety of colors. Maximum size is 22&quot;x28&quot;.</li>
		<li>
			Matboard &mdash; Thicker, stronger board in variety of colors. Maximum size is 32&quot;x40&quot;.</li>
		<li>
			Foamboard &mdash; Strong board with foam center - white only. Maximum size is 40&quot;x60&quot;.</li>
	</ul>

	<p>
		Samples of each board and colors are available in Design Works office.</p>
