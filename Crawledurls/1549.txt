				<h1>About</h1>
				<div class="well">
					<div class="col-md-8">
	<p id="about_text">
		The University Program Council (UPC) is UNT&#39;s student-run programming board that is devoted to planning and implementing events for students that are educational, social, cultural and entertaining. UPC events range from movie screenings to the latest hot topics and even trips to off campus venues. UPC is unique in that it is a department and a student organization. With an executive board of student programmers, UPC strives to plan events that meet the needs of UNT students.</p>

			<p>
	Thank you for interest in co-sponsoring an event with the University Program Council (UPC). We value opportunities to program diverse events to benefit the University of North Texas community and collaborating with other organizations helps us accomplish this goal.</p>

<p>
	Because UPC plans events a semester in advance, we highly recommend that you submit your Co-Sponsorship proposal as early as possible. For example if you&rsquo;re interested in a date that is during the spring semester, it is best to have your form turn in by late October of the previous semester.</p>

<p>
	If you are looking strictly for funding for your event, and you are a student organization, please consider requesting funds from Eagle Nest sponsored by SGA Funding Request Committee. More information can be found here:&nbsp;<strong><a href="http://sga.unt.edu/eagles-nest" target="_blank">http://sga.unt.edu/eagles-nest</a></strong></p>

<p>
	Ways in which UPC can assist your organization/department:</p>

<ul>
	<li>
		Volunteers for the day of event</li>
	<li>
		If there are any bookings to be done for artists we will arrange them or help your group arrange them</li>
	<li>
		Provide guidance on the programming process as a whole</li>
	<li>
		Advertising assistance for events</li>
	<li>
		Assistance in obtaining facilities</li>
	<li>
		Possible, but in no cases exclusive, funding for the event</li>
</ul>

<p>
	For more information about Co-Sponsorships and to apply, please fill out the form and submit to the UPC Office, Union 345.</p>

<p>
	<a class="button button-block" href="http://studentaffairs.unt.edu/sites/default/files/pdf/Organizational%20Co-Sponsorship%20Proposal%202014-2015.pdf" target="_blank" title="Co-Sponsorship Request Form">Co-Sponsorship Request Form</a></p>		</div>
