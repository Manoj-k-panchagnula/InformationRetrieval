<h3>Promotional Products Now Available For Browsing Online</h3>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Printing and Distribution Solutions is pleased to announce the latest addition in our line of quality services. PDS is now a distributor for purchasing branded promotional products that promote our family of brands – UNT, North Texas and Mean Green. This new program will provide the UNT community a below-retail cost advantage and exceptional customer service when purchasing branded products. This program features an online catalog that you can browse with easy to navigate product categories with a large variety of items. Please visit our website: <a href="http://www.promoplace.com/untpromos">http://www.promoplace.com/untpromos</a> to start shopping for your promotional items today.</p>
