	<p>&nbsp;<img hspace="200" alt="" width="343" height="154" src="/edo/sites/default/files/WomensCtr.jpg" /><br />
&nbsp;<br />
The Women&rsquo;s Center works with the University of North Texas and Denton communities to provide resources and services that enhance the experience of all students.&nbsp; The Women&rsquo;s Center contributes to an academic atmosphere in which positive role models are highly visible and gender bias and inequities can be addressed and eradicated.<br />
&nbsp;<br />
The University of North Texas recognizes that gender is a significant aspect of individual identity.&nbsp; Explorations of gender issues contribute to the overall intellectual inquiry.&nbsp; The UNT Women&rsquo;s Center provides programs to help students, faculty, and staff explore issues relevant to women in today&rsquo;s society.&nbsp; It is a safe and supportive environment open to everyone in the UNT community.<br />
&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>The UNT&nbsp;Women's Center is located on the second level of the University Union, Suite #216B.</strong><br />
