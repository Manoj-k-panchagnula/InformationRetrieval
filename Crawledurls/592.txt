<h1>AURP Innovation U Virtual Seminars</h1>
<img src="/assets/Images/aurp-innovation-u-banner-final.jpg" alt="" width="750"/>
<p>Creating university communities of innovation is AURP&rsquo;s core expertise. And as university innovation communities evolve, AURP announces university innovation community builders&rsquo; latest professional development opportunity &mdash; Innovation U Virtual Seminars. We&rsquo;ll be featuring information and knowledge about the latest trends in innovation and university districts &mdash; the why, the how-to and &ldquo;the who is doing it and where?&rdquo;</p>
