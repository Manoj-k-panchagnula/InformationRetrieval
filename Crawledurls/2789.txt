<p class="p1"><span style="font-size: medium;"><strong>&nbsp;</strong></span>&nbsp;</p>
<p class="p1"><span style="font-size: medium;"><strong>Hours Change in DMS to Support Final Exams</strong></span></p>
<p class="p1"><span style="font-size: small;"><strong><img style="float: left; margin-left: 10px; margin-right: 10px;" src="/benchmarks/sites/default/files/green-apple-final-exams.png" alt="final exam dates" width="124" height="84" /></strong>The hours of UIT's <a href="http://it.unt.edu/datamanage">Data Management Services</a> will be extended to accommodate increased usage during the 2016 final exam period, May 7-12, said <strong>JoAnn Luksich</strong>, manager, DMS. The adjusted hours are as follow.</span></p>
<p class="p1" style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span class="s1">May 7,&nbsp;</span><span class="s2"></span><span class="s1"></span><span class="s1">8 a.m.-5 p.m. &nbsp; | &nbsp;&nbsp;</span><span class="s1">May 9</span><span class="s2"></span><span class="s1">-12,&nbsp;</span><span class="s2"></span><span class="s1">8 a.m.-7 p.m.</span></span></p>
<p class="p1">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1" style="font-size: medium;"><strong>CRAN Releases User Update</strong></span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1"><strong><img style="float: right; margin-left: 10px; margin-right: 10px;" src="/benchmarks/sites/default/files/Rlogo.png" alt="" width="105" height="81" /><span style="font-size: small;">Attention, researchers:&nbsp;</span></strong></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span class="s1">Did you know that CRAN recently released a&nbsp;<a href="https://cran.r-project.org/index.html" target="_blank">new update of R; from R 3.2.3 to R 3.2.4</a>?&nbsp;</span>CRAN&nbsp;is a network of FTP and web servers around the world that store identical, up-to-date versions of code and documentation for R software.&nbsp;Contact <strong><a href="mailto:jonathan.starkweather@unt.edu">Jon Starkweather</a></strong>, R&amp;SS, for more information.</span></p>
<p class="p1">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="p1"><strong><span style="font-size: medium;"><a href="http://it.unt.edu/benchmarks/issues/2016/04/save-date-attend-forum-teaching-learning"><img style="float: left; margin-left: 10px; margin-right: 10px;" src="/benchmarks/sites/default/files/uftl2016_sm-1.png" alt="university forum poster" width="113" height="170" /></a><span></span>CLEAR Presents Renowned Teaching and Learning Expert</span></strong></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small;">Make your reservation today to hear <strong>Alan November</strong> at the University Forum on Teaching and Learning April 22, 2016, 8:30-10:30 a.m. in the University Union. November will discuss how to&nbsp;apply emerging tools to make thinking visible in new ways and patterns. <a href="http://it.unt.edu/benchmarks/issues/2016/04/save-date-attend-forum-teaching-learning">Read more about it and register today!</a></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p class="p1"><strong><span style="font-size: medium;">ITSM Provides ServiceNow Implementation Update and Training&nbsp;<a href="http://it.unt.edu/benchmarks/issues/2016/04/servicenow-implementation-update-training"><img style="float: right; margin: 10px;" src="/benchmarks/sites/default/files/servicenow.jpg" alt="" width="170" height="88" /></a></span></strong></p>
<p class="p1"><span style="font-size: x-small;"> By&nbsp;<a href="mailto:wil.clark@untsystem.edu">Wil Clark</a>, chief technology officer,&nbsp;<a href="https://itss.untsystem.edu/itss_divisions/it-service-management">ITSM</a>, UNT System IT Shared Services</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span style="font-size: small;"><span class="s1">This month we hit a major milestone in the IT Service Management implementation of ServiceNow. Acorio, our implementation&nbsp;partner, completed its work for ITSM. With user-acceptance testing complete as well, the ITSS team will focus on completing the Service Catalog request items and templates used for incident reporting and change requests.&nbsp; We have a significant amount of work left to prepare for the May 2, 2016</span><span class="s2"></span><span class="s1">&nbsp;go-live for ITSM. <a href="http://it.unt.edu/benchmarks/issues/2016/04/servicenow-implementation-update-training">Read more about it</a>.</span></span></p>
<p class="p1">&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: medium;"><strong>American Statistical Association Takes a Stand Against<br />P-Values</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: xx-small;">By <a href="mailto:jonathan.starkweather@unt.edu">Jonathan Starkweather</a>, <a href="http://www.unt.edu/rss/">Research and Statistical Support</a>, Research IT</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small;">The American Statistical Association recently convened a committee to evaluate the prospect of issuing a statement regarding the use of&nbsp;p-values. A&nbsp;p-value&nbsp;is a function of the observed sample results – a test statistic – about a statistical model, which measures how extreme the observation is.&nbsp;<a href="http://it.unt.edu/benchmarks/issues/2016/04/american-statistical-association-takes-position-against-p-values">Read more about it.</a></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: small;"><strong><span style="font-size: medium;">Microsoft Home Use Program for Employees</span><img style="float: right; margin: 10px;" src="/benchmarks/sites/default/files/ms-hup.png" alt="microsoft home use program logo" width="192" height="63" /></strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small;">The UNT System has a site license for Microsoft products. Employees are automatically eligible to use Microsoft Office Professional and Microsoft Learning on their home PC or Mac.&nbsp; If you are an employee of the UNT System, UNT, UNT Dallas or UNT Health Science Center, there is no longer a fee throughout your employment. <a href="http://it.unt.edu/benchmarks/issues/2016/04/microsoft-home-use-program-unt-employees">Read more about it.</a></span></p>
