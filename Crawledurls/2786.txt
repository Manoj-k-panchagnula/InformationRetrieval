    <h3><strong><span style="font-size: medium; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">UIT Mission</span></strong></h3>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">The University Information Technology Department of the University of North Texas is tasked to provide effective computing and information technology services to all members of the university community in order to facilitate the accomplishment of the university's mission and <a href="http://www.unt.edu/features/four-bold-goals/#fourboldgoals" target="_blank" title="Four Bold Goals">Four Bold Goals</a>. The department's mission is accomplished in open cooperation and partnership with its customers and other service providers.</span></p>
