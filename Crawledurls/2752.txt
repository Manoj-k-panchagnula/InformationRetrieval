              <div class="breadcrumb"><a href="/">Home</a></div><h2>How to View the Schedule of Classes Online with MyUNT</h2><div id="node-43" class="node clear-block">



  <div class="meta">
  
    </div>

  <div class="content">
    <p>To see courses online at My UNT (<a href="http://my.unt.edu">my.unt.edu</a>):</p>
<ol>
<li><strong>Go to <a href="http://my.unt.edu">my.unt.edu</a>.</strong></li>
<li>If you are not a UNT student, click Search in the <strong>Class Search</strong> box, then follow steps 5 - 14.</li>
<li>For UNT students, enter your EUID and password. For help with your EUID or password, click the help links below the login boxes.</li>
<li>Click the <strong>Search for Classes</strong> link.</li>
<li>Select <strong>University of North Texas</strong> as your institution.</li>
<li>Select <strong>Undergraduate</strong> or <strong>Graduate</strong> as the career of the classes you need.</li>
<li>Select <strong>term</strong>.</li>
<li>Click <strong>Go</strong>.</li>
<li>Enter the <strong>4-letter subject abbreviation</strong> for the class (for example, ENGL).</li>
<li>If you would like to see a specific class within that group of classes, type the catalog number of the class (for example, <strong>1310</strong>). To see every English class offered, leave the catalog number blank.</li>
<li>Click <strong>Search</strong>.</li>
<li>Click <strong>OK</strong> if your search returns over 50 classes.</li>
<li>Click View All Sections as necessary on the blue status bar to view all sections.</li>
<li>Click the section number link for the class to view important information. </li>
</ol>
<h3>Additional Resources</h3>
<p>For more information, contact the Registrar's Information Counter by calling 940-565-2378 or visiting Eagle Student Services Center (ESSC) Room 147.</p>
