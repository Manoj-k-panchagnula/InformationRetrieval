<h2 class="inlet" style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><span class="title">Bus Routes and General Parking Information</span></h2>
<div class="inlet" style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><span class="title"></span>&nbsp;</div>
<div class="inlet" style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><span class="title">Bus routes that stop directly in front of the Rec Center are the <a href="http://www.unt.edu/transit/routes/meangreen.html" target="_blank">Mean Green</a>, <a href="http://www.unt.edu/transit/routes/northtexan.html">North Texan</a>, <a href="http://www.unt.edu/transit/routes/discoverypark.html" target="_blank">Discovery Park</a>, and <a href="http://www.unt.edu/transit/routes/campuscruiser.html" target="_blank">Campus Cruiser</a> (Weekends).</span></div>
<div class="inlet" style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><span class="title"></span>&nbsp;</div>
<div class="inlet" style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><span class="title">Parking for the Pohl Recreation Center is controlled by UNT Parking Services. There&nbsp;is metered on the North Side of the building (Sycamore Street). The building is also located near spaces requiring a permit: </span></div>
<ul style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
<li style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
<div class="inlet" style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><span class="title">D Permit: street parking on East Side of building (between Rec Center and Health Center)</span></div>
</li>
<li style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><span class="title">R Permit: Lot on North East corner of building (across from McConnell hall)</span></li>
<li style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><span class="title">G and P Permits: South and West of building (in the Fouts Field/Traditions Hall lot)</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: none;">University of North Texas Parking Rules and Regulations</h2>
<h3 style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: none;">6.5 Parking Lot Enforcement</h3>
<ul style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: none;">
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: none;"><strong>6.5.1</strong> Student parking lots are enforced when school is in session (see also 8.16).</li>
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: none;"><strong>6.5.2</strong> Faculty/Staff parking is enforced when the university is open (see also 4.10).</li>
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: none;"><strong>6.5.3</strong> Yellow curbs, handicap spaces, fire zones, motorcycle and bicycle zones are enforced 24 hours a day, seven days a week.</li>
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: none;"><strong>6.5.4</strong> Spaces with marked time limits are enforced from 7:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m. Monday through Thursday, and 7:00 a.m. to 3 p.m. Friday.</li>
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: none;"><strong>6.5.5</strong> Service Vehicle Spaces are enforced 24 hours, Monday through Friday, with a valid UNT permit required from 10:00pm to 7:00am.</li>
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: none;"><strong>6.5.6</strong> Trucks, Vans, Sport Utility Vehicles, and Jeeps are not allowed to park on Highland Street from Avenue C to Welch Street.</li>
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: none;"><strong>6.5.7</strong> Meters are enforced 7:00am to 10:00pm Monday through Thursday, and 7:00 a.m. to 3:00 p.m. Friday, except university holidays, or as otherwise posted.</li>
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: none;"><strong>6.5.8</strong> Reserved "A" Spaces are enforced M-F 7:00am to 5:00pm when the university is open. Vehicles in these spaces must display the correct permit for the space during these times/day or they are subject to impounding.</li>
</ul>
<h2 style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: none;"></h2>
<h2 style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: none;">Non-permit Alternative Parking</h2>
<ul style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: none;">
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: none;">Meters are available on West Sycamore St., just North of the Rec Center.</li>
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: none;">Fouts Field is free after 5pm, Monday through Thursday, and after 3pm on Fri.</li>
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: none;">Any street, lot or metered area (except R and the handicapped parking spots) after 10pm is free.</li>
<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; list-style-type: none;">A <a href="http://www.unt.edu/parking/permits.html" target="_blank">G Permit</a> can be purchased.</li>
</ul></div>
