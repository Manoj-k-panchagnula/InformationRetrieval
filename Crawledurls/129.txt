									<h2><font color="green">Google Chrome drops popular plug-in support <div style="float: right;">September 2015</div></font></h2>
									<p style="margin-bottom:0;">Google Chrome 45, released September 1st, 2015, has completely dropped support for NPAPI plug-ins.  Popular NPAPI plug-ins include <b>Java</b> and <b>Silverlight</b>, amongst others.</p>
									<p>Some portions of Blackboard Learn may use one of these plug-ins, in which case, you'll need to switch to a browser that supports it, such as <a href="http://www.firefox.com">Firefox</a>, <a href="https://support.apple.com/downloads/#safari">Safari</a>, or <a href="http://windows.microsoft.com/en-us/internet-explorer/download-ie">Internet Explorer</a>.</p>
									<br />
								
								
								
									<h2><font color="green">"The Mainframe" Apple Store opens on campus <div style="float: right;">July 2015</div></font></h2>
									<p style="margin-bottom:0;">The Mainframe has opened a temporary location in the Stovall Temporary Union Building (STUB) room 161.  You can visit the store now to see their deals on products and make purchases on campus.</p>
									<p>When the store opens in the new Union, you will find that it is a full fledged Apple Store, providing Apple products, accessories, and Apple-certified techs for full-service repairs.  This is an exciting addition to UNT, especially considering the nearest Apple Stores are in Southlake and Frisco.</p>
									<p>Check out their <a href="http://studentaffairs.unt.edu/mainframe">Union website</a> and <a href="https://www.facebook.com/themainframeUNT">"The Mainframe" Facebook page</a>.</p>
