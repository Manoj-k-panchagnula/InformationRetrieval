    <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even"><h3>
	Logos, graphics, templates and other digital assets</h3>
<p>
	Visual recognition of our brand is primary to building awareness about UNT. You can help by using university-approved digital assets. You can download these assets by clicking the appropriate link below and then providing your EUID and password.</p>
<p>
	<a href="/download-brand-toolkits/unt-marks">Download UNT, athletic and spirit logos</a></p>
<p>
	<a href="/download-brand-toolkits/lockups" title="Download UNT unit lock-ups">Download UNT unit lockups (college, department, centers, institutes and division lockups)</a></p>
<p>
	<a href="/download-brand-toolkits/other-resources">Download brand resources (theme art, templates for PowerPoint, brochures and more)</a></p>
<p>
	<a href="/download-brand-toolkits/stock-photography">Download stock photography and video stills</a></p>
<p>
	<a href="/download-brand-toolkits/hd-video-stock">Download HD video stock</a></p>
<p>
	<a href="/download-brand-toolkits/125th-Anniversary-Mark-Guidelines">Download 125th anniversary marks</a></p>
<p>
	Be sure to review our brand standards guide thoroughly before beginning any project. Licensed vendors can access all of our marks via the <a href="http://www.clc.com/">Collegiate Licensing Company</a>. Hiring departments may not give vendors their EUID and passwords to provide access, as this is a violation of university policy.</p>
<p>
	If you are not a member of the UNT community and need one of our digital assets, please contact Sean Zeigler&nbsp;<a href="mailto:brandreview@unt.edu">by email</a> or call 940-565-4814.</p>
