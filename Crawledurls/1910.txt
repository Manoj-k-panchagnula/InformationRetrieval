<h2>Serving Texas Veterans</h2>
<p>Since the days of the Republic, Texas has always supported her Veterans in return for their military service.&#160; In the early days, it was in the form of land given by the state for military service in the Texas Revolution.&#160; In 1946, this tradition was formally recognized with the creation of the Texas Veterans Land Board (VLB) to administer benefits exclusively for Texas Veterans.&#160; Since then, more than 200,000 VLB loans have been funded for Texas Veterans.For over half a century, the VLB has been keeping the promise to serve Texas Veterans.</p>
