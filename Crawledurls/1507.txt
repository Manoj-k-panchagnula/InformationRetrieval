<h2 class="real-title">University Relations, Communications &amp; Marketing</h2>
<h3>What does URCM do?</h3>

<p>As the chief communications organization for the University of North Texas, we collaborate with our internal and external partners to:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Advance and protect UNT’s institutional identity and reputation</li>
  <li>Reinforce and grow UNT’s brand in graphics, images and words</li>
  <li>Increase UNT’s visibility in public and in the higher education marketplace</li>
  <li>Support UNT’s student recruitment, retention and graduation goals</li>
  <li>Strengthen UNT’s relevance and value to key audiences, including alumni, donors, faculty and staff</li>
</ul>

<p>What’s your goal?  We’re here to help you – and UNT – succeed.</p>  </div>
