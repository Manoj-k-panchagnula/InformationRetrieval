                                <h1 class="title">Recognition, Accreditation, and Accountability</h1>                                                                <div id="content-content" class="content-content">
                                  
<div id="node-762" class="node odd full-node node-type-book">
  <div class="inner">
    
    
    
    
    <div class="content clearfix">
      <p>U.S. News and World Report ranked the College of Education <strong>13th in the nation</strong> for Best Online Graduate Education Programs, including its master's degree in Educational Psychology with a <a href="/educational-psychology/graduate-programs/gifted-and-talented-education">Concentration in Gifted and Talented Education</a> and a new accelerated online master's degree in <a href="http://www.edmastersonline.unt.edu/">Educational Leadership</a>.<br /><br />U.S. News and World Report ranked the counseling program <strong>13th in the nation</strong> in the student counseling and personnel services category in its recently released 2014 Best Graduate School rankings. The program is the top-ranked in Texas.</p>
<hr />
<p>Programs within the College of Education are accredited by nationally recognized bodies.</p>
<table style="width: 100%;" border="0">
<tbody>
<tr>
<td style="width: 30%;"><a href="http://www.ncate.org/"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="/sites/default/files/762/762/CAEP.png" alt="CAEP Logo" width="250" /></a></td>
<td style="width: 70%;">
<p>All <strong>Teacher Education</strong> programs in the College of Education are accredited by the <a href="http://www.caepsite.org/standards.html">Council for the Accreditation of Educator Preparation</a> (CAEP).</p>
<p>Details of Teacher Education accreditation and accountability are&nbsp;<a href="/recognition-and-accreditation/teacher-education-accreditation">available here</a>.</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><a href="http://www.cacrep.org"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="/sites/default/files/762/CACREP.png" alt="CACREP Logo" height="103" width="91" /></a></td>
<td>
<p>The <strong>Counseling Program</strong> is accredited by the <a href="http://www.cacrep.org">Council for the Accreditation of Counseling and Related Educational Programs (CACREP)</a>.</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>&nbsp;<a href="http://www.naeyc.org/"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="/sites/default/files/762/naeyc.jpg" alt="NAEYC Logo" height="50" width="145" /></a></td>
<td>
<p>The <strong>Early Childhood Program</strong> is accredited by the <a href="http://www.naeyc.org/">National Association for the Education of Young Children (NAEYC)</a>.</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: center;">&nbsp;<a href="http://www.earlylearningleaders.org/?page=accreditation"><img src="/sites/default/files/762/762/NAC-logo-150.png" alt="National Accreditation Commission logo" height="112" width="150" /></a></td>
<td>
<p>The <strong>Child Development Lab</strong> (CDL) is accredited by the&nbsp;<a href="http://www.earlylearningleaders.org/?page=accreditation">National Accreditation Commission for Early Care and Education Programs (NAC)</a>. The State of Texas, through North Texas Child Care Services, has designated the CDL as a Four Star Designated Vendor and has certified it through the Texas Department of Protective and Regulatory Services.</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>&nbsp;<a href="http://www.nrpa.org"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="/sites/default/files/762/NRPA.jpg" alt="NRPA Logo" width="250" /></a></td>
<td>
<p>The&nbsp;<strong>Recreation, Event and Sport Management Program</strong> in the Department of Kinesiology, Health Promotion, and Recreation is accredited by the <a href="http://www.nrpa.org">National Recreation and Park Association</a>. This national accreditation recognizes programs which meet rigorous standards. The only other accredited program in Texas is at Texas A&amp;M.</p>
</td>
</tr>
