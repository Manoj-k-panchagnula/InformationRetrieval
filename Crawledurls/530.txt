    <h3>Organized Research and Service Units</h3>
<p>The listing below shows organized research and service units that engage in research and/or public service and training.&nbsp; Those units that offer degree programs or provide credit courses in support of other degree programs are shown on the primary organization chart.&nbsp; Centers/institutes indicated with a "+" have been exempted by the Provost and Vice President for Academic Affairs from periodic review under UNT <a href="http://policy.unt.edu/sites/default/files/untpolicy/16.8_Policy%20on%20Centers%20and%20Institutes.pdf" target="_blank">Policy 16.8</a> because the primary mission of each unit is to provide educational support and/or provide internship sites for its constituent academic unit.</p>
