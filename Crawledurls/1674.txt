							<h2>Eagle Express plan: How it works for freshmen</h2><div id="node-1" class="node clear-block">



  <div class="meta">
      <span class="submitted">Submitted by Anonymous on Wed, 03/12/2014 - 14:59</span>
  
    </div>

  <div class="content">
    <div id="home-buttons"><a class="brown-button" href="/transfer">Transfer student?</a> 
<!-- <a class="blue-button" href="/current">Current student?</a> --><!-- <a class="blue-button" href="/current">Current student?</a> --><p> <a class="blue-button" href="https://studentaccounting.unt.edu/eagle-express-tuition-plan">Already enrolled in<br /> Eagle Express?</a></p></div>
<p>Tuition costs are a big part of planning for college – and a big concern. UNT offers you an innovative solution to rising college costs with the Eagle Express plan. Eagle Express is more than a tuition plan, it’s a plan for success.</p>
<ul>
<li>UNT’s Eagle Express plan locks in your total academic costs for four years.</li>
<li>It pays to graduate on time. Save up to $3,000 in your last 15 hours (typically your last semester) by graduating in four years, and you may also qualify for the <a href="https://registrar.unt.edu/graduation-and-diplomas/1000-tuition-rebate">statewide tuition rebate of $1,000</a>.</li>
<li>You’ll save time and money by knowing your academic costs up front, graduating on time and earning a full-time salary sooner.</li>
</ul>
<h3 style="font-style: normal; line-height: 18px;">Take these four easy steps for success</h3>
<p><img src="/sites/default/files/u4/four-easy-steps-smal.png" alt="Four easy steps" width="900" height="120" /></p>
<p style="font-family: Verdana, Geneva, sans-serif; font-size: 12px; font-style: normal; line-height: 1.5;">&nbsp;</p>
<h3 style="font-style: normal; line-height: 18px;">Eligibility</h3>
<p>The Eagle Express plan is available to all new freshmen and <a href="/transfer">transfer students</a> entering UNT.&nbsp;</p>
<!-- <p><a href="/current">Current students</a>&nbsp;have a one-time opportunity to opt-in to the plan, but no continuing students will be allowed to join the plan after Fall 2014.</p> --><!-- <p><a href="/current">Current students</a>&nbsp;have a one-time opportunity to opt-in to the plan, but no continuing students will be allowed to join the plan after Fall 2014.</p> --><p>You must meet <a href="https://registrar.unt.edu/transcripts-and-records/residency-information">Texas residency requirements</a> and be seeking a bachelor’s degree.&nbsp;If you do not meet the Texas residency requirements, you will be automatically enrolled in the <a href="/traditionalplan">traditional tuition plan</a>.</p>
<p>If you choose not to opt in to the Eagle Express plan, you will be automatically enrolled in the <a href="/traditionalplan">traditional tuition plan</a>.</p>
<h3 style="font-style: normal; line-height: 18px;">Benefits of Eagle Express</h3>
<ul>
<li>You’ll save up to $3,000 if you graduate on time.</li>
<li>You’ll know your total academic costs for four years, helping you plan expenses. Course/instructional fees are included in the locked-in amount.</li>
<li>You’ll graduate with less debt.</li>
<li>You'll get the best of both worlds – the best bachelor's degree experience in Texas at an affordable price.</li>
<li>You’ll get a head start on your career sooner – whether you want to land a full-time job or continue on to graduate school.</li>
</ul>
<p>The Eagle Express graduation incentive is based on&nbsp;how many hours you complete at UNT. Any college credit hours you earned before high school graduation do not count against new freshmen.</p>
<p class="p1">For example, if you’re a high school graduate with 40 AP and/or dual credit hours, you would still be eligible for the $3,000 graduation incentive – which is a fabulous deal.</p>
<h3>Eagle Express by the numbers</h3>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Eagle Express Plan vs. Traditional Plan</strong><br /> For New Freshmen<br /> (Total Academic Costs* by Year)</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<div style="float: right; width: 300px; font-size: 13px; line-height: 15px; margin-left: 20px;">
<p style="margin-bottom: 0 !important;">*Total Academic Cost is based on taking 15 semester hours in the fall semester and 15 semester hours in the spring semester.<br /> This includes:</p>
<ul>
<li>Board-designated tuition</li>
<li>State-mandated tuition</li>
<li>Mandatory fees</li>
<li>Course/instructional fees (averaged for traditional plan)</li>
</ul>
<div style="font-size: 11px;">
<p>**In your final 15 semester credit hours, the Eagle Express plan will save you $3,000. With the $1,000 tuition rebate, you could save up to $4,000.&nbsp;</p>
</div>

<p>If you choose the Eagle Express plan and <a title="If you don't graduate within the allotted time, you will move to the traditional plan effective for the next semester.&nbsp;" href="/sites/default/files/u4/ee-box-chart-2.png" rel="lightbox">do not graduate on time</a>, you’ll end up paying about the same as you would under the traditional tuition plan despite losing the graduation incentive.</p>
<p><strong>The choice is yours! If you decide the Eagle Express plan is the perfect fit for you, call 844-236-6855 for details on how and when to choose Eagle Express.</strong></p>
