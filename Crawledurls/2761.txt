                <h2 class="title" id="page-title">Refunds</h2>
                        <div class="block block-system block-main block-system-main odd block-without-title" id="block-system-main">
  <div class="block-inner clearfix">
                
    <div class="content clearfix">
      <div  about="/refunds" typeof="foaf:Document" id="node-page-14" class="ds-1col node node-page view-mode-full node-not-promoted node-not-sticky author-sgm0071 odd clearfix clearfix">

  
  <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"><p><span style="color: #ff0000;"><strong>Important Information Regarding Refunds</strong></span>  </p>
<p>Some types of financial aid can only be used to pay for certain charges. Therefore, you may receive a refund even if you have outstanding charges on your student account. If a balance remains on your account after you receive a financial aid refund, you are responsible for paying it in full on time each semester.  Please review your student account frequently throughout each term for balances owed.</p>
<h3>Refund Options</h3>
<div class="cta-right cta" style="float: right; width: 25%; margin: 0 0 3em 3em;">
<div class="inside" style="padding: 1em; background: #72b844; border-radius: 5px; text-align: center;"><a style="line-height: 1.35em; display: block;" href="https://studentaccounting.unt.edu/class-drop-and-withdrawal-refunds">Get information about class drop and withdrawal refunds »</a></div>
</div>
<p>The University of North Texas has partnered with Higher One® to process student refunds for all UNT students. This partnership allows for more efficient processing of refunds.  Refunds sent to HigherOne will be processed according to the refunding method the student has elected. In order to minimize delays, students should log into the UNT refunding preference website by <a href="https://untdebitcard.higheroneaccount.com/">CLICKING HERE</a> and make a refund method selection. Failure to select an option will delay your refund.</p>
<ul><li><strong>Direct Deposit (ACH)</strong> – If you choose this option, you will need to enter your existing bank routing and account numbers, your refund will be deposited into this account.  Typically this takes 2-3 days for the money to become available in your account from the date your refund is processed. </li>
<li><strong>Paper Check - </strong>If you choose this option, a paper check will be mailed to you.  Please note that it may take up to 10 days from the date of disbursement for you to receive your refund from the date your refund is processed.  Make sure that your current mailing address is up-to-date in the student center to prevent additional delays of your initial refund.</li>
<li><strong>One Account Debit Card -</strong> If you choose this option, a debit MasterCard will be mailed to you.  You will immediately have access to a virtual debit card that you can use for on-line transactions until you receive your debit card in the mail (Please note: the virtual card may not be accepted for in-person transactions).  Typically your money will be available in this account the same day refunding is processed by the university.</li>
</ul><p>Students should choose the option that best fits their needs.  <a href="https://www.fdic.gov/consumers/consumer/moneysmart/">FDIC's "Money Smart"</a> is a good resource to consult when deciding which option to choose. Please note that if you do not select a refunding option, any refunds due to you may be delayed.</p>
<p><strong>For New Students</strong></p>
<p>Students that are new to the university will receive a <span style="color: 008000;">GREEN ENVELOPE</span> sent to the mailing address listed on your student account after you have registered for at least 6 hours or have a credit balance on your account to be refunded.  The envelope will contain a personal code to login to the UNT refunding preference website which can be accessed by <a href="https://untdebitcard.higheroneaccount.com/">CLICKING HERE</a>.  Follow the directions to choose 1 of the 3 options listed above to receive your refund.  All options are free to students for refund processing (other fees may apply).</p>
<p><strong>For Returning Students</strong></p>
<p>Students that have not yet chosen their refunding preference will need to locate the previously mailed <span style="color: 008000;">GREEN ENVELOPE</span> or contact Student Accounting for assistance.</p>
<p><strong>ATM Accessibility</strong></p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #ff0000;"><strong>Beginning May 4th</strong></span></strong></p>
<p>Higher One (One Card) customers will have access to the Allpoint network, the largest ATM network.  Customers will be able to get their cash surcharge-free at 55,000 ATM’s worldwide!  Please click on the <a href="http://www.allpointnetwork.com/index.aspx#whatis-info">Allpoint network locator</a> to find the location closest to you. </p>
<p><strong>ATM Locations</strong></p>
<ul><li>Your Existing Bank – Check your bank’s website for the ATM locator to find the closest location to you.</li>
<li>EasyRefund Debit Card – This card can be used at any of the <a href="http://www.allpointnetwork.com/index.aspx#whatis-info">Allpoint locations</a>.</li>
<li>Wells Fargo and Bank of America ATM’s are located on the UNT Denton campus in various locations.</li>
</ul><p><strong>FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS:</strong></p>
<p>The answers to many of your questions can be found by <a href="https://higherone.custhelp.com/app">CLICKING HERE</a>. </p>
<p><strong><em>When will I receive the green envelope?</em></strong></p>
<p>Students that are new to the university will receive a <span style="color: 008000;">GREEN ENVELOPE</span> sent to the mailing address listed on your account after you have registered for at least 6 hours or have a credit balance on your account to be refunded.</p>
<p><strong><em>How do I select a refund preference?</em></strong></p>
<p>Selecting your refund preference can only be completed online. However, never fear...it's fast and easy! To ensure timely disbursement of your refund, please select your refund preference as soon as possible. To get started, simply:</p>
<ul type="disc"><li>Visit the website listed on your refund selection kit</li>
<li>Click the '<strong>Get Started</strong>' button.</li>
</ul><p>When selecting your refund preference, we suggest making a mental note of your password and/or PIN for future use in logging into your account or Profile.</p>
<p><strong><em>How can I change my refund preference?</em></strong></p>
<p>You may change your refund preference at any time at no cost to you. It's quick and easy!</p>
<ul type="disc"><li><a href="https://higherone.custhelp.com/cgi-bin/higherone.cfg/php/admin/display/faq_print.php?p_aid=77">Log in to your profile.</a></li>
<li>Under the <strong>'Refunds'</strong> tab, click <strong>'Refund Preferences.'</strong></li>
<li>Choose your desired refund preference.</li>
<li>Click <strong>'Update Preference'</strong> to submit your request.</li>
</ul><p>Please be aware that changes to your refund preference only affect future refunds, not refunds that have already been disbursed.</p>
<p><strong><em>How does the Easy Refund Debit Card (OneAccount) work?</em></strong></p>
<p>Please note:  The OneAccount is only one of 3 available refund options and is not required.  However, a refund selection must be made by every eligible student even if you are not expecting a refund.</p>
<p>The OneAccountisa non-interest bearing, FDIC insured internet-only checking account, serviced by Higher One, Inc. with student-focused features. Funds are deposited to the OneAccount the same business day they are released by your school. With the OneAccount, you can enjoy:</p>
<ul type="disc"><li>No monthly fee for students</li>
<li>No minimum balance requirements</li>
<li>The convenience of using Debit MasterCard® at millions of locations worldwide</li>
<li>Surcharge free ATMs that are on the Allpoint network</li>
<li>Mobile App</li>
<li>Online Bill Pay &amp; Photo Bill Pay</li>
<li>Mobile deposits</li>
<li>Card On/Off Switch</li>
<li>Online and mobile money management tools</li>
<li>Financial education information</li>
