	      <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"> <p>Commencement draws many people to the UNT campus to celebrate with graduates. Because so many people will be converging on campus at the same time, there will be more congestion and traffic on campus than usual all day Friday, May 13, and Saturday, May 14.</p>
<p>Drivers should be prepared that they may encounter delays before they reach Denton due to the Texas Department of Transportation's <a href="http://www.35express.org/">35 Express Project</a>. A portion of North Texas Boulevard leading into campus (between Eagle Drive and Highland Street) will be closed to vehicles during commencement events for pedestrians' safety.</p>
<p>Please be aware, <strong>expect delays, plan ahead and allow yourself ample time to get to your destination.</strong> Below is information including:</p>
<ul><li><a href="#directions">Directions to and from campus</a></li>
<li><a href="#shuttlepark">Where to park for commencement</a>. Guests will park along the eastern edge of campus on Welch Street and will be able to take handicap-accessible shuttles provided by DCTA to and from commencement events all day Friday and Saturday.</li>
<li><a href="#shuttle">DCTA shuttle service information</a></li>
<li><a href="#parking">Parking for Undergraduate College Recognition Ceremonies and Doctoral and Master's Convocation</a><br />
	including instructions for guests, special needs, graduates, and employees</li>
<li><a href="#universitywide">Parking for University-wide Commencement</a><br />
	including instructions for guests, special needs, graduates, and employees</li>
<li><a href="#train">A-Train service to and from Denton</a></li>
</ul><p><strong>Note:</strong> If you are a guest driving to campus for a commencement event, please allow 30 to 45 minutes to park, ride the shuttle to the event venue, and find a seat inside. If you are a graduate participating in a commencement event, please allow 30 to 45 minutes to park and get to the event venue.</p>
<p>If you are a guest taking the A-Train to Denton please allow 30-45 minutes to park, ride the A-Train, catch the UNT Shuttle at the Downtown Denton Transit Center, ride the shuttle to the event venue and find a seat inside the commencement venue.</p>
<p><a href="https://www.unt.edu/commencement/schedule.htm">See the Commencement Schedule of Events</a>. </p>

</ol><h4><a name="train" id="train"></a>A-Train service</h4>
<h5>Friday, May 13</h5>
<p>DCTA will run regular weekday A-train service and special UNT Commencement Shuttle services instead of standard weekday UNT Shuttle service. Commencement shuttle buses will operate frequently from the Downtown Denton Transit Center to the Murchison Center and UNT Coliseum. This shuttle will pass through downtown Denton so passengers can shop and dine throughout the day. <a href="https://www.dcta.net/images/uploads/content_files/news_events/Restaurants_Near_the_Eulene_Brock_Downtown_Denton_Transit_Center.pdf" target="_blank">Click here to view restaurants within walking distance of the DDTC</a>. The shuttle service will run from 7 a.m. to 10:30 p.m.</p>
<h5>Saturday, May 14</h5>
<p>DCTA will run regular Saturday A-train service and special UNT Commencement Shuttle services throughout the day. On Friday, commencement shuttle buses will operate frequently from the DDTC to Apogee Stadium, the Murchison Performing Arts Center and UNT Coliseum.  This shuttle will pass through downtown Denton so passengers can shop and dine throughout the day. On Saturday, commencement shuttle buses will operate frequently from the Downtown Denton Transit Center to the Murchison Performing Arts Center and UNT Coliseum. This shuttle will pass through downtown Denton so passengers can shop and dine throughout the day. <a href="http://www.dcta.net/images/uploads/content_files/news_events/Restaurants_Near_the_Eulene_Brock_Downtown_Denton_Transit_Center.pdf" target="_blank">Click here to view restaurants within walking distance of the DDTC.</a></p>
<p><a href="https://www.dcta.net/routes-schedules/a-train"><strong>View the Friday and Saturday A-train schedule</strong></a><strong>.</strong></p>
<p>If you are a guest taking the A-train to Denton please allow as much as an-hour-and-a-half to park, ride the A-Train, catch the UNT Shuttle at the Downtown Denton Transit Center, ride the shuttle to the event venue and find a seat inside the commencement venue.</p>
<h4><a name="shuttle" id="shuttle"></a>DCTA Shuttle service May 13 and May 14</h4>
<h5>Friday, May 13</h5>
<p>UNT Shuttle and DCTA Connect bus service for UNT will be limited service for commencement on May 13 so the usual UNT shuttle routes will not be provided.<br />
Shuttles will begin operating at 7:30 a.m. and run continuously throughout the day until all guests have been returned to their vehicles Friday following the Doctoral and Master's Convocation. Beginning at 7:30 a.m., shuttles will start picking up guests  who park in lots in the  east  portion of campus off of Welch St and taking them to Apogee Stadium for the University-wide Commencement ceremony. After the University-wide Commencement ceremony, shuttles will be available where guests were dropped off to return those guests to their vehicles.</p>
<h5>All DCTA buses are special needs and handicapped-accessible.</h5>
<p>Please allow 30 to 45 minutes to park, ride the shuttle to the event venue, and find a seat inside.</p>
<h5>Shuttle pickup locations for commencement parking on Welch Street:</h5>
<ul><li><a name="shuttlepark" id="shuttlepark"></a>All UNT commencement guests will be directed to park in the parking lots along Welch Street</li>
<li>Guests will be directed to park in a specific Welch Street lot in a specific order. As a lot fills, it will be closed and later guests will begin parking in the next lot.</li>
<li>Shuttles will take guests from the Welch Street lot that is currently being filled to a drop off stop at the venues being used at that time.  Follow the commencement signs and traffic control officer directions to the lot where guests are currently being parked.</li>
</ul><h5>Shuttle drop off/pickup location for the Coliseum and the Murchison Performing Arts Center:</h5>
<ul><li>DCTA buses will drop off guests at a stop on North Texas Boulevard between the Coliseum and the Murchison Performing Arts Center so they can attend College Recognition Ceremonies or the Doctoral and Master's Convocation with Doctoral/MFA hooding.</li>
<li>The pickup location to transport guests back to commencement parking on Welch Street is across the street from the drop off location in the Gateway Center circle drive.</li>
<li>All shuttles will stop at each Welch Street Lot on the trips to return guests to their vehicles.</li>
</ul><h5>Shuttle drop off/pickup location University-wide Commencement:</h5>
<ul><li>UNT Shuttle and DCTA Connect buses will drop off guests at Gate 3 at Apogee for the University-wide Commencement. This also will be the pickup location to transport guests back to University-wide commencement parking lots in the northwest portion of campus.</li>
</ul><h5>Saturday, May 14</h5>
<p>UNT Shuttle and DCTA Connect bus service for UNT will be limited service for commencement on May 14. Shuttles will begin operating at 6:30 a.m. and run continuously throughout the day until all guests have been returned to their vehicles Saturday following the final College Recognition Ceremony.</p>
<p><strong>All DCTA buses are special needs and handicapped-accessible.</strong></p>
<p>Please allow 30 to 45 minutes to park, ride the shuttle to the event venue, and find a seat inside.</p>
<h5>Shuttle pickup locations for commencement parking on Welch Street:</h5>
<ul><li>All UNT commencement guests will be directed to park in the parking lots along Welch Street</li>
<li>Guests will be directed to park in a specific Welch Street lot in a specific order. As a lot fills, it will be closed and later guests will begin parking in the next lot.</li>
<li>Shuttles will take guests from the Welch Street lot that is currently being filled to a drop off stop on North Texas Boulevard between the Coliseum and the Murchison Performing Arts Center for College Recognition Ceremonies  Follow the commencement signs and traffic control officer directions to the lot where guests are currently being parked.</li>
</ul><h5>Shuttle drop off/pickup location for commencement events:</h5>
<ul><li><strong>For College Recognition Ceremonies:</strong> DCTA buses will drop off guests at a bus stop on North Texas Boulevard between the Coliseum and the Murchison Performing Arts Center. The pickup location to transport guests back to commencement parking on Welch Street is across the street from the drop off location in the Gateway Center circle drive.</li>
</ul><h3><a name="parking" id="parking"></a>Parking for College Recognition Ceremonies and Doctoral and Master's Convocation</h3>
<p>The Commencement Parking and Transportation Plan will start Friday morning May 13 at 7 a.m.</p>
<h4>Instructions for guests</h4>
<ul><li>All guests should take Fort Worth Drive (Highway 377) north to Eagle Drive, then take Eagle Drive West to Welch Street North to park in the lots along Welch Street. Officers will be directing traffic to the designated parking lots.</li>
<li>Handicap-accessible shuttles will run continuously to transport guests from these lots to and from the Coliseum and the Murchison Performing Arts Center before and after the College Recognition Ceremonies and Doctoral and Master's Convocation. <a href="#shuttle">See shuttle service info</a>.</li>
<li>Please allow 30 to 45 minutes to park, take the shuttle to the commencement venue, and find a seat inside.</li>
<li>Please make note of which lot you parked in for your return.</li>
<li>Maple Street will be open to allow drivers access to the Coliseum Circle on Avenue D, on the east side of the Coliseum, to drop off and pick up graduates. If guests are dropping off graduates at the Coliseum, after doing so, they should go east on Maple Street to park in the Welch Street lots.  <strong>This area will be very congested so expect delays.</strong></li>
</ul><h4>Instructions for handicapped and special needs parking</h4>
<p>Special needs and handicapped drop off and parking will be available at the Coliseum and Murchison Performing Arts Center until the parking lots have filled.</p>
<h4>For the Coliseum:</h4>
<ul><li>Special needs and handicapped drop off at the Coliseum Circle is on Avenue D on the east side of the Coliseum.</li>
<li>Handicapped parking for the Coliseum is along Avenue D and in Lot 27, which is east of the Coliseum between Highland Street and Maple Street.</li>
</ul><h4>For the Murchison Performing Arts Center:</h4>
<ul><li>Special needs and handicapped drop off at the Murchison Performing Arts Center is on the northwest side of the center in the circle drive.</li>
<li>Handicapped parking for the Murchison Performing Arts Center is on the south side of Lot 20 (Fouts Field parking). Handicapped parking is very limited.</li>
</ul><p><strong>Note:</strong> At times there will be major congestion in these handicapped parking areas. <strong>To avoid congestion, guests with special needs also can choose to park in the Welch Street lots and ride the shuttles to these venues.</strong> All DCTA buses are handicapped and special needs accessible.</p>
<h4>Instructions for graduates</h4>
<ul><li>North Texas Boulevard will be closed between Eagle Drive and Highland Street all day Friday, May 13 and Saturday, May 14.</li>
<li>Fouts Field parking lots will only be accessible from:
<ul><li>the I-35 access road past the Murchison Performing Arts Center</li>
<li>Allen Saxe Drive next to Traditions Hall</li>
</ul></li>

<li>If Fouts Field parking lots are full, graduates can park in any lots north of Fouts Field and walk to the Coliseum or Murchison Performing Arts Center.</li>
</ul><h4>Instructions for UNT employees</h4>
<ul><li>North Texas Boulevard will be closed between Eagle Drive and Highland Street all day Friday, May 13and Saturday, May 14.</li>
<li>Fouts Field parking lots will only be accessible from:
<ul><li>the I-35 access road past the Murchison Performing Arts Center</li>
<li>Allen Saxe Drive next to Traditions Hall</li>
</ul></li>

<li>The North Texas Boulevard bridge over I-35 may be closed at times to traffic crossing I-35.</li>
<li><strong>Be aware that there will be major congestion at North Texas Boulevard and Bonnie Brae Street near the Coliseum and Murchison Performing Arts Center throughout the day Friday and Saturday.</strong></li>
<li>Faculty and staff participating in the College Recognition Ceremonies should park in Lot 26, north of Highland Street. You may park in the Fouts Field parking lots but space is limited and the surrounding streets will be congested with event traffic. Expect delays. </li>
</ul><h3><a name="universitywide" id="universitywide"></a>Parking for University-wide Commencement</h3>
