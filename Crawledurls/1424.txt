<h2>Celebrate Mean Green Pride Day every Friday.</h2>
    <p>At UNT, we have a lot to be proud of. This is why <strong>every Friday is Mean Green Pride Day</strong> – a chance for UNT students, faculty, staff and alumni to represent the Mean Green nation. Whether you are conducting groundbreaking research on campus in Denton, making history in your profession across the U.S. or making your mark internationally, wear your green and show off your UNT pride. We have a lot to be proud of, so let everyone know you’re proud to be UNT.</p>
