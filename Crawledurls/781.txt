<h1 class="tennis">Waranch Tennis Complex</h1>
<div class="sidebar">
<p class="sideimage"><img src="sidebar-tennis.jpg" width="350" height="200" alt="Waranch Tennis Complex" /><br />
<h2>Waranch Tennis Complex</h2>
<ul><li>Venue for: Tennis</li>
<li>Opened: 2006</li>
<li><a class="toplinks" href="http://www.northtexasfanguide.com/tennis.html" target="_new">North Texas Tennis Fan Guide</a></li>
<li><a class="toplinks" href="http://www.unt.edu/transit/pdf/2011-2012%20ParkingMap.pdf" target="_new">Campus Map</a></li></ul>
</div>
<p>Located at the south end of the Mean Green Village is Waranch Tennis Complex, a three-million dollar, 12-court facility that's home to the Mean Green women's tennis team.</p>
<p>The Complex is at the corner of Bonnie Brae and Walt Parker Drive, in close proximity to the Athletic Center's Strength and Conditioning Center and Sports Medicine facilities and to the Victory Hall student-athlete residences.</p>
<p>Named for North Texas alumnus Ronald Waranch, the Tennis Complex opened in 2006. It has six courts on either side of the Complex's clubhouse, which houses a player's lounge, coachesâ€™ offices, meeting rooms and locker rooms.</p>
<p>As one of the top facilities in the region, Waranch Tennis Complex has hosted multiple Sun Belt Conference Championships, including 2007, 2009 and 2012.</p>
<p>Admission to the Mean Green's tennis matches at Waranch are free and open to the public. <a class="para" href="http://www.meangreensports.com/sports/w-tennis/sched/ntex-w-tennis-sched.html" target="_new">Click here for a schedule.</a></p>
