    <p><span class="bold">Kerr will close at 2:00pm on Thurs., May 12. <br>It will CLOSE Fri., May 13 for the Summer.</span></p>
<p>Located on Maple Street between Avenues A and B in Kerr Hall, Kerr Cafeteria is UNT’s largest dining facility. It features two salad bars, multiple entrée lines, a burger grill and a taco bar. The cafeteria features unique custom-designed food lines modeled after the historic Denton Square. It's also adorned with graphics featuring famous UNT alumni and prominent Texans, and a history of Denton, including historical photographs.</p>
<p><span class="bold">Adjusted Hours for Spring 2016</span></p>
<p>Since the University Union wasn’t ready last semester, Kerr Cafeteria was opened late nights and weekends. Now that the Union is open, Kerr will still be open Sundays, but it will no longer be serving weekdays after 9:00pm or on Saturdays. Instead, you can now enjoy expanded late night and weekend service at some of the Union Food Shops. For days and hours, please visit our <a href="http://www.dining.unt.edu/retailrestaurants" target="_blank">Retail Locations</a> page.</p>
<p><span class="bold">Why were Kerr late night and Saturday hours changed?</span></p>
<p>Hours were adjusted to accommodate the shift of customers from Kerr to the Union. Cafeterias are designed to serve a high volume of customers, and late nights and Saturdays are low volume times. </p>
<p> </p>
