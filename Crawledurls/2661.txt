                <h2 class="title" id="page-title">Room Reservation Requests</h2>
                        <div class="block block-nivo-slider block-nivo-slider block-nivo-slider-nivo-slider odd block-without-title" id="block-nivo-slider-nivo-slider">
  <div class="block-inner clearfix">
                
    <div class="content clearfix">
          </div>
  </div>
</div><div class="block block-system block-main block-system-main even block-without-title" id="block-system-main">
  <div class="block-inner clearfix">
                
    <div class="content clearfix">
      <div  about="/reservations" typeof="sioc:Item foaf:Document" id="node-page-27" class="ds-1col node node-page node-promoted view-mode-full node-not-sticky author-jdw0204 odd clearfix clearfix">

  
  <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"><div><span style="font-size:36px"><strong>Now processing requests for: Summer 2016*</strong></span></div>
<div> </div>
<div><span style="font-family:georgia,serif;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>*We are unable to book rooms on: 6/2, 7/8, 7/29, and 8/12 due to Finals Days</strong>. Please contact the Registrar’s office directly to request approval for an event on one of those dates.</span></span></div>
<div> </div>
<div><span style="font-family:georgia,serif;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Fall 2016 availability (August 29 and on) will not be known until August 2016.</strong> We will schedule Fall 2016 requests once the Registrar releases the availability <u>in August</u>. The requests will be filed until then, and will later be scheduled in the order received. <strong>Please do not call our offices to inquire about your Fall 2016 reservation request. </strong>This will allow us to process requests as quickly as possible.</span></span></div>
<div> </div>
<p><strong><span style="font-size:36px"><span style="font-size:24px">Room Reservation Guidelines</span></span></strong><br /><span style="font-family:georgia,serif"><em>You will be asked to verify that you have read these guidelines before submitting your reservation request.</em></span></p>
<ul><li><strong><span style="font-family:georgia,serif"><span style="font-size:20px"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><u>FOUR-DAY RULE:</u></span> Reservation requests and reservation changes must be submitted <u><em>at least four days in advance</em></u> of your event or meeting, due to university risk management and safety requirements.</span></span></strong>
<ul><li><span style="font-size:18px;"><span style="font-family: georgia,serif;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><strong><u>Example:</u></strong></span> Today is Thursday, February 11<sup>th</sup> – your event must occur on or after Monday, February 15<sup>th</sup>. If you have an emergency situation and are in need of a room before the four day minimum, please send an email to <a href="mailto:madelynn.camp@unt.edu">madelynn.camp@unt.edu</a> with a brief explanation of your situation and all pertinent event details (according to the room reservation request form).</span></span></li>
</ul></li>
<li><span style="font-family:georgia,serif"><span style="font-size:20px">The room reservation request form should be used by UNT faculty and staff to reserve General Purpose Classrooms only (Space Use code 110). This form cannot be used to reserve labs, conference rooms, lounge areas, etc.</span></span></li>
<li><span style="font-family:georgia,serif"><span style="font-size:20px">Reservation requests are not be accepted over the phone. Only requests that are submitted online will be processed. </span></span></li>
<li><span style="font-family:georgia,serif"><span style="font-size:20px">Reservation requests are processed in the order that they have been received.</span></span></li>
<li><span style="font-family:georgia,serif"><span style="font-size:20px"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif"><strong><u>Students and Student Groups</u>:</strong> </span>No student event room requests can be processed by LLPD.</span></span>
<ul><li><span style="font-family:georgia,serif"><span style="font-size:20px">Students and student groups wishing to reserve space on campus <em>must</em> submit their request to <span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif"><a href="http://studentactivities.unt.edu/orgs/events" target="_blank">Student Activities</a></span>. Student requests made using the form below will not be processed.</span></span></li>
</ul></li>
<li><span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size:20px;"><strong><u>Events with Minors in Attendance (Non-UNT students ages 18 and under)</u></strong></span></span><strong><span style="font-size:20px;"><span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif;">:</span></span> </strong><span style="font-family:georgia,serif;"><span style="font-size: 20px;">If you are expecting minors to attend your event, all persons interacting with the minors must go through training and a background check, hosted by UNT Risk Management. Please contact the Risk Management office at 940-565-2109 to set up your training and background check, as well as insurance requirements.. Please note: If parents attend the event with the minors and are with the minor 100% of the time, no training or background check is necessary.</span></span></li>
<li><span style="font-family:georgia,serif"><span style="font-size:20px"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif"><strong><u>Room Access and Custodial Services</u>:</strong></span> If your event is taking place after normal business hours or on the weekend, please be advised that the door to your reserved room may be locked. It is your responsibility to contact David Barkenhagen (<span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif"><a href="mailto:David.Barkenhagen@unt.edu">David.Barkenhagen@unt.edu</a></span>, 940-369-7311) in advance of your event to arrange for room access and custodial services. After hours cleaning may incur additional charges.</span></span>
<ul><li><span style="font-family:georgia,serif"><span style="font-size:20px">NOTE: Room reservation requests must be completed using the form below. Please do not contact Custodial Services (David Barkenhagen) to reserve a room.</span></span></li>
</ul></li>
<li><span style="font-family:georgia,serif"><span style="font-size:20px"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif"><strong><u>Instructional Equipment</u>:</strong></span> Please note that the instructional equipment in General Purpose Classrooms is supplied and maintained by <span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif"><a href="http://www.css.unt.edu/" target="_blank">Classroom Support Services</a></span> (940-565-2691).</span></span></li>
<li><span style="font-family:georgia,serif"><span style="font-size:20px"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif"><strong><u>Upcoming Semesters</u>:</strong></span> We cannot preempt the Registrar’s office when fulfilling reservation requests. Reservation requests placed for upcoming semesters will be held until classrooms for that semester have been released by the Registrar. At that time, we will begin processing requests in the order they were received.</span></span></li>
</ul><p class="rtecenter"><span style="font-size:26px"><a href="/reservations/room-reservation-request-form"><strong>Room Reservation Request Form</strong></a></span></p>
</div></div></div></div>
