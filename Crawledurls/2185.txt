		<h2>
			Apply for the Distinguished Lecture Series Committee</h2>

		<p>
			To apply for the Distinguished Lecture Series Committee you must 1) Be in good standing with the university 2) Have a cumulative GPA of 2.5 or above 3) Be able to attend all DLS meetings and speaker events</p>

		<p>
			The committee usually meets bimonthly and is expected to attend and work all events, including various tabling and marketing efforts (as schedule permits). Average weekly hours for DLS is approximately 3, though some weeks may require more.&nbsp;</p>

		<p>
			<strong>Applications are due Wednesday, May 4th, 2016</strong>. If you have any questions contact Ellysia Dierker&nbsp;either by email: ellysia.dierker@unt.edu or phone: 940-565-3355&nbsp;</p>
	</div>

		<h1 class="middle">
			UNT brings the DLS</h1>

		<p class="middle">
			The University of North Texas Distinguished Lecture Series is chartered as a university-wide standing Administrative Committee. The objective is to provide the university and greater communities with a variety of distinguished lecturers and speakers that will bring significant interest, visibility and added prestige to the University of North Texas.</p>
		&nbsp;

		<h1 class="middle">
			Prestigious speakers</h1>

		<p class="middle">
			The selection of speakers will be conducted on a consensus basis, after suggestions have been sought throughout the university. The selected speakers must reflect a wide public recognition and appeal. Under no circumstances will the Series be used to promote the discipline of any particular academic unit, department, school or college within the University.</p>
		<br />
		<br />
		<!--Heading and Text 3-->
		<h1 class="middle">
			Your committee</h1>

		<p class="middle">
			The Series will be administered by a committee composed of three (3) faculty representatives appointed by the Faculty Senate, three (3) staff representatives appointed by the UNT Staff Council, six (6) students appointed by the UNT Student Government Association, and one permanent ex officio faculty and/or staff representative to serve as administrative assistance to the committee, appointed by the Vice President for Student Affairs. The President of the UNT Student Government Association or his/her designee shall serve as chair.</p>
