      <h2>About ApplyTexas</h2>


<p>ApplyTexas was created through a collaborative effort between the Texas Higher Education Coordinating Board and the colleges and universities represented on the site. The goal of the project is to offer a centralized means for both Texas and non-Texas students to apply to the many outstanding postsecondary institutions available in Texas.</p>

<p>On ApplyTexas you can accomplish a multitude of tasks including:</p>

<ul>
<li>Apply for admission to any Texas public university, as well as to participating community and private colleges.</li>
<li>Apply for undergraduate, international and graduate admission.</li>
<li>Copy a submitted application to another institution.</li>
<li>Submit your application essays online.</li>
<li>Apply for scholarships from participating universities.</li>
<li>Search for and view both general and university specific information.</li>
</ul>

<p>Please read through the informational pages of this website before you begin using this site.</p>

<p>We wish you the best on your journey to college!</p>

<ul>
<li>Questions about the online application? Please review the <a href="adm_help.html">instructions for applying</a>.</li>

<li>Questions about going to college in general? Visit <a href="http://www.collegeforalltexans.com/">College for All Texans</a>.</li>

<li>Questions about a specific Texas college or school's admissions process? Please contact the <a href="../parms/c_univ_info.WBX">individual college or school's admissions office</a>.</li>
