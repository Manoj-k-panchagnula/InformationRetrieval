        <h2>Featured Collections</h2>
        <div id="features">
        	
			
            <div id="featureNumber1" class="feature">
            <a href="/explore/collections/TNCS/"><img src="/media/collection_icons/collection_TNCS.jpg" alt="" title="Toscanini: The Centennial Series" class="featured-collection" /></a><h3><a href="/explore/collections/TNCS/">Toscanini: The Centennial Series</a></h3>
            <p>The radio series "Toscanini: The Centennial Series," was a tribute to conductor Arturo Toscanini on the hundredth anniversary of his birth, and was a subset of the radio series "Toscanini: The Man Behind the Legend." Originally aired between January 4, 1967 and December 27, 1967, the broadcasts consist of music performed by the NBC Orchestra as well as interviews with ...</p>
            </div>
            
            <div id="featureNumber2" class="feature">
            <a href="/explore/collections/UNTGD/"><img src="/media/collection_icons/collection_UNTGD.jpg" alt="" title="University of North Texas Government Documents Department" class="featured-collection" /></a><h3><a href="/explore/collections/UNTGD/">University of North Texas Government Documents Department</a></h3>
            <p>Serving as both a federal and a state depository library, the UNT Libraries Government Documents Department maintains a collection of over 1 million items in a variety of formats including print, microform, audiovisual, maps, posters, musical scores, LPs, CD-ROMs, and Web documents. The department is a member of the FDLP Content Partnerships Program and an Affiliated Archive of the National ...</p>
            </div>
            
            <div id="featureNumber3" class="feature">
            <a href="/explore/collections/ANNALS/"><img src="/media/collection_icons/collection_ANNALS.jpg" alt="" title="Annals of Congress" class="featured-collection" /></a><h3><a href="/explore/collections/ANNALS/">Annals of Congress</a></h3>
            <p>The <em>Annals of Congress</em> is a record of the United States Congress from the First Congress in 1789 through the First Session of the Eighteenth Congress in 1824.  The Annals are a summary rather than a transcription, compiled after 1834 from newspaper accounts.   It is succeeded by the <em>Register of Debates</em>, the <em>Congressional Globe</em>, and the <em>Congressional Record</em>.</p>
            </div>
            
            <div id="featureNumber4" class="feature">
            <a href="/explore/collections/CALLNO/"><img src="/media/collection_icons/collection_CALLNO.jpg" alt="" title="SLIS Call Number" class="featured-collection" /></a><h3><a href="/explore/collections/CALLNO/">SLIS Call Number</a></h3>
            <p>The <em>Call Number</em> is a departmental and alumni newsletter published by the School of Library and Information Sciences at the University of North Texas beginning in 1939.  Issues contain news items and announcements concerning the library school and describe the activities of students, faculty, staff, and alumni.</p>
            </div>
            
            <div id="featureNumber5" class="feature">
            <a href="/explore/collections/RSOURC/"><img src="/media/collection_icons/collection_RSOURC.jpg" alt="" title="ReSource" class="featured-collection" /></a><h3><a href="/explore/collections/RSOURC/">ReSource</a></h3>
            <p>The ReSource collection contains magazines covering research, scholarship, and the arts at the University of North Texas.  Included are issues of <em>ReSource</em>, published from 1984-2004, and <em>UNT Research</em>, published from 2006--.  Highlights include features, news briefs, alumni spotlight, faculty books and portraits.</p>
            </div>
