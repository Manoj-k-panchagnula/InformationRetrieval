          <div class="breadcrumb"><a href="/">Home</a> Â» <a href="/scholarships">Scholarships</a></div><h2 class="header-title">How to Apply for Scholarships</h2><div id="node-264" class="node clear-block">



  <div class="content clear-block">
         <p>Student <a class="glossary-term" href="/glossary/term/67"><span hovertip=67>Financial Aid</span></a><span class="hovertip" id="67">Money provided to the student and the family to help them pay for the student's education. Major forms of financial aid include gift aid (grants and scholarships) and self help aid (loans and work).</span>&nbsp;and Scholarships&nbsp;(SFAS) oversee a range of scholarships that&nbsp;are funded by&nbsp;the university and donors who support UNT.&nbsp;&nbsp;In order to be considered for these scholarships, students must complete the <a href="https://unt.academicworks.com/">General Scholarship Application</a> annually.&nbsp; When completing the annual application, please review specific scholarship opportunity criteria and related deadlines.</p>
<p>UNT General Scholarship Application completion is encouraged for <strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;">all</span></strong> UNT students:</p>
<ul>
<li>Entering freshman</li>
<li>New Transfer students</li>
<li>Continuing undergraduate students</li>
<li>New graduate students</li>
<li>Continuing graduate students</li>
<li>International students</li>
</ul>
<p>Students must be fully admitted and active in a UNT degree-seeking program to receive any UNT Scholarship award.&nbsp; Students compete for scholarships based on a completed General Scholarship Application, provided they meet individual&nbsp;scholarship opportunity criteria.</p>
<h2>Important Information for Newly Admitted Students</h2>
<p>New students must have an assigned UNT ID number and active myUNT account to complete the application. &nbsp;For help with your EUID and/or password, please visit <a href="http://ams.unt.edu/">http://ams.unt.edu</a>.&nbsp;</p>
<p>UNT is a selective university and does not guarantee admission of all applicants.&nbsp; Submit the <a href="https://www.applytexas.org/adappc/gen/c_start.WBX">ApplyTexas Application</a> by February 1 to ensure that all high school records, prior college records and entrance exams are current with UNT for scholarship consideration.&nbsp;ApplyTexas Applications received after February 1 may not be complete in time for eligibility to be confirmed for the General Scholarship Application.&nbsp; Admission&nbsp;to UNT&nbsp;does not guarantee a scholarship award.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Specific questions regarding your admission application should be directed as follows below:</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Undergraduate Admissions:</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <a href="http://apply.unt.edu/admissions">Entering Freshman</a></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <a href="http://transfernow.unt.edu/how-to-transfer-to-unt">Transfer Students</a></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Toulouse Graduate School:</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <a href="http://tsgs.unt.edu/">New Graduate Students</a></p>
<h3>Important Application Instructions</h3>
<p>Please be prepared to provide&nbsp;essays, email contact for individuals providing letters of recommendation, or other documents that may be required.&nbsp; These documents <strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;">must</span></strong> be uploaded to your online application prior to the scholarship opportunity deadline. The application will state if letters of recommendation, essays, etc. are required for a scholarship. All document attachments become the property of UNT and will not be returned.&nbsp;</p>
<p>If you encounter problems or have questions&nbsp;while using the General Scholarship application, please view the <a href="http://financialaid.unt.edu/online-general-scholarship-application-help">Application Help</a> and <a href="http://financialaid.unt.edu/scholarships/gas-faq">Frequently Asked Questions</a>.&nbsp;&nbsp;Some users may experience slow website performance as the deadline approaches.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p>If selected for a scholarship award, award notification will be sent to EagleConnect, UNTâs official email service. &nbsp;Students are encouraged to monitor&nbsp;EagleConnect and myUNT for award information. Most awards&nbsp;are announced by late June.</p>

<p>According to Texas Coordinating Board Rules for Public Institutions of Higher Education, relatives of University of North Texas board members may not receive certain scholarships from the institution or system on whose board the member serves.</p>
