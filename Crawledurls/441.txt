			<h2>Research Your Options</h2>

<p>Not sure which college or university you'd like to apply to? Not sure about the requirements for applying to the one you?ve chosen? Here's some information you might find helpful:</p>

<h4><a href="../parms/c_univ_info.WBX">Four-year University Info</a></h4>
Includes contact information, details about essays, fees, and deadlines.
<h4><a href="../parms/c_univ_info.WBX">Graduate School Info</a></h4>
Includes contact information, details about essays, fees, and deadlines.

<h4><a href="../../adappj/parms/j_univ_info.WBX">Two-year College Info</a></h4>
Includes contact and school information as well as fee and deadline information.

<h4><a href="../../adapps/parms/s_univ_info.WBX">Scholarship Info</a></h4>
Deadline and other information about colleges and universities that award scholarships through the ApplyTexas scholarship application.

<h4><a href="http://www.collegeforalltexans.com/apps/Publications" target="blank">College Application Deadlines</a></h4>
A list of four-year college deadlines for freshman applications is available on the College for All Texans website: <a href="http://www.collegeforalltexans.com/apps/Publications" target="blank">College for All Texans</a>. The College for All Texans website also contains other information you may find helpful. 

<h4><a href="../gen/c_add_info.WBX">Additional Information for Applicants</a></h4>
Links to public university web sites in Texas, information about top 10% automatic admission, immunization requirements, nondegree seeking students, tuition rebates, and the possibility of additional tuition charges for students with excessive hours.
