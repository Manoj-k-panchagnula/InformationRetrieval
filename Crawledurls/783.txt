<h1 class="swim">Pohl Recreation Center & PEB Natatorium</h1>
<div class="sidebar">
<p class="sideimage"><img src="sidebar-pohl.jpg" width="350" height="200" alt="The Super Pit" /><br />
<h2>Pohl Recreation Center & PEB Natatorium</h2>
<ul><li>Venue for: swimming and diving</li>
<li>Opened: 2003</li>
<li><a class="toplinks" href="http://www.northtexasfanguide.com/swimming.html" target="_new">North Texas Swimming &amp; Diving Fan Guide</a></li>
<li><a class="toplinks" href="http://www.unt.edu/transit/pdf/2011-2012%20ParkingMap.pdf" target="_new">Campus Map</a></li></ul>
</div>

<p>The home of the Mean Green Swimming and Diving team is located north of the Super Pit on Chestnut Street, spanning the block from North Texas Boulevard to Avenue D, and its distinctive design has been the subject of articles in Texas Architect and Mondo Track & Field.</p>
<p>The massive 138,000-square-foot, two-story building has an eight-lane pool with bleachers for fans and an electronic scoreboard. The facility is also equipped with a six-lane 25-meter pool for training and warm-ups. Adjadent to the Recreation Center is the PEB Natatorium, which houses the diving facilities: two one-meter springboards and a three-meter springboard.</p>
<p>The Center opened in 2003, not only providing the swimming and diving team with a home but also possessing a dizzying array of fitness facilities for students, faculty, staff and alumni. Indoors, there are three basketball courts, three volleyball courts and four badminton courts, and outdoors there are also full and half basketball courts and a beach volleyball court.</p>
<p>The jogging/walking track is an eighth of a mile circuit with four lanes, and the 14,500 square-foot weight, fitness and cardio room has more than 100 cardio machines, including treadmills, ellipticals, AMTs, arch trainers, stationary bikes, upper body ergometers, stepmills, rowers, weight-lifting machines and free weights.</p>
<p>There is a 45-foot climbing wall and a 10-foot bouldering wall, a soccer gym, lap pools, a leisure pool and a spa with whirlpools. The Center also provides equipment rental and a lounge area with televisions and a ping pong table. Thereâ€™s even a Smoothie King.</p>
<p>North Texas also uses the outdoor long-course pool at TWU, three miles from campus, and the 40,000 square-foot Carroll ISD Aquatics Center in Southlake, which includes a 50-meter course, two 25-yard courses and diving springboards of one and three meters. The complex serves as the Mean Green's home away from home when hosting the annual North Texas Relays each September.</p>
<p>Admission to the Mean Green's swimming and diving meets at the natatorium is free and open to the public. <a class="para" href="http://www.meangreensports.com/sports/w-swim/sched/ntex-w-swim-sched.html" target="_new">Click here for a schedule.</a></p>
