				<h3>
					Mission</h3>

				<p>
					The Student Health and Wellness Center is committed to providing excellent healthcare to our students. Student success, development, and preparation for global citizenship is dependent on the many facets of good health. Mindful of our diverse population we promote responsible personal health choices to enhance each individual&#39;s lifelong health and well being.</p>

				<p>
					Within our purpose we support the mission of the University to ensure service and quality in teaching performance, public service and outreach.</p>

				<h3>
					Staff</h3>

				<p>
					Our professional staff includes licensed physicians, nurse practitioners, nurses, certified lab technologists, radiology technician, pharmacists, administrative, business, and medical records personnel. Part time staff includes psychiatrists, registered dietitian, and a massage therapist.</p>

				<h3>
					Patient Privacy and Confidentiality</h3>

				<p>
					The Student Health and Wellness Center is compliant with HIPAA, FERPA, and other state and federal law. Our staff takes all possible steps to protect the privacy and confidentiality of patients&#39; Protected Health Information. For more information, visit our&nbsp;<a href="/medical-records">medical records department</a>.</p>

				<h3>
					<a href="/billing-insurance">Billing and Insurance</a></h3>

				<p>
					The visit with a general practitioner is free but the SHWC offers competitive pricing on ancillary services such as pharmacy, lab work and x-ray. Payment is expected at the time of service and may be made by cash, check, or by credit card (AMEX, Discover, Mastercard, or Visa).</p>

				<p>
					As an extension of our customer service, the SHWC files medical insurance claims for the University endorsed student insurance policy underwritten by United Healthcare Student Resources. The pharmacy also accepts some prescription insurance plans.<span class="rangySelectionBoundary" id="selectionBoundary_1439301793151_2538779917159597" style="line-height: 0; display: none;">﻿</span></p>
