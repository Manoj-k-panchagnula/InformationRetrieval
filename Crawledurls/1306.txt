<h3>Tickets</h3>
<p>The Mean Green Sports Pass allows fans to attend every North Texas home regular-season volleyball, softball and soccer game for just $50 for adults and $30 for children 17 and under. To purchase your Mean Green SportsPass, call the North Texas Athletic Ticket Office at 1-800-UNT-2366.</p>
<p>Individual-game tickets are available on game days at Volleyball Center at $5 for adults and $3 for children 17 and under. All seating is general admission.</p>
<p>North Texas students are admitted free with their valid student ID.</p>
<p>Parties of 10 or more are eligible for group discounts. Contact the North Texas Athletic Ticket Office at 1-800-UNT-2366 for details on how to order.</p>

<h3>North Texas Volleyball Center</h3>
<p>The Mean Green volleyball team plays its home games at the North Texas Volleyball Center, located at 1536 South Bonnie Brae Street.</p>
<p>The Volleyball Center opened in 2006 and was one of the first facilities in the Mean Green Village. The Center houses the team's home court (a Robbins SportWood parquet floor), as well as locker rooms, coaches' offices, meeting rooms, officialsâ€™ dressing rooms and auxiliary training facilities. The Volleyball Center seats 600. <a class="mainlink" href="http://www.meangreenmap.com/volleyball.html" target="_blank">Click here for more information on the North Texas Volleyball Center</a>.</p>

<h3>Mean Green Club</h3>
<p>The Mean Green Club raises funds for North Texas athletic scholarships. Donations to the Mean Green Club help support nearly 350 North Texas student-athletes each year and provide them with the best collegiate experience possible.</p>
<p>Members receive exclusive access to North Texas athletics and year-long benefits such as invitations to member-only events, the best parking and seating available, and more.
<p>For more information or to join Mean Green Club, call us at (940) 369-7284, email us at meangreenclub@unt.edu, or <a class="mainlink" href="http://www.meangreensports.com/sports/mg-club/spec-rel/ways-to-give.html" target="_blank">click here to visit us on the web</a>.</p>
