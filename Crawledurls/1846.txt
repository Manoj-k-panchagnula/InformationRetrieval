<h2>Texas Key Laws &amp; Resources</h2>

<p>Information regarding Key Laws for Texans with Disabilities is covered under the resource topics listed on the left. These sections provide information related to Key Laws for Texans with Disabilities and to the Texas Statutes by topic.&nbsp; This section is not intended to be used for legal advice.</p>

<p>For more local resources, please dial 211. If you are calling from outside Texas, or have technical difficulties when dialing 211, please call (877) 541-7905.&nbsp; You may also visit the web page, <a href="https://www.211texas.org/cms/" target="_blank" title="https://www.211texas.org/cms/"><strong>Finding Help in Texas</strong></a>. You may also wish to visit <strong><a href="http://www.txp2p.org/resources/" target="_blank">Texas Parent to Parent</a></strong> for a directory of a variety of resources, searchable by county and topic.</p>

<p>Additionally, we offer a &ldquo;<a href="http://gov.texas.gov/files/disabilities/2011_Resource.doc " target="_blank"><strong>Resource Guide on Disability Topics Important to Texans</strong></a>&rdquo;.&nbsp; Please <a href="https://gov.texas.gov/contact/contactus.aspx?contact=8472945"><b>send us a message</b></a>, or call us at 512-463-5739.</p>
