      <h2 class="title">Transfer Now</h2>


			<div id="node-17" class="node node-type-page build-mode-full clearfix">
  
  
  
  
  <div class="content">
    <div id="front-intro" class="slide">
  <div class="story">
    <div class="copy">
      <h2 id="intro-header">You’ve reached an important point in your college life. It’s time to cross over from where you are to where you want to be. UNT is here to help you every step of the way.</h2>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

<div id="front-how-to-transfer" class="slide">
  <div class="story">
    <div class="copy">
      <h2>How to transfer</h2>
      <h3>We're here to help you</h3>
      <p>Transfer Counselors guide you every step of the way. They’ll help you learn what courses will transfer and assist you through the transfer process. </p>
      <p><strong>Credits transfer easily</strong> from community colleges or other universities because we participate in the Texas Common Course Numbering System. UNT’s <a href="http://essc.unt.edu/registrar/articulation/index.html">Transfer Guides</a> include a four-year plan to help you stay on track and graduate on time. UNT's <strong>Transferology</strong> service allows you to set up a personal account to list and evaluate courses you have completed or plan to complete. Transferring here couldn’t be easier and we will help you succeed.</p>
      <p class="deck-link"><a href="/how-to-transfer-to-unt">View the simple steps for transferring to UNT and apply now &raquo;</a></p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

<div id="front-academics" class="slide">
  <div class="story">
    <div class="copy">
      <h2>Academics</h2>
      <h3>There’s no limit to your opportunities here</h3>
      <p>Most of our 100 bachelor’s degrees can lead into a master’s program in the same field at UNT. You can transfer once and be set. If you are unsure about your major, don’t worry. We have professional academic advisors who specialize in working with students who are undecided or undeclared.</p>
      <p class="deck-link"><a href="/academics">Find your major, discover your passion and get your career &raquo;</a></p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

<div id="front-cost" class="slide">
  <div class="story">
    <div class="copy">
      <h2>Cost</h2>
      <h3>We worry about the cost of college too</h3>
      <p>UNT has been named one of <em>America's 100 Best College Buys&reg;</em> for 20 consecutive years, and we offer many options to help you pay for your college education.</p>
      <p><strong>Optimum value</strong> means UNT offers the quality of a private university at an affordable cost. Plus, you can fast track your degree by taking 15 credit hours per semester for about the same in tuition/fees as you would for 12 credit hours.</p>

      <p class="deck-link"><a href="/cost">Learn about your many choices for paying for college &raquo;</a></p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

<div id="front-campus-life" class="slide">
  <div class="story">
    <div class="copy">
      <h2>Campus Life</h2>
      <h3>A full college experience</h3>
      <p>Experience world renowned quality at 1,000 concerts and performances presented by our College of Music and the Department of Dance and Theatre. Meet people and get involved in your choice of 400 student organizations.</p>
      <p class="deck-link"><a href="/campus-life">See what a full college experience looks like &raquo;</a></p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

<div id="front-see-tour" class="slide">
  <div class="story">
    <div class="copy">
      <h2>Take a tour</h2>
      <h3>Come see for yourself</h3>
      <p>The best way to see if UNT is the right place for you is to take a student-guided tour.</p>
      <p>Each week, we offer transfer tours which are led by students who transferred to UNT. Who better to answer your questions than someone who has been there, done that? These tours last about 90 minutes and focus on transfer student needs.</p>
      <p class="deck-link"><a href="/see-tour">Schedule a tour online, we’d love to meet you &raquo;</a></p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

<div id="front-connect" class="slide">
  <div class="story">
    <div class="copy">
      <h2>Connect with us</h2>
      <p>Like us on <a href="http://facebook.com/northtexas" onclick="javascript:urchinTracker('/transfernow/untfacebook');">Facebook</a> or, once you’re admitted to UNT, you can join <a href="http://unt.schoolsapp.com" onclick="javascript:urchinTracker('/transfernow/inigral');">UNT’s private social network</a> so you can make friends before setting foot on campus.</p>
      
      <p>Connect to others by major, hometown, residence hall or organization. Discover who likes the same movies, bands, shows or teams. Suggest a meetup and hang out over coffee or a game of basketball.</p>
      
      <p class="deck-link"><a href="/transfer-events">Come visit UNT for one of our special Transfer events</a>. You'll tour the campus, learn more about transfer admissions and financial aid, meet student service groups, have your transcripts unofficially evaluated and more.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

<div id="front-contact-us" class="slide">
  <div class="story">
    <div class="copy">
      <h2>Contact Us</h2>
      <h3>People to help you</h3>
      <p>Individual assistance makes transferring easy. Our Transfer Counselors can answer your questions, review your transcripts and guide you through the admission process.</p>
      <p class="deck-link"><a href="/contact-us">We're ready to answer your admission questions in person or online &raquo;</a></p>
      <p style="margin-top: 20px;">Need more? <a href="http://pages.unt.edu/undergradinfo" onclick="javascript:urchinTracker('/transfernow/requestinfo');">Sign up to receive more information</a></p>
    </div>
