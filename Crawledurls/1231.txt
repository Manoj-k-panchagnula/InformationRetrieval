    <h3>Welcome to the University of North Texas!&nbsp;</h3>
<p>We are excited to be part of your future success, and help you reach your academic goals. Since the University of North Texas is a large institution, we want to ensure your educational transition is easy. The “Getting Started” section of the website has a wide variety of resources and services to support your entry to UNT. Please get acquainted with this information to answer preliminary questions and connect you to resources to orientate you to our campus.</p>
