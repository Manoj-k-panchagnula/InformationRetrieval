	<p>
		&nbsp;</p>

	<p>
		Thank you for checking out the UNT Center for Leadership and Service Website. UNT has over 36,000 students and 5,000 employees, making it an excellent source of volunteers. Whether you need someone with a particular skill, a volunteer for one hour a week, or a lot of bodies to staff an event, we want to help.</p>

	<p>
		The University of North Texas Center for Leadership and Service (CLS) serves as a connectivity resource, linking the students, faculty, and staff of UNT with the community agencies and individuals in need of volunteers. The Center for Leadership and Service sponsors events for the UNT Community, like Adopt-A-Block, Alternative Spring Break, the BIG Event and Make a Difference Day. In addition, the center also maintains a database of community agencies and an up to date calendar of volunteer opportunities. It is our hope that every UNT student will participate in at least one community service experience before graduation.</p>

	<p>
		In order to create our volunteer packets, we rely on the volunteer agencies and individuals to let us know what their need is. This can be done by filling out our&nbsp;<a href="https://orgsync.com/76344/forms/106595">Request Form for Volunteers</a>, which is available by e-mail, on-line, and in paper form. We do not guarantee volunteers! We merely advertise on your behalf about your current needs.</p>

	<p>
		To help in planning some of our sponsored events, like Make A Difference Day and the BIG Event, we may contact you to see if you have any specific opportunities available. For this to work, we need up to date contact information. Please be sure to update us about phone number, e-mail, and volunteer coordinator changes!</p>

	<p>
		<strong>Register as a Service Partner</strong></p>

	<p>
		Sign up as a community agency with us by filling out this short form: <a href="https://orgsync.com/76344/forms/107069">Service Partner Registration Form&nbsp;</a></p>

	<p>
		The Center for Leadership and Service will create a portal for your agency in our service management database and advertise volunteer opportunities that you submit to CLS. If any UNT students, staff, or faculty express interest in volunteering for your event, we will submit their information to you by email.</p>

	<p>
		As a service partner, we ask that you do a few things:</p>

	<ol>
		<li>
			Keep us up to date on changes in contact information and opportunities.</li>
		<li>
			E-mail us with any ideas, questions, or comments.</li>
		<li>
			Recognize your UNT volunteers and let us know how things are going.</li>
		<li>
			Send a representative to the Government &amp; Nonprofit Expo, sponsored by the Career Center. For more information <a href="http://careercenter.unt.edu/" target="_blank" title="Career Center"><u>click here</u></a> to register with Eagle Network. Fairs are held each semester.</li>
	</ol>

	<p>
		<strong>Looking for Volunteers?</strong></p>

	<ol>
		<li>
			Sign up as a service partner with us.</li>
		<li>
			Fill out a <a href="https://orgsync.com/76344/forms/106595">Request Form for Volunteers </a>, he sooner we get the dates the better!</li>
	</ol>

	<p>
		The information on this site will help you most effectively make the UNT Community aware of your needs and recruit volunteers. Please note that we receive numerous requests for volunteers each month, and that some of those requests do go unfilled.</p>

	<p>
		For your records, our contact information is as follows:</p>

	<p>
		<strong>UNT Center for Leadership and Service</strong><br />
		1155 Union Circle Box #305007<br />
		Denton, TX 76203-5017<br />
		Phone: (940) 565-3021<br />
		Fax: (940) 369-8440<br />
		E-mail: <a href="mailto:center.volunteer@unt.edu">leadandserve@unt.edu</a></p>

	<p>
		If we can be of any help to you, please do not hesitate to contact us! We thank you for your cooperation in helping the students of the University of North Texas as well as your dedication to helping the people in the Dallas/Fort Worth/Denton metroplex.</p>

	<p>
		<strong>Things to Consider</strong></p>

	<p>
		As you recruit and work with UNT students, you will want to take into account that UNT works on an academic calendar.</p>

	<p>
		You may also want to consider placing a classified ad in the NT Texas Daily (on campus student newspaper). For more information, contact the Main Office at (940) 565-2851, <a href="mailto:dailyads@unt.edu">dailyads@unt.edu</a>.</p>

var Voter = function() {
 	this.upButton;
    this.downButton;
    this.nid;
    this.inactiveClass = "inactive";
}

Voter.prototype.init = function() {
	this.upButton = document.querySelector(".up-vote");
    this.upButton.addEventListener("click", this.clickHandler.bind(this), false);
    this.downButton = document.querySelector(".down-vote");
    this.downButton.addEventListener("click", this.clickHandler.bind(this), false);
    //Get the node value
    this.nid = document.querySelector(".row.blog").getAttribute("id");//.split("-")[1];
}

Voter.prototype.clickHandler = function(e){
	var url = "/preska2/relationship/add";
    var data = "";
    var element = e.target || e.srcElement;
    //Get Node id
    data += "&nid=" + this.nid;
	//if upButton was pressed, prepare up vote string
    if (element === this.upButton) {
    	this.upButton.classList.remove(this.inactiveClass);
        this.downButton.classList.add(this.inactiveClass);
    	data += "&action=up_vote";
    } else if (element === this.downButton) {
    	this.downButton.classList.remove(this.inactiveClass);
        this.upButton.classList.add(this.inactiveClass);
    	//if downButton was pressed, prepare down vote string
        data += "&action=down_vote";
    }
    
    //Send the Ajax request
    var xmlhttp;
    if (window.XMLHttpRequest)
      {// code for IE7+, Firefox, Chrome, Opera, Safari
      xmlhttp=new XMLHttpRequest();
      }
    else
      {// code for IE6, IE5
      xmlhttp=new ActiveXObject("Microsoft.XMLHTTP");
      }
    xmlhttp.onreadystatechange=function()
      {
      if (xmlhttp.readyState==4 && xmlhttp.status==200)
        {
        document.getElementById("myDiv").innerHTML=xmlhttp.responseText;
        }
      }
    xmlhttp.open("POST", url, true);
    xmlhttp.setRequestHeader("Content-type","application/x-www-form-urlencoded");
    xmlhttp.send(data);
	console.log(data);
}

function startVoter () {
	
    var v = new Voter();
    v.init();

}

window.addEventListener("load", startVoter, false);
