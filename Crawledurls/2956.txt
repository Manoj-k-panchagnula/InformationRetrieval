        <h1 class="pagetitle">Employment Opportunities</h1>
        <!-- Content unit - One column -->
        <div class="column1-unit">
          <p>You  may view current employment vacancies and submit an on-line application at <a href="http://jobs.unt.edu" target="_blank">jobs.unt.edu</a>.&nbsp;  In order to be considered for employment, you must submit an on-line  application.</p>
          <p>          All  qualified applicants will be given consideration and notified by phone if they  have been selected to participate in an oral interview board.&nbsp; The oral  interview board will pertain to your qualifications and job requirements.&nbsp;  Based on these findings, the board will then consider your application further  to determine whether you have been considered for a formal background  investigation.</p>
          <p>          Upon  favorable recommendation from the board, you will be asked to submit a personal  history statement (PHS).   Your application and PHS will be assigned to  an investigator who will conduct a thorough background investigation to verify  your qualifying credentials, review any criminal record and verify personal  references. All positions within the UNT Police, Parking, and Transportation Division  have been designated security sensitive and require that applicants  satisfactorily pass a criminal history check.&nbsp; </p>
          <p>          All  background investigations will be reviewed and forwarded to the Chief of Police  for recommendation for a final interview.&nbsp; The Chief of Police will make a  final determination on whether to make an offer of employment.&nbsp; </p>
          <p>          The  complete selection process from the time your application is considered through  the background investigation may take several weeks to accomplish.&nbsp; I will  keep you informed of your status throughout this process. The position will  remain open until the best qualified applicant is selected.</p>
          <p>          In  the event that you are not selected for this position, you may reapply for a  similar or any other position within our department or the University of North  Texas.&nbsp; All applicants will be notified when the position is filled.</p>
          <p>            Again,  thank you for your interest in the University of North Texas Parking and  Transportation Department.        </p>
