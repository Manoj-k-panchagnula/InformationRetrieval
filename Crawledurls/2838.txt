<p>The Dean of Students Office enforces the Code of Student Conduct. The Code explains what conduct is prohibited, the process the university uses to review alleged violations, and the sanctions that can be imposed. When students may have violated the Code they meet with a university official to discuss the violation in an educational process.</p>

<p><b>View:&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</b><a href="/sites/default/files/code_of_student_conduct.pdf"><b>Code of Student Conduct</b></a></p>

<p>Anyone can report alleged misconduct to the Dean of Students Office by calling <a href="tel:+19405652039">940.565.2039</a>, emailing <a href="mailto:conduct.dos@unt.edu">conduct.dos@unt.edu</a>, or visiting the office in the University Union, suite 409.</p>

<p>Anyone who has experienced dating violence, domestic violence, stalking, sexual harassment, or sexual violence can contact the Dean of Students Office (<a href="tel:+19405652039">940.565.2039</a>), the Title IX Coordinator (<a href="tel:+19405652711">940.565.2711</a>), or the UNT Police Department (<a href="tel:+19405653000">940.565.3000</a>) to file a report. Assistance, support, and referrals to other support agencies are available regardless of any request for disciplinary action.</p>

<p>Learn More:&nbsp;<a href="/sites/default/files/what_to_do_about_sexual_violence_brochure.pdf">Where to Report Sexual Violence.</a></p>

<p>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;<a href="/sites/default/files/title_ix_conduct_brochure.pdf">Understanding the Conduct Process for Allegations of Sexual Harassment/Violence.</a></p>

<h2><strong>Conduct-Related Forms</strong></h2>

<h3><strong>Forms for Students Participating in the Conduct Process</strong></h3>

<ul>
	<li><a href="/sites/default/files/student_statement_form.pdf">Student Statement form</a>&nbsp;- Students accused of violating the Code have the can&nbsp;submit a written statement to the Dean of Students Office.</li>
	<li><a href="/sites/default/files/images/ferpa_authorization.pdf" target="_blank">FERPA Authorization</a>&nbsp;- Students who want the Dean of Students Office to discuss a conduct case with a third party can complete this form to give the university consent to share information.</li>
</ul>

<h3><strong>Forms for Students Requesting a Review of Their Case</strong></h3>

<ul>
	<li><a href="/sites/default/files/request_for_review_rev_2-2016.pdf">Request for Committee Review (Accused Student)</a>&nbsp;- Students receiving a permanent record sanction from the Dean of Students Office can request a review of their case by the Committee on Student Conduct by completing this form.</li>
	<li><a href="/sites/default/files/request_for_review_title_ix_complainant_rev_3-2016.pdf">Request for Committee Review (Complaining Student)</a>&nbsp;- In certain circumstances, students who filed a complaint with the Dean of Students Office can ask the Committee on Student Conduct to review the case by completing this form.</li>
	<li><a href="/sites/default/files/vpsa_review_request_2014-2015_rev_2-2016.pdf">Request for VPSA Review</a>&nbsp;- In certain circumstances, an accused student or complainant can ask the vice president for student affairs to review their case by completing this form.</li>
</ul>
