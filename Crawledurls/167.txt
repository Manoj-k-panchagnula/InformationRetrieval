</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-6684256340418007517' itemprop='description articleBody'>

<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
<a href='http://learn4unts.blogspot.com/2014/04/ongoing-unt-networking-issues.html'>Ongoing UNT Networking Issues</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-1452443167733932544' itemprop='description articleBody'>
At approximately 1:30 PM, UNT Denton experienced a networking hardware failure that has caused networking issues on UNT Denton and Discovery Park campuses. These issues have the potential to affect all UNT services, including Blackboard Learn. You may experience slow page loads and your connection may reset. The appropriate teams are working on a solution.
