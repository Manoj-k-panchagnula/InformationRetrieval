<ul class="list_standard">
    <li><strong><a href="http://www.tvc.texas.gov/Hazlewood-Act.aspx">Hazlewood Act</a> - </strong>The Hazlewood Act Exemption is an education benefit for honorably discharged or separated Texas veterans. </li>
    <li><strong><a href="http://www.tvc.texas.gov/Claims.aspx">Claims Representation</a> -</strong> The Texas Veterans Commission is the designated agency to represent the State of Texas and its veterans. </li>
    <li><strong><a href="http://gibill.va.gov/">GI Bill</a> - </strong>A federal education benefit provided by the Department of Veterans Affairs that is available for eligible veterans. </li>
    <li><strong><a href="http://www.texasbar.com/AM/Template.cfm?Section=Texas_Lawyers_for_Texas_Veterans">Texas Lawyers for Texas Veterans</a> - Texas Lawyers for Texas Veterans is a State Bar of Texas program to develop and assist pro bono legal clinics throughout the state for military veterans who otherwise cannot afford or do not have access to the legal services they need.</strong></li>
</ul>
