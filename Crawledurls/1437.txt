              <h2>First Year Students can commute from home</h2><div id="node-1251" class="node clear-block">



  <div class="meta">
      <span class="submitted">Submitted by Mark McLeod on Fri, 2013-11-22 10:52</span>
  
    </div>

  <div class="content">
    <p><img alt="commuter banner" src="/sites/default/files/pdf/Commuter_Banner.png" width="667" height="251" /></p><p></p><p><a href="http://policy.unt.edu/policy/18-5-1">UNT policy </a>requires first year undergraduate students to live on campus.&nbsp;&nbsp;A student that graduated from high school the semester prior to enrolling at UNT and is enrolled for 9 or more semester hours is subject to that policy.&nbsp;</p><p>UNT Housing will consider requests for a commuter exemption for students that fall in to the following categories:</p><ul><li>They wish to commute from the home of a parent, guardian or other adult relative that lives within a commutable distance.</li><li>They have significant life experience or maturity that would not be compatible with the objective of the on-campus housing policy.</li><li>They would suffer significant hardship because of insufficient finances or medical reasons that would result if the student were required to live on campus.</li></ul><p>If you fall in to one of these categories and want to commute as a first year college student, the process is fairly simple. &nbsp;</p><ol><li>Create an <a href="https://housing.unt.edu/ehousing/Login.php">eHousing</a> account</li><li>Select “Apply to Commute” from the menu</li><li>Select the term that you’ll be coming to UNT</li><li>Review the information about the commuter application</li><li>Print (or save to print later) the Commuter Exemption request form</li><li>Complete the Commuter Exemption request form and have it properly notarized</li><li>Submit the completed Commuter Exemption request form and any necessary supporting documentation to UNT Housing for review</li></ol><p>At the point that Housing reviews&nbsp;and approve the Commuter Exemption request, the students application is noted as complete on their eHousing account.&nbsp; Housing will also sends confirmation of approval via email to student prior to the start of the semester that commuter status was requested.&nbsp;</p><p>For questions please contact UNT Housing by phone during regular business hours at 940–565–2610 or by email at <a href="mailto:housinginfo@unt.edu">housinginfo@unt.edu</a>&nbsp;</p><p></p>  </div>
