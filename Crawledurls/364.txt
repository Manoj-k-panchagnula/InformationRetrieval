			<h2 class="title" id="page-title">Eagle Bound</h2>
			
			        
                <div class="block block-system block-main block-system-main odd block-without-title" id="block-system-main">
  <div class="block-inner clearfix">
                
    <div class="content clearfix">
      <div  about="/eagle-bound" typeof="sioc:Item foaf:Document" id="node-page-1828" class="ds-1col node node-page view-mode-full node-not-promoted node-not-sticky author-wdc odd clearfix clearfix">

  
  <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"><div class="grid-9 col-left">
<div class="inside">
 <p>The University of North Texas (UNT) maintains Admission Partnership agreements with several community colleges through the Eagle Bound Program.</p>
 
 <h4>UNT's Eagle Bound partners are:</h4>
 <ul>
 <li><a href="http://www.collin.edu" title="Collin College website (opens in a new window)" target="_blank">Collin College</a></li>
 <li><a href="http://www.dcccd.edu" title="Dallas County Community College website (opens in a new window)" target="_blank">Dallas County Community College</a></li>
<li><a href="http://grayson.edu/" title="Grayson College website (opens in a new window)" target="_blank">Grayson College</a</li>
 <li><a href="http://www.hillcollege.edu" title="Hill College website (opens in a new window)" target="_blank">Hill College</a></li>
 <li><a href="http://www.nctc.edu" title="North Central Texas College website (opens in a new window)" target="_blank">North Central Texas College</a></li>
 <li><a href="http://wc.edu" title="Weatherford College website (opens in a new window)" target="_blank">Weatherford College</a></li>
 </ul>
 
 <h4>How does it work?</h4>
 <p>The community college and UNT collaborate to provide transfer resources for students so that their transfer to UNT is seamless.</p>
 <h4>What does it provide participants?</h4>
 <ul>
 <li>Guaranteed admission to UNT with completion of an associate degree and by meeting the university's transfer admission requirement.</li>
 <li>Opportunity to schedule one-on-one pre-admission counseling appointments with UNT on the community college campus.</li>
 <li>Information for obtaining scholarship and financial assistance.</li>
 <li>Eagle Pass card issued to allow access to UNT campus with a valid community college ID card.</li>
 <li>Use of the UNT Library with a TexShare card.</li>
 <li>Special student rates for activities and sporting events, including the Pohl Recreation Center.</li>
 </ul>
 <h4>How do I apply to participate?</h4>
 <p>To apply for the Eagle Bound program, submit a completed Eagle Bound Pre-Admission Application to your community college's Registrar’s Office so they can provide UNT with confirmation of your enrollment:</p>
 <ul>
 <li><a href="/sites/default/files/COLLIN_PA.pdf">Collin College*</a></li>
 <li><a href="/sites/default/files/DCCC_PA.pdf">Dallas County Community College*</a></li>
 <li><a href="/sites/default/files/grayson_pa.pdf">Grayson College*</a></li>
 <li><a href="/sites/default/files/Hill_PA.pdf">Hill College*</a></li>
 <li><a href="/sites/default/files/NCTC_PA.pdf">North Central Texas College*</a></li>
 <li><a href="/sites/default/files/WC_PA.pdf">Weatherford College*</a></li>
 </ul>
<p class="notes">* You may need to download <a href="http://get.adobe.com/reader" title="Get Adobe Reader (opens in a new window)" target="_blank">Acrobat Reader</a> to view this .pdf file.</p>
