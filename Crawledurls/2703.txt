	<h2>UNT Insider</h2><div class="imagefit"><img src="/sites/default/files/u56/16_334_university-day_gp919.jpg" alt="" width="1180" height="672" /><br />
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>University Day -- one of the most festive days on our campus -- was extra special this year with Scrappy and UNT's Student Government Association unveiling a time capsule for opening in year 2041.</strong></p>

<div class="imagefit" style="padding-left: 120px;">&nbsp;</div>
<p class="p1">Dear alumni and friends,</p>
<p>I'm excited to bring you the <a href="http://insider.president.unt.edu/apr16/">April 2016 issue of <em>UNT Insider</em></a>, my monthly e-newsletter about the latest happenings on our campus. In this issue, you'll find out about our Wingspan Gala celebration, four new Goldwater scholars and new NSF CAREER award winner.</p>
<p>And don't forget to follow me <a href="http://www.twitter.com/UNTPrez">@UNTPrez</a> on Twitter for more highlights about our community.</p>
<p class="p2">UNT proud,<br /> Neal Smatresk<br /> President</p>
<h3>About <em>UNT Insider</em></h3>
<p>The <em>UNT Insider</em>, a monthly e-newsletter, connects UNT alumni and others to the university by letting you hear directly from the president.</p>
<p>The <em>UNT Insider</em> is brought to you by the Office of the President in conjunction with the <a href="http://urcm.unt.edu/">Division of University Relations, Communications and Marketing</a>.</p>
