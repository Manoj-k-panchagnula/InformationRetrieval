                                <h1>On &amp; Off Campus Access</h1>
        </header>
	  

	        
	  	  <div class="col-sm-12">
	        
                        
        <a id="main-content" name="main-content"></a>         
		                                 
		        
		        
		  <div class="region region-content">
    <section id="block-system-main" class="block block-system clearfix">

      
  <div class="panel-display panel-standard_two_column row" >

	
    <section class="col-sm-8 col-sm-push-4 panel-primary-content">
		<div class="panel-pane pane-custom pane-1"  >
  
      
  
  <div class="pane-content">
    <div class="alert alert-info"><i class="fa fa-info-circle"></i> <strong><a href="http://iii.library.unt.edu/search/y">Find Databases &amp; e-Journals</a></strong></div>
  </div>

  
  </div>
<div class="panel-separator"></div><div class="panel-pane pane-node-body"  >
  
      
  
  <div class="pane-content">
    <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even"><p>Electronic Databases provided by&nbsp;UNT&nbsp;Libraries can be accessed by any&nbsp;UNT&nbsp;student, faculty, or staff member from virtually any computer in the world.</p>

<h2>Campus Access</h2>

<p>Connecting to Electronic Databases on any of the&nbsp;UNT&nbsp;Campuses (Denton, Discovery Park, Health Science, or Dallas) is available via:</p>

<ul>
	<li><strong>UNT&nbsp;Campus:&nbsp;</strong>Any wired computer in the libraries or on campus.</li>
	<li><strong>UNT&nbsp;Wireless Network:</strong>&nbsp;Access the campus&nbsp;<a href="http://www.unt.edu/helpdesk/wireless/">wireless network</a>. (Requires&nbsp;EUID/Password Authentication, See Below)</li>
</ul>

<h2>Off-Campus &amp; Wireless Access</h2>

<p>Most subscription based Electronic Databases will require you to login with your&nbsp;EUID&nbsp;and password.</p>

<ul>
	<li><a href="https://ams.unt.edu/whatsmyeuid.php">Look up Your&nbsp;EUID</a></li>
	<li><a href="https://ams.unt.edu/activate.php">Activate your Account</a></li>
	<li><a href="https://ams.unt.edu/password.php">Reset your Password</a></li>
</ul>

<h2>Firewall Information</h2>

<p>UNT Libraries use a transparent URL rewriting proxy server to connect to databases, e-Journals, and other electronic media. If you access the Internet through a high speed solution other than a dial-up or modem connection, you may be behind a firewall. Please tell your network administrator to configure the firewall to allow ports 2048 through 9999 to connect to server IP address 129.120.93.218. Microsoft RDP clients use port 3389 for server IP address 129.120.92.70.</p>

<p>A user name and password are necessary for you to gain access to most&nbsp;&nbsp;databases, e-Journals, and other electronic media. A UNT Internet Services Account is assigned to every student, staff and faculty member. Your account must be activated before you can use these resources. The <a href="http://ams.unt.edu">Account Management System</a> will activate your UNT Internet account, and can be used to&nbsp;lookup your EUID and reset your password if you have forgotten it.&nbsp;</p>

<h2>Access after graduation, termination or retirement:</h2>

<p>If you are a student or alumni, access to the electronic resources will continue for two long semesters after your last enrolled semester. If you are a UNT employee, you will lose access on your termination date.<br />
<br />
Terminated UNT employees have no access.<br />
<br />
UNT retirees who are having problems with their access should email the <a href="mailto:lib.support@unt.edu ">Library Help Desk</a> or call 940-565-3024.</p>

<p><strong>See Also:&nbsp;<a href="https://policy.unt.edu/policy-by-number/3">UNT Computer Use Policy (3-10)</a>.</strong></p>

<h2>Resources that Require Special Software</h2>

<p>For a small group of products that are loaded on CD-ROMs, you must use a downloadable helper program. Individual resources will list this requirement in their descriptions.</p>

<h2>Problems</h2>

<p>If you have problems accessing Electronic Databases try the following:</p>

<ul>
	<li>Verify your&nbsp;EUID and Password are correct.</li>
	<li>Check for any limits on number of &quot;Simultaneous Users,&quot; or other notes about outages, etc.&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Attempt access through the&nbsp;<a href="https://vpn.unt.edu">Campus VPN</a>.</li>
	<li>Use our&nbsp;<a href="/node/1817">Ask Us</a>&nbsp;service for help.</li>
	<li><a href="mailto:Lib.Support@unt.edu?subject=Electronic%20Resources%20Problem">Report a problem via e-mail</a>.</li>
</ul>

<ul>

	<li>Click on it to exit. &nbsp;This frees up the product so others can use it.</li>
</ul>
