  <div class="content">
    <h3><strong>REAL Application Process</strong></h3><p>To join one of the Living-Learning Communities, you'll need to take the following steps:</p><p></p><p>1. Decide which&nbsp;Living-Learning&nbsp;Community you want to join</p><p>2. Login to eHousing to complete an application for Housing for the coming fall semester</p><ul><li>Select the&nbsp;Living-Learning option on the Select Application Year and Type step of the Housing Application</li><li>Select the REAL Community you want to join from the drop down list on the&nbsp;Living-Learning&nbsp;Communities step of the Housing Application</li></ul><p>3. Pay the $400 Housing pre-payment to complete your application and secure your space on the&nbsp;Living-Learning&nbsp;Community you chose</p><p>Space on each&nbsp;Living-Learning&nbsp;Community is limited and is reserved on a first come, first served basis. &nbsp;If you aren't able to join the community of your choosing, complete your application as a traditional student so that you have a space in Housing for the coming year and revisit your application preferences later to see if space has become available.&nbsp;</p><p>Use the below process for changing your existing application preference to a&nbsp;Living-Learning&nbsp;community.&nbsp;</p><h3><strong></strong>&nbsp;</h3><h3><strong>Changing your application preference to a Living-Learning&nbsp;Community</strong></h3><p>If you've already completed a Housing application and have an existing hall/room preference but you now want to join a&nbsp;Living-Learning&nbsp;community following the below steps will allow you to make the change on your own to get into a&nbsp;Living-Learning&nbsp;community (provided there is still space available within the community).&nbsp;</p><p>1. Login in to your eHousing account</p><p>2. Select “View My Applications” from the menu on the left</p><p>3. Select the “App No” for your Fall application</p><p>4. Select “Edit” for your Application Type and choose the&nbsp;Living-Learning&nbsp;option. Hit "Continue".</p><p>5. Select the&nbsp;Living-Learning&nbsp;Community you want to join from the drop down list on the&nbsp;Living-Learning&nbsp;Communities step of the Housing Application. &nbsp;Hit "Submit".</p><ul><li>If the&nbsp;Living-Learning&nbsp;community you want is not available, click the link provided to direct you back to the Application Type step and re-select "Traditional Student"</li></ul><p>6. Continue past the Application Summary step to the step that requests the application prepayment</p><p>7. If you haven’t already, make the $400 Housing pre-payment to finalize your Housing application and secure your space on the&nbsp;Living-Learning&nbsp;Community you chose</p>  </div>
