      <h2 class="main-title"><span class="green-72b844">Explore</span> What<br />
        We Have To Offer</h2>
      <p>The College of Music at the University of North Texas is one of the nation’s finest comprehensive music schools. We have a full-time resident faculty of over one hundred outstanding and internationally recognized performers, composers, scholars, and researchers, who are dedicated to helping each student reach his or her highest musical potential.</p>
<p>North Texas graduates are exceptionally accomplished, and are consistently competitive at the highest levels of their chosen professions. Leading opera companies, symphony orchestras, military bands, recording studios, educational institutions ranging from elementary school through graduate programs, and churches regularly engage musicians who have studied at UNT.</p>
