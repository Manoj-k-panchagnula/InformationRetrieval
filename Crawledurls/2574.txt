      <div  about="/tuition-and-fees" typeof="foaf:Document" id="node-page-23" class="ds-1col node node-page view-mode-full node-not-promoted node-not-sticky author-sgm0071 odd clearfix clearfix">

  
  <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"><h3>Tuition Plan Options for Undergraduate Students</h3>
<p>Undergraduate students at the University of North Texas have the choice between two tuition plans: the <strong>Eagle Express Tuition Plan</strong> and the <strong>Traditional Tuition Plan</strong>.</p>
<p>The Eagle Express Tuition Plan lets a students lock in their total academic costs and save up to $4,000 when they graduate on time. Students must meet Texas residency requirements and be seeking a bachelor's degree to be eligible for this plan.</p>
<p>Students on the Traditional Plan will pay a 3.9 percent increase in tuition each year. Students who do not meet the Texas residency requirements will be automatically enrolled in the traditional tuition plan.</p>
<p>Please visit our <a title="Eagle Express Tuition Plan information page" href="/eagle-express-tuition-plan">Eagle Express Tuition Plan information page</a> or the <a title="UNT Eagle Express Tuition Plan website (opens in new window)" href="http://eagleexpress.unt.edu/" target="_blank">Eagle Express Tuition Plan website</a> for more information. UNT Dallas Students with questions on the Eagle Express Tuition Plan may email <a href="mailto:UNTD.StudFinSvcs@unt.edu">UNTD.StudFinSvcs@unt.edu</a> or call either 972-780-3658 or 972-780-3659.</p>
<div class="faded-line imagefit"><img src="/sites/all/themes/greenlight_sub/images/faded-line.png" alt="" /></div>
<h3><a name="rates" id="rates"></a>Tuition Rates (Effective Fall 2016-Summer 2017)</h3>
<ul><li>Statutory Tuition for <strong>Texas Residents</strong> is $50.00 per credit hour.</li>
<li>Statutory Tuition for <strong>Oklahoma Residents</strong> is $100.00 per credit hour.</li>
<li>Statutory Tuition for <strong>Non-Residents</strong> (not residents of Texas or Oklahoma) is $458.00 per credit hour.</li>
<li>Board Designated Tuition for the <strong>Traditional Tuition Plan </strong>(Undergraduates only) is $216.27 per credit hour.</li>
<li>Board Designated Tuition for the <strong>Eagle Express Tuition Plan</strong> (Undergraduates only) is $237.11 per credit hour for freshman and transfer students who choose the plan during the Fall 2016 to Summer 2017 academic year. It remains $223.01 per credit hour for students who chose the Eagle Express Tuition Plan in the Fall 2015 to Summer 2016 academic year and $209.44 per credit hour for students who chose the Eagle Express Tuition Plan in the Fall 2014 to Summer 2015 academic year.</li>
<li>Board Designated Tuition for<strong> Graduate students</strong> is $177.79 per credit hour. Additionally, graduate students are also charged Board Authorized Tuition of $50.00 per credit hour and Board Designated Graduate Tuition of $25.00 per credit hour. Graduate students do not pay the Undergraduate Advising fee.</li>
<li><a title="UNT Dallas Tuition Rates (opens in new window)" href="http://www.untdallas.edu/admissions-aid/student-financial-services/cost-semester-credit-hour" target="_blank">See <strong>UNT Dallas</strong> Tuition Rates.</a></li>
</ul><div class="grid-5 column">
