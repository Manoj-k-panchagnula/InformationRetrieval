<P><!-- LINKS TO RELATED PAGES BEGIN --> <!-- LINKS TO RELATED PAGES END --></P><P><img src="http://grfx.cstv.com/photos/schools/ntex/sports/m-golf/auto_wide_story_new/11918413.jpeg?1462466424" alt="" width="640" height="350" /><br /><em>The North Texas men's golf team earned an at-large bid into the NCAA Tucson Regional - May 16-18 at the Gallery Golf Club.</em> &nbsp;<br /><br /><a href="http://www.ncaa.com/news/golf-men/article/2016-05-05/teams-and-individuals-selected-2016-ncaa-division-i-mens-golf">NCAA release</a><br /><br />DENTON --&nbsp;The North Texas men&rsquo;s golf team received an at-large bid into the 2016 NCAA Regional for the second time in school history under head coach <a href="/sports/m-golf/mtt/brad_stracke_850741.html">Brad Stracke</a> and will be participating in the Tucson regional May 16-18 held at the Gallery Golf Club, announced by the NCAA on Thursday morning live on the Golf Channel. <br /><br />&ldquo;I am very excited and proud of my team for making the West Regional in Tucson, Arizona,&rdquo; head coach <a href="/sports/m-golf/mtt/brad_stracke_850741.html">Brad Stracke</a> said. &ldquo;We have a very talented bunch of players and I believe they will perform at a very high level this post season.&rdquo; <br /><br />It marks the fifth NCAA regional appearance under Stracke and the 35th all-time in the history of the program. The Mean Green were ranked No. 62 in the nation according to Golfstat.com rankings and were able to receive one of 41 at-large bids. <br /><br />The three-day event will take place at Gallery Golf Club which has hosted the 2008 WGC-Accenture Match Play Championship won by Tiger Woods. <br /><br />North Texas is coming off a third-place finish at the Conference USA Championship before falling in the semifinals of match play to eventual champion LA Tech. <br /><br />Fourteen teams, including nine in the top-50, will be competing for the right to advance to the NCAA Championship hosted by Oregon on May 27- June 1 at Eugene Country Club. The top five teams at each regional will advance to the next round.<br /><br /></P><P>
