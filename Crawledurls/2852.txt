    <div class="alert alert-warning">
	<h4>
		If you have an emergency, call 911.</h4>

	<p>
		The <a href="/student-health-and-wellness-center">Student Health &amp; Wellness Center</a> (SHWC)&nbsp;is&nbsp;<strong>NOT</strong>&nbsp;an Emergency Room.&nbsp; By calling 911, first responders will provide you with the most prompt response to an emergency, whether on or off campus.</p>
</div>

<h2>
	The Student Health and Wellness Center will be closed on March 30, 2016 from 11 am to 1:30 pm.</h2>

<h1>
	To make an appointment</h1>

<ul>
	<li>
		Call us at <strong style="margin: 0px;">940-565-2333</strong></li>
	<li>
		Sign on to <a href="https://myosh.unt.edu/" target="_blank"><strong>MyOSH.unt.edu</strong></a> <em>(eligible students only)</em></li>
	<li>
		Stop by the Health Center</li>
</ul>

<p>
	Main Clinic: 940-565-2333</p>

<p>
	<strong style="margin: 0px;">Provider visits are by appointment only.<br />
	Additional charges may be incurred during your visit.<br />
	We do not file insurance to commercial carriers but will file directly to the University endorsed student insurance policy.</strong></p>

<p>
	Please note that limited appointments can be made online through <a href="https://myosh.unt.edu/" target="_blank">MyOSH.unt.edu</a>. If you cannot find your appointment reason online please call the Scheduling office at 940-565-2333, option 2.&nbsp; Additionally, same day only&nbsp;appointments are available by calling the Scheduling office at 940-565-2333.</p>

<p>
	During this busy cold and flu season, appointments with providers may book up quickly. If an appointment is not available with a provider, you can come into the clinic for a walk-in consultation with a licensed professional nurse.&nbsp; Consultations are available Monday through Thursday 8:30am to 5pm and Friday 9:30am to 5pm.</p>

<p>
	<strong>Appointment times are reserved for the problems or injuries for which you have originally set the appointment. As a courtesy to all our patients, any additional concerns will need to be addressed at a separate appointment.</strong></p>

<p>
	When we are closed, you can call the 24-hour nurse call line at&nbsp;855-894-3803 PIN 1072 to speak with a registered nurse for phone triage and home care treatment options.</p>

<h2>
	Meadows Center for Health Resources</h2>

<p style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); line-height: 20px; font-family: Verdana, Geneva, sans-serif; font-size: 12px; margin-bottom: 8px !important;">
	For massage therapy, Mean Green Screen and health education appointments please call 940-565-2787.</p>

<p style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); line-height: 20px; font-family: Verdana, Geneva, sans-serif; font-size: 12px; margin-bottom: 8px !important;">
	Student Interview Requests</p>

<p style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); line-height: 20px; font-family: Verdana, Geneva, sans-serif; font-size: 12px; margin-bottom: 8px !important;">
	To schedule an interview with a medical provider or other Student Health and Wellness Center staff member, please submit the <a href="/sites/default/files/healthcenter/information_needed_for_student_interview_requests.docx">Information Needed for Student Interview Requests</a> to <a href="mailto:SHWCOutreach@unt.edu?subject=Student%20Interview%20Request">SHWCOutreach@unt.edu</a>.</p>

<h2>
	What Do I Need When I Come to the Student Health and Wellness Center?</h2>

<ul>
	<li>
		ID card</li>
	<li>
		Local address</li>
	<li>
		Phone number</li>
	<li>
		Checkbook, credit card or cash</li>
</ul>

<h1 style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">
	Cancelling or Rescheduling an appointment</h1>

<p>
	&nbsp;</p>

<p>
	To cancel your appointment by phone, students should call (940) 565-2793. To reschedule your appointment, please call (940) 565-2333 and speak to an appointment scheduler or go online to <a href="https://myosh.unt.edu/" target="_blank">myOSH.unt.edu</a>.</p>

<p>
	As a courtesy to other students who may need to be see, please try to reschedule your appointment 24 hours in advance if possible.&nbsp; Patients who do&nbsp;not cancel or reschedule at least an hour before their scheduled appointment time will be charged a $10 no show fee.</p>
