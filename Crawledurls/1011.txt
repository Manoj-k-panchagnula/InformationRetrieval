  <h2 class="node__title node-title">Training and Development</h2>
  
  <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"><p>Talent Management aims to create a productive and successful organization, using best practices to attract, develop and retain employees.</p>
<p><a href="https://hr.untsystem.edu/meet-human-resources-team" title="Talent Management">Talent Management</a> activities, including regularly scheduled classes, help employees identify opportunities to expand skills in current jobs and learn new information that supports career growth.</p>
<p>Managers will find useful classes and ideas for leadership development and performance management that helps individuals and teams contribute to the success of a department, division or campus.</p>
<ul><li>Learn about the<a href="https://hr.untsystem.edu/talent-management-leadership-development-program"> Leadership Development program</a>.</li>
<li><a href="https://untranet.unt.edu/untsystem/UNT%20System%20HR/talent_management/SitePages/Home.aspx">Find a current schedule of training and classes</a>. (SharePoint site; requires login.)</li>
<li>Find Supervisor Buiding Blocks program information (<a href="http://untranet.unt.edu/untsystem/UNT%20System%20HR/talent_management/SitePages/Register%20for%20a%20Class.aspx">SharePoint</a>; EUID needed for log in.)</li>
<li>Find <a href="https://untranet.unt.edu/untsystem/UNT%20System%20HR/talent_management/PerformanceManagement/SitePages/Home.aspx">ePerformance training materials and tools</a> (SharePoint; EUID needed for log in.)</li>
</ul><div class="accButton"><button class="expand AllAcc">Expand All</button></div>

</li>

<li>
<h2>Frequently Asked Questions About Classes</h2>
<div class="info">
<p><strong>How do I register for classes?</strong></p>
<p>All training registration is done online on the <a href="https://untranet.unt.edu/untsystem/UNT%20System%20HR/talent_management/SitePages/Register%20for%20a%20Class.aspx" title="Training and Development Registration">Share Point website</a> or on <a href="https://my.unt.edu/psp/papd01/EMPLOYEE/EMPL/h/?tab=NTPA_GUEST">MyUNT</a>, <a href="https://my.unt.edu/psp/papd01/EMPLOYEE/EMPL/h/?tab=HSPA_GUEST">MyHSC</a>, <a href="https://my.unt.edu/psp/papd01/EMPLOYEE/EMPL/h/?tab=DLPA_GUEST">MyUNTDallas</a>, <a href="https://my.unt.edu/psp/papd01/EMPLOYEE/EMPL/h/?tab=SYPA_GUEST">MySystem </a>or <a href="https://my.unt.edu/psp/papd01/EMPLOYEE/EMPL/h/?tab=JDPA_GUEST">MyLaw</a>.  Log in with your EUID and password, and then follow the prompts. Please note that the previous manager's certification program at UNT is no longer offered. Please contact <a href="mailto:shelly.dietderich@untsystem.edu?body=manager's%20certification%20program">Shelly Dietderich</a> for assistance with updated learning opportunities.</p>
<p><strong>Who can attend training and development classes?</strong></p>
<p>Faculty, staff and student employees are eligible to attend training. Most classes are offered at no cost.  A supervisor’s consent is required to attend.</p>
<p><strong>Do I have to use my personal time off (vacation, floating holiday) to attend? </strong></p>
<p>Policies allow employees to attend classes during work hours. Supervisory approval is required before registration. Learn more:</p>
<ul><li><a href="http://policy.unt.edu/policy/1-3-17">UNT Policy 1.3.17</a></li>
<li><a href="https://app.unthsc.edu/policies/PoliciesPDF/Training%20and%20Development.pdf">UNTHSC Policy 5.701</a></li>
<li><a href="http://www.untdallas.edu/sites/default/files/page_level2/pdf/policy/5.019%20Training%20and%20Education.pdf">UNT Dallas Policy 5.019</a></li>
</ul><p> <strong>How do I cancel a class registration?</strong></p>
<p>Please contact the Human Resources Support Center, 1-855-878-7650 or <a href="mailto:talent.manaagement@untsystem.edu">Talent Management</a> as soon as possible. Space is limited for some classes and your reserved space can often be reassigned to someone on our waiting list.</p>
</div>
